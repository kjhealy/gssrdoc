# What percentage of population is american india

usamind

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Just your best guess"what percentage of the United States
population is each group? (INSTRUCTION: PERCENTAGES DO NOT HAVE TO ADD
TO 100pct AND THE LISTED GROUPS MAY OVERLAP. ENCOURAGE ESTIMATES FOR ALL
GROUPS, BUT DISCOURAGE RESPONDENTS REVISING THEIR ESTIMATES.) F.
American Indians?
