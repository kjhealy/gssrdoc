# Bible prayer in schools:version z

prayerx

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question C. Which of these statements comes closest to your feelings
about prayers in the public schools? The Lord”s prayer or some Bible
verse should be read daily. There should be a moment for silent prayer
or meditation daily. No prayer or other religious observances should be
held in the public schools. Other (SPECIFY).
