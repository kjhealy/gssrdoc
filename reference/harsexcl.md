# Since 18 r has ever found self the object of sexual advances from religious lead

harsexcl

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now think about church, synagogue, or other religious settings.
Since you turned 18, have you ever found yourself the object of sexual
advances or propositions from a minister, priest, rabbi, or other
clergyperson or religious leader who was not your spouse or significant
other?
