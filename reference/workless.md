# Cut back on your work?

workless

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your (present/most recent) job, (have you ever done/did you
ever do) any of the following because of your responsibilities to
members of your family? D. Cut back on your work?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable

  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
