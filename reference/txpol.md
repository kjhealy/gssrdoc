# How well elected official knows effect of reducing fed inc tax

txpol

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question The first issue is federal income taxes. Some people say that
reducing income taxes almost always helps the economy grow. Others say
that reducing income taxes has very little long-term impact on economic
growth but could cause a big increase in the deficit. Please look at
Card B10. (On a scale of 1 to 5, where 1 means "Very Well" and 5 means
"Not at All"), how well do the following groups understand the likely
effects of reducing federal income taxes? C. Elected Officials

- `[5]` not at all
