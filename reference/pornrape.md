# Materials lead to rape

pornrape

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question The next questions are about pornography–books, movies,
magazines, and photographs that show or describe sex activities. I”m
going to read some opinions about the effects of looking at or reading
such sexual materials. As I read each one, please tell me if you think
sexual materials do or do not have that effect. READ EACH ITEM. CODE ONE
FOR EACH C. Sexual materials lead people to commit rape
