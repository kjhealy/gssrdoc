# How intense would you say your anger was

howangry

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How intense would you say your anger or irritation was, if 0
means the least intense emotion that you can imagine experiencing and 10
is the most intense emotion that you can imagine having.

## Values

- `[0]` weakest

- `[10]` most intense

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
