# Number of ppl owning second home in voluntary association

acqvahme

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Next, we are going to ask about people that you are acquainted
with through schools, clubs, associations, or places of worship. D. How
many are you pretty certain own a second home?

## Values

- `[1]` 0

- `[2]` 1

- `[3]` 2 - 5

- `[4]` 6 - 10

- `[5]` more than 10

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
