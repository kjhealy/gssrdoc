# How get paid last week

howpaid

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question As of last week, did your main job pay an hourly wage, daily
wage, weekly wage, monthly salary, annual salary, or other pay rate
(e.g. piece rate, commission, wage + tips, and other mixed payment
methods), irrespective of the time between payrolls and the use of
bonuses or profit-sharing?

## Values

- `[1]` hourly wage

- `[2]` daily wage

- `[3]` weekly wage

- `[4]` monthly salary

- `[5]` annual salary

- `[6]` other pay rate

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
