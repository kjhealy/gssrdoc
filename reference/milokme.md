# Military service good experience for men

milokme

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. For most young men, do you think military service is
definitely a good experience, probably a good experience, probably not a
good experience, or definitely not a good experience for them?

## Values

- `[1]` definitely good

- `[2]` probably good

- `[3]` prob not good

- `[4]` def not good

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
