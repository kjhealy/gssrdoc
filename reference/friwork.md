# Number of close friends are coworkers

friwork

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking now of close friends - not your husband or wife or
partner or family members - but people you feel fairly close to… A. How
many of these close friends are people you work with now?

## Values

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
