## Source

## Details

Question A. Age of interviewer As of 2024, INTAGE has been recoded to
only account for fewer categories. This has been retroactively applied
to all previous years of INTAGE. The original INTAGE containing data
from 2004 to 2018 has been renamed INTAGE_0418.
