# Thinking us how often feel angry

usangry

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I want to ask some questions about how you feel when you
think about this country, the United States. When you think about the
United States: E. When you think about the United States, how often do
you feel ANGRY? Do you feel angry:

- `[3]` some of the time

- `[4]` rarely
