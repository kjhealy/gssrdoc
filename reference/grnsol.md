# Accept cut in living stnds to help envir?

grnsol

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question C. And how willing would you be to accept cuts in your standard
of living in order to protect the environment?

## Values

- `[1]` very willing

- `[2]` fairly willing

- `[3]` neither willing nor unwilling

- `[4]` fairly unwilling

- `[5]` very unwilling

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
