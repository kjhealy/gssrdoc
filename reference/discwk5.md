# Discrimination at work in past 5 yrs

discwk5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Over the past five years, have you been discriminated against
with regard to work, for instance when applying for a job, or when being
considered for a pay increase or promotion?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[3]` did not work or did not seek work

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
