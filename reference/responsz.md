# Responsible-version z

responsz

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please look at the qualities listed on this card. (SHOW CARD:
LET R. INSPECT) All of the qualities may be desirable for a child to
have, but could you tell me whether the quality is extremely important,
very important, fairly important, not too important, or not at all
important. READ AND RECORD RESPONSES FOR EACH. J. That he is responsible

## Values

- `[1]` extremely impt

- `[2]` very impt

- `[3]` fairly impt

- `[4]` not too impt

- `[5]` not at all impt

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
