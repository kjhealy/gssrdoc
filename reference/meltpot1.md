# Better to maintain distinct cultures

meltpot1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people say that it is better for a country if different
racial and ethnic groups maintain their distinct customs and traditions.
Others say that it is better if these groups adapt and blend into the
larger society. Which of these views comes closer to your own?
