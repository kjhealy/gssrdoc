# Hours religious act outside of home

relhrs2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. In the past month, about how many hours have you spent doing
religious services activities outside your home (such as attending
religious services, prayer groups, Bible studies, fellowship meetings,
church leadership meetings, etc.)?
