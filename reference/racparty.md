# Which party helps blacks more

racparty

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which party, the Republican or the Democratic, do you feel will
Do more to help blacks in the next few years, or do you think there
isn't much difference between the two?

## Values

- `[1]` republican party

- `[2]` democratic party

- `[3]` not much difference
