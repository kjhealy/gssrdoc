# Spouse's father's occ when spouse was 16

sphedocc

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. Now we would like to find out what kind of work your
(husband's/wife's) father (OR SUBSTITUTE) did when your (husband/wife)
was about 16 years old. What kind of work was your (husband's/wife's)
father (OR SUBSTITUTE) doing? That is, what was (his/her) job called? B.
IF NOT ALREADY ANSWERED, ASK: What did (he/she) actually do in that job?
Tell me what were some of (his/her) main duties? C. What kind of place
did (he/she) work for? D. IF NOT ALREADY ANSWERED, ASK: What did they
(make/do)? E. IF ALREADY ANSWERED, CODE WITHOUT ASKING: Was (he/she)
self-employed or did (he/she) work for someone else?
