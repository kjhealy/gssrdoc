# Employers' right to hire by genetic tests

genehire

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. Should employers have the right not to hire workers if tests
show they have an inherited tendency to develop certain forms of cancer
or heart disease, or should they not have the right?
