# How much r thinks cabinet member makes

paycabnt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question We would like to know what you think people in these jobs
actually earn. Please write in how much you think they usually earn,
each year, before taxes. Many people are not exactly sure about this but
your best guess will be close enough. This may be difficult, but it is
very important. So please try. nPlease write in how much they actually
earn each year. K. A member of the cabinet in the federal government?

## Values

- `[1e+06]` \$1 million (or more, for 1987 & 2000)

- `[99999997]` never worked

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
