# Quit if couldn't integrate club

racquit

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If you and your friends belonged to a social club that would
not let (Whites/Negroes/Blacks) join, would you try to change the rules
so that (Whites/(Negroes/Blacks/African-Americans) could join? A. IF
YES: If you could not get the rules changed, do you think you would
resign from the club, even if your friends didn't?
