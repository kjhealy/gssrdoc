# Type of current job held by r

lastjob

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question And how about your job now? (If you are not working now, please
tell us about your last job.)

## Values

- `[1]` prof or tech

- `[2]` higher admin

- `[3]` clerical

- `[4]` sales

- `[5]` service

- `[6]` skilled worker

- `[7]` semi-skilled worker

- `[8]` unskilled worker

- `[9]` farm

- `[10]` never had job

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
