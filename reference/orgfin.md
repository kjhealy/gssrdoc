# R's financial situation after org. changes

orgfin

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question As a result of these organizational changes, would you say that
your financial situation is now much better, better, worse, much worse,
or have these things not affected your finances?

## Values

- `[1]` much better

- `[2]` better

- `[3]` worse

- `[4]` much worse

- `[5]` no effect

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
