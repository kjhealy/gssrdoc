# Last time r felt really angry

angrywhy

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Within the past month, think about the last time you felt
really angry, irritated or annoyed. Could you describe in a couple of
sentences what made you feel that way – what the situation was?
(INTERVIEWER: IF RESPONDENT CANNOT RECOVER AN EVENT, PROBE WITH "You
haven't felt irritated or annoyed in the past month?") D. Reasons why
people get angry:
