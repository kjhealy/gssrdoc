# At what age you first told someone that you were gay/les/bi

toldsxor

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question At about what age were you when you first told someone that you
were (gay(IF MALE)/lesbian(IF FEMALE)/bisexual)?

## Values

- `[1]` under age 14

- `[2]` age 14-18

- `[3]` age 18-25

- `[4]` age 26-35

- `[5]` age 36-45

- `[6]` age 46-55

- `[7]` age 56-65

- `[8]` age 66 or older

- `[9]` i have never told anyone

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
