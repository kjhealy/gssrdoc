# Govt money for high achieving students

aidsmart

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people think the government should provide financial
assistance to college students. Others think the government should not
provide such aid. In each of the circumstances listed below should the
government provide grants that would not have to be paid back, provide
loans which the student would have to pay back, or should the government
not provide any financial assistance? B. For students who have
outstanding grades in high school.

## Values

- `[1]` govt give grants

- `[2]` govt make loans

- `[3]` no govt asst

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
