# Birth control information

pill

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. In some places in the United States, it is not legal to
supply birth control information. How do you feel about this–do you
think birth control information should be available to anyone who wants
it, or not?

## Values

- `[1]` available

- `[2]` not available

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
