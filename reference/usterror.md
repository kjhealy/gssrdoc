# Terrorism by u.s. citizens more threat 10 yrs

usterror

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Are the following threats to the United States greater, about
the same, or less today than they were 10 years ago? C. Terrorism by
U.S. citizens

- `[2]` about the same

- `[3]` less
