# Pro-choice vs pro-life

abbelief

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which of the following best represents you in terms of your
belief about abortion? I am...

NOTE: This variable originates on the GSS Next follow-on, and should not
be analyzed without the GSS Next-specific weights WTSSPS_NEXT or
WTSSNRPS_NEXT

## Values

- `[1]` strongly pro-choice

- `[2]` slightly pro-choice

- `[3]` both pro-choice and pro-life

- `[4]` neither pro-choice nor pro-life

- `[5]` slightly pro-life

- `[6]` strongly pro-life

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
