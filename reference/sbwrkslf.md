# R sibling self-employed?

sbwrkslf

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Respondent's Sibling's Occupation, Prestige, and Industry A.
What kind of work (is/was) (BROTHER'S NAME/SISTER'S NAME) doing? That
is, what was (his/her) job called? OCCUPATION (See REMARKS) B. IF NOT
ALREADY ANSWERED, ASK: What (does/did) (s/he) actually do in that job?
Tell me some of what (are/were) some of (his/her) main duties? C. What
kind of place did (s/he) work for? INDUSTRY (See REMARKS) D. IF NOT
ALREADY ANSWERED, ASK: What (do/did) they (make/do)? E. IF ALREADY
ANSWERED, CODE WITHOUT ASKING: (Is/Was) (he/she) self-employed or
(does/did) (he/she) work for someone else?

## Values

- `[1]` self employed

- `[2]` someone else

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
