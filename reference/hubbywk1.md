# Men should earn money women keep house

hubbywk1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question And, do you agree or disagree . . . A. A man”s job is to earn
money; a woman”s job is to look after the home and family.

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree
