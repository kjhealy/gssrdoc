# Allow socialists book in library

libsoc

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Or consider a person who favored government ownership of all
the railroads and all big industries. C. If some people in your
community suggested a book he wrote favoring government ownership should
be taken out of your public library, would you favor removing this book,
or not?
