# Financial distribution in household

orginc

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How do you and your spousepartfill organize the income that one
or both of you receive? Please choose the option that comes closest.

## Values

- `[1]` r manage all money and gives spouse/partner his/her share

- `[2]` r spouse/partner manages all money and gives r share

- `[3]` r and spouse/partner pool all money and take what we need

- `[4]` r and spouse/partner pool some money and keep rest separate

- `[5]` r and spouse/partner keep money separate

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
