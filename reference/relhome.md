# Relationship to the previous owner of the home

relhome

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which of the following best describes your relationship to the
previous owner of the home you purchased?

## Values

- `[1]` a relative including in-laws

- `[2]` a friend or acquaintance

- `[3]` a friend of a friend-relative

- `[4]` had previous business dealings

- `[5]` no prior relationship

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
