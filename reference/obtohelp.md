# Obligation to care for ill parent or spouse?

obtohelp

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. Sometimes people are asked to make great sacrifices for
others in their family by caring for an elderly parent or a spouse who
is seriously ill or injured. When someone is asked to give such care, is
it their obligation to do so, or should they give care only if they
really want to?
