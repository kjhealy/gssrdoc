# Greenhouse effect danger to envir

tempgen

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. In general, do you think that a rise in the world's
temperature caused by the \`greenhouse effect', is…

## Values

- `[1]` extremely dangerous

- `[2]` very dangerous

- `[3]` somewhat dangerous

- `[4]` not very dangerous

- `[5]` not dangerous at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
