# Does r or spouse supervise anyone

wksup

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your (SPOUSE'S) job, (do you/does he/she) supervise anyone
who is directly responsible to (you/him/her)?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
