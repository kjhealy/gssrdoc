# R express admiration for blacks

admirblk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, I would like to ask whether you have ever felt the
following ways about blacks and their families. For each of the feelings
that I ask you about, please tell me whether you have felt that way very
often, fairly often, not too often, or never? B. How often have you felt
admiration for blacks?

## Values

- `[1]` very often

- `[2]` fairly often

- `[3]` not too often

- `[4]` never
