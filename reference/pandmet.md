# Change in socialization over covid pandemic

pandmet

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking about how often you met with your extended family and
friends in person before the COVID-19 pandemic, would you say you now
meet them in person more often, less often, or about the same?

NOTE: This variable originates on the GSS Next follow-on, and should not
be analyzed without the GSS Next-specific weights WTSSPS_NEXT or
WTSSNRPS_NEXT

## Values

- `[1]` much less often

- `[2]` a little less often

- `[3]` about the same

- `[4]` a little more often

- `[5]` much more often

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
