# Other pacific islander

raceacs14

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What is your race? Indicate one or more races that you consider
yourself to be. Other Pacific Islander As of 2024, RACEACS14 has been
recoded to include RACEACS11 (Native Hawaiian), RACEACS12 (Guamanian or
Chamorro), and RACEACS13 (Samoan). This has been retroactively applied
to all previous years of RACEACS14. The original RACEACS14 containing
data from 2021 to 2022 has been renamed RACEACS14_2122.
