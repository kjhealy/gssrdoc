# In last 5 yrs, r has ever been harassed on job bc r was believed to be gay/les/b

harjob5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In the last five years, have you been harassed verbally or in
writing on the job by a co-worker, supervisor, or boss because you were
believed to be gay, lesbian, or bisexual?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[3]` not employed in last 5 years

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
