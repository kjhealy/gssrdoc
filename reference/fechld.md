# Mother working doesn't hurt children

fechld

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to read several more statements. As I read each
one, please tell me whether you strongly agree, agree, disagree, or
strongly disagree with it. For example, here is the statement: A. A
working mother can establish just as warm and secure a relationship with
her children as a mother who does not work.
