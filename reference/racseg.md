# Whites have right to seg. neighborhood

racseg

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some opinions other people have expressed in
connection with (Negro/Black)-white relations. Which statement on the
card comes closest to how you, yourself, feel? The first one is . . . B.
White people have a right to keep (Negroes/Blacks/African-Americans) out
of their neighborhoods if they want to, and
(Negroes/Blacks/African-Americans) should respect that right.
