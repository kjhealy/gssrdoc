# Use chatrooms about arts

artschat

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Considering all these arts topics, do you participate in chat
rooms, Usenet groups, discussion forums, bulletIn boards, or similar
interactive services devoted to these topics?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
