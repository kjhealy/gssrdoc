# How many years without a job

wojobyrs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Since your first full-time job, has there ever been a time when
you”ve been without a job and not looking for a job? B. How many years
in total?

## Values

- `[1]` less one year

- `[2]` one to 3 years

- `[3]` 3 to 5 years

- `[4]` five - ten years

- `[5]` ten years/over

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
