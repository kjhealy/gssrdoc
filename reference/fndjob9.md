# Heard job other (specify)

fndjob9

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question There are many ways people hear about jobs – such as from ads,
employment agencies, or from other people. Could you tell me how you
found out about work at your present employer (IF SELF-EMPLOYED: current
organization)? Please tell me all the ways that you found out about this
job. I. Other (SPECIFY).
