# Second most important to r

socid2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question We are all part of different groups. Some are more important to
us than others when we think of ourselves. in general, which in the
following list is most important to you in describing who you are? and
the second most important? and the third most important? B. Second Most
Important
