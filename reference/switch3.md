## Details

Question I'd like to go over your religious preferences since you were
raised as a (RELIGION AND/OR DENOMINATION MENTIONED IN RELIG16/
DENOM16). Have you ever had another religious preference besides being
(RELIGION AND/OR CHURCH DENOMINATION menTIONED IN RELIG16/DENOM16).
Yes................(ASK A) No..............(GO TO MARELIG) A. Starting
when you were 16 years old, what other religious preferences have you
had? Please mention your earliest preference first. Any others? 3. Third
Mention
