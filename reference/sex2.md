# Sex of person number 2

sex2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (NAME) is (male/female)? Is that correct? ASK FOR EACH NAME. 2.
Sex of Second Person

## Values

- `[1]` male

- `[2]` female

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
