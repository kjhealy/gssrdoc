# Did r live with partner before marriage

livnowed

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Did you ever live with a partner you didn't marry?

## Values

- `[1]` yes, with a previous partner

- `[2]` yes, with my present partner

- `[3]` yes, with previous and present partner

- `[4]` no, never

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job

  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
