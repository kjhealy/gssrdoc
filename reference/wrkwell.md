# How well youcompare to people do same job

wrkwell

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Compared to other people who do the same or similar kind of
work that you do, how well would you say you do your job? Would you say
much better, somewhat better, about the same, somewhat worse or much
worse?

## Values

- `[1]` much better

- `[2]` somewhat better

- `[3]` about same

- `[4]` somewhat worse

- `[5]` much worse

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
