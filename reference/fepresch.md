# Preschool kids suffer if mother works

fepresch

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to read several more statements. As I read each
one, please tell me whether you strongly agree, agree, disagree, or
strongly disagree with it. For example, here is the statement: C. A
preschool child is likely to suffer if his or her mother works.
