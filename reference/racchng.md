# Try to integrate social club

racchng

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If you and your friends belonged to a social club that would
not let (Whites/Negroes/Blacks) join, would you try to change the rules
so that (Whites/(Negroes/Blacks/African-Americans) could join?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[3]` wdnt belong

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
