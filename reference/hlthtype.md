# Source of health insurance

hlthtype

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What is the source of your health insurance?

## Values

- `[1]` my employer

- `[2]` employer of my spouse/partner

- `[3]` employer of someone else in my family

- `[4]` medicaid

- `[5]` medicare

- `[6]` individual plan from private insurer not related to current or
  past employment

- `[7]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
