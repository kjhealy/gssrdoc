# Importance of race relations issues to r

racimp

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. How important is the race relations issue to you–would you
say it is one of the most important, important, not very important, or
not important at all?

## Values

- `[1]` one of most imp

- `[2]` important

- `[3]` not very imp

- `[4]` not imp at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
