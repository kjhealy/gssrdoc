# Main source of economic support

econsup1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (HAND CARD D24)             What is your main source of
economic support?             Please select one source.

## Values

- `[1]` current spouse/partner

- `[2]` other family members

- `[3]` pension (private or government)

- `[4]` unemployment benefits

- `[5]` social assistance/welfare

- `[6]` occasional work

- `[7]` student loans/stipends

- `[8]` savings

- `[9]` investments or rental income

- `[10]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
