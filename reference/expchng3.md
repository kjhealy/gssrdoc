# Cause of religious change3

expchng3

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Open-ended questions for how people who had undergone spiritual
or religious change described their experiences. Occurrence or aspects
of religious or spiritual change. C. Third Mention

## Values

- `[96]` no mention

- `[97]` nothing special happened

- `[99]` no asnwer

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
