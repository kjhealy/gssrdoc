# How paid in main job

waypaid

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your main job, are you salaried, paid by the hour, or what?

## Values

- `[1]` salaried

- `[2]` paid by the hour

- `[3]` other

- `[4]` commission

- `[5]` self-employed

- `[6]` paid by the job

- `[7]` salary and bonus/commission

- `[8]` tips

- `[9]` piece work

- `[10]` paid by day

- `[11]` paid by percent

- `[12]` paid by contract

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
