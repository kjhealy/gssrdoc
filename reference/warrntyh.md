# Did the seller provide any warranty

warrntyh

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Did the seller guarantee to pay for some or all unexpected
repairs needed for some period after the sale (i.e., did the seller
provide a formal or informal warranty)?

## Values

- `[1]` yes – informal assurance

- `[2]` yes – written guarantee

- `[3]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
