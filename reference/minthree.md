# Imputed poverty line for family of three

minthree

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question And finally, what amount of weekly income would you use as a
"poverty line" for a family of three, made up of a woman and two
children, in this community?

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
