# Region of residence, age 16

reg16

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In what state or foreign country were you living when you were
16 years old? As of 2024, REG16 has been recoded to only account for
four categories. This has been retroactively applied to all previous
years of REG16. The original REG16 containing data from 1972 to 2022 has
been renamed REG16_7222.

## Values

- `[0]` foreign

- `[1]` northeast

- `[2]` midwest

- `[3]` south

- `[4]` west

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
