# Trust results of surveys

polltrue

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How often do you think you can trust the results of surveys, Do
you think they are almost always right, right most of the time, only
some of the time, or hardly ever right?

## Values

- `[1]` almost always

- `[2]` most of the time

- `[3]` some of the time

- `[4]` hardly ever

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
