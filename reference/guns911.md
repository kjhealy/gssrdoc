# Should gun control law be stricter after 911

guns911

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question As a result of the 9/11 terrorist attacks, do you think that
gun control laws should be stricter, making it harder for people to
purchase firearms or that gun control laws should be less strict, making
it easier for people to purchase firearms?

## Values

- `[1]` should be stricter

- `[2]` should be less strict

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
