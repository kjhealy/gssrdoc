# Influence on reducing fed inc taxby economists

ecoinftx

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please look at Card B12. How much influence should each of the
following groups have in deciding whether to reduce federal income
taxes? A. Economists. (Would you say a great deal of influence, a fair
amount, a little influence, or none at all?)

## Values

- `[1]` a great deal of influence

- `[2]` a fair amount

- `[3]` a little influence

- `[4]` none at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
