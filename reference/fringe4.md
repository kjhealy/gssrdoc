# Sick leave with full pay

fringe4

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question I am going to read a list of fringe benefits that workers
sometimes get in addition to their wages. Whether you receive it or not,
please tell me whether you are eligible to receive each fringe benefit.
D. Sick leave with full pay.

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no
