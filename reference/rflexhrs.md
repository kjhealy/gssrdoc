# R's job has flexible hrs.

rflexhrs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question For each statement about your main job below, please circle one
code to show how much you agree or disagree that it applies to your job.
I. My job has flexible working hours

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
