# Total children under 18 in hh (experimental verison)

childs_exp_22

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Calculated variable: Total count of children under 18 in
household As of 2024, CHILDS_EXP has been calculated to only account for
0 to 6+ children in the household. This has been retroactively applied
to all previous years of CHILDS_EXP. The original CHILDS_EXP containing
data in 2022 has been renamed CHILDS_EXP_22.

## Values

- `[8]` 8 or more

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
