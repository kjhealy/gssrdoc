# Mandatory conscription for males?

meserve

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. And how would you feel about such a program for all young
men–Would you strongly favor it, probably favor it, probably oppose it,
or strongly oppose it? RECORD UNDER "B."

## Values

- `[1]` strongly favor

- `[2]` probably favor

- `[3]` probably oppose

- `[4]` strongly oppose

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
