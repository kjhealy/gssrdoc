# How long does it take to get to where mother live

matime1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question About how long would it take to get to where your mother lives?
Think about the time it usually takes door to door.

## Values

- `[1]` less than 2 minutes

- `[2]` less than 15 minutes

- `[3]` between 15 and 30 minutes

- `[4]` between 30 minutes and 1 hour

- `[5]` between 1 and 2 hours

- `[6]` between 2 and 3 hours

- `[7]` between 3 and 5 hours

- `[8]` between 5 and 12 hours

- `[9]` over 12 hours

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
