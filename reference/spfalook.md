# Hours spouse spends looking after family members

spfalook

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question And on average, how many hours a week does he/she spend looking
after family members (e.g. children, elderly, ill or disabled family
members)?

## Values

- `[1]` 1 hour

- `[97]` 97 hours or more
