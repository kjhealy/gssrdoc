# How often did r read bible last year

readword

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you read any part of the Bible at home within the last
year. Yes..........(ASK A)...............1
No….…................................2 IF YES: A. How often have you
read the Bible in the last year? (USE CATEGORIES AS PROBES IF NEEDED).

## Values

- `[1]` several x day

- `[2]` once a day

- `[3]` several x week

- `[4]` once a week

- `[5]` lt once a week

- `[6]` didnt read last year

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
