# Job allows r use of skills

myskills

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I”m going to read you a list of statements that might or
might not describe your main job. Please tell me whether you strongly
agree, agree, disagree, or strongly disagree with each of these
statements. H. My job lets me use my skills and abilities

- `[3]` disagree
