# People act as if they think r is not smart

notsmart

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your day-to-day life how often have any of the following
things happened to you? People act as if they think you are not smart.

## Values

- `[1]` almost every day

- `[2]` at least once a week

- `[3]` a few times a month

- `[4]` a few times a year

- `[5]` less than once a year

- `[6]` never

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
