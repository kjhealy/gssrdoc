# Ones own efforts don't count

usclass4

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are different opinions about social differences in this
country. Please tell me for each one whether you strongly agree,
somewhat, agree, somewhat disagree, or strongly disagree. READ EACH
STATEMENT. CIRCLE ONE CODE FOR EACH. D. What one gets in life hardly
depends at all on one's own efforts, but rather on the economic
situation, job opportunities, union agreements, and the social services
provided by the government.
