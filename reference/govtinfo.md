# Should govt info be public

govtinfo

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (HAND CARD C6)  Here is a scale from 0 to 10 where 0 is "All
government information should be publically available, even if this
means a risk to public security" and 10 is "public security should be
given priority, even if this means limiting access to government
information". Where would you place yourself on such a scale? ENTER THE
RESPONDENT'S ANSWER, 0-10:

## Values

- `[0]` 0 - all government information should be publically available,
  even if this meant a risk to public security

- `[10]` 10 - public security should be given priority, even if this
  means limiting access to government information

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
