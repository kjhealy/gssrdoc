# Should govt collect citizen info without knowledge

givinfusa

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people think that governments should have the right to
take certain measures in the name of national security. Others
disagree.  Do you think that the American government should or should
not have the right to do the following? Collect information about anyone
living in America without their knowledge?  Would you say...

## Values

- `[1]` definitely should have the right

- `[2]` probably should have the right

- `[3]` probably should not have the right

- `[4]` definitely should not have the right

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
