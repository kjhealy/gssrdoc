# How many of r's friends know each other?

frndknow

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people have friends who mostly know one another. Other
people have friends who don't know one another. would you say that all
of your friends know one another, most of your friends know one another,
only a few of your friends know one another, or none of your friends
know one another?

## Values

- `[1]` all know each other

- `[2]` most know each other

- `[3]` few know each other

- `[4]` none know each other

- `[5]` no friends(vol.)

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
