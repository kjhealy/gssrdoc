# How difficult r takes an hour off during working hours

timeoff

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How difficult would it be for you to take an hour or two off
during working hours, to take care of personal or family matters?

## Values

- `[1]` not difficult at all

- `[2]` not too difficult

- `[3]` somewhat difficult

- `[4]` very difficult

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
