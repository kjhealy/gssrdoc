# True-false: inuit live north of the arctic circle

inuit

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question The next few questions are about the Arctic and the Antarctic.
The Arctic is the region around the North Pole; Antarctic is the region
that contains the South Pole. These questions are like ones you might
see on a television game show. If you don't know or aren't sure, just
tell me so, and we will skip to the next question. Remember true, false,
or don't know. C. Inuit (often called Eskimos) live north of the Arctic
Circle. (Is that true or false?)
