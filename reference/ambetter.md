# Agree america is a better country

ambetter

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with the following
statements? D. Generally speaking, America is a better country than most
other countries

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
