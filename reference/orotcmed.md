# In what order take non-prescription medication

orotcmed

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are the things you said NAME should do. INTERVIEWER: READ
"YES" ANSWERS AND ASK: In what order should s/he do them? H. Take
non-prescription medication, like over the counter sleeping pills.

## Values

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
