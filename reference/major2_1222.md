## Source

## Details

Question What was your major or field of study when you received your
(RESPONDENT'S COLLEGE DEGREE) degree? As of 2024, MAJOR2 has been
recoded to only account for 39 categories. This has been retroactively
applied to all previous years of MAJOR2. The original MAJOR2 containing
data from 2012 to 2022 has been renamed MAJOR2_1222.
