# People can or cannot be trusted

trust5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B1. Generally speaking, would you say that most people can be
trusted, or that you can't be too careful in dealing with people? please
tell me what you think, where 1 means you can't be too careful and 5
means most people can be trusted.

## Values

- `[1]` you can't be too careful

- `[5]` most people can be trusted

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
