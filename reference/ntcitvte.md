# Long-term residents should vote

ntcitvte

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question On a scale of 1 to 7, where 1 is not at all important and 7 is
very important, how important is it: C. That long-term residents of a
country, who are not citizens, have the right to vote in that country”s
national elections

## Values

- `[1]` not at all important

- `[7]` very important

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
