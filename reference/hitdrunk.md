# Drunk bumped into man & wife

hitdrunk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Are there any situations that you can imagine in which you
would approve of a man punching an adult male stranger? B. Was drunk and
bumped into the man and his wife on the street?
