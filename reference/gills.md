# How fish get oxygen

gills

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How do most fish get the oxygen they need to survive?

## Values

- `[1]` they take in water and break it down into hydrogen and oxygen

- `[2]` using their gills, they take in oxygen that is dissolved in
  water

- `[3]` they get their oxygen from the food they eat

- `[4]` they come to the surface every few minutes to breathe air into
  their lungs
