# R last use crack cocaine

crack30

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How long has it been since you last used "crack" cocaine in
chunk or rock form?

## Values

- `[1]` within the past 30 days

- `[2]` more than 30 days ago but within the past 12 months

- `[3]` more than 12 months ago but within the past 3 years

- `[4]` more than 3 years ago

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
