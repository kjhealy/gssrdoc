# How often r watches public tv shows

tvpbs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question I'll now ask about some different kinds of television shows.
Would you tell me how often you watch prime-time drama or situation
comedy programs? Would you say every day, several times a week, several
times a month, rarely, or never? C. Programs shown on public television.
