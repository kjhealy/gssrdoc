# Wrong to give govt false info for benefits?

govcheat

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Consider the situations listed below. Do you feel it is wrong
or not wrong if. . . B. A person gives the government incorrect
information about himself to get government benefits that he is not
entitled to.

## Values

- `[1]` not wrong

- `[2]` a bit wrong

- `[3]` wrong

- `[4]` seriously wrong

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
