# Business profits are distributed fairly

equal7

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question SHUFFLE THE GREY CARDS. READ STATEMENTS IN ORDER OF CARDS TO
RESPONDENT. CIRCLE APPROPRIATE CODE FOR EACH STATEMENT. G. Generally
speaking, business profits are distributed fairly in the United States.

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` somewhat agree

- `[3]` somewhat disagree

- `[4]` strongly disagree
