# Responsibility for internet data protection

dataprot

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Who should be mostly responsible for protecting personal
information when using the internet?

## Values

- `[1]` online companies - as they need to ensure they process personal
  information safely

- `[2]` people - as they need to take care of their personal information

- `[3]` public authorities - as they need to ensure that personal
  information is protected
