# Allow communist to speak

allowcom

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question D. Now, I would like to ask you some questions about a man who
admits he is a Communist. Suppose this admitted Communist wanted to make
a speech in your community. Should he be allowed to speak?

## Values

- `[1]` yes, allow

- `[2]` no, not allow

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
