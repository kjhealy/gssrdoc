# Lot of the average man getting worse

anomia5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to read you several more statements. Some people
agree with a statement, others disagree. As I read each one, tell me
whether you more or less agree with it, or more or less disagree. E. In
spite of what some people say, the lot (situation/condition) of the
average man is getting worse, not better.
