# Outside leisure activity in the past 12 months

activnat

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In the last twelve months how often, if at all, have you
engaged in any leisure activities outside in nature, such as hiking,
bird watching, swimming, skiing, other outdoor activities, or just
relaxing?

- `[2]` several times a week

- `[4]` several times a year

- `[5]` never
