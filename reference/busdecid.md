# Govt should let biz decide how to protect envir

busdecid

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. And which one of the following closest to your views?
Government should let businesses decide for themselves how to protect
the environment, even if it means they don't always do the right thing,
or government should pass laws to make businesses protect the
environment, even if it interferes with business' right to make their
own decisions.
