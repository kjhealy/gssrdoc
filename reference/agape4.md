# Would endure all things for the one r love

agape4

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you agree strongly, agree somewhat, neither agree nor
disagree, disagree somewhat, or strongly disagree with the following
statements? D. I would endure all things for the sake of the one I love.

## Values

- `[1]` agree strongly

- `[2]` agree somewhat

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree somewhat

- `[5]` disagree strongly

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
