# 1st mention what r did when felt mental illness

mnildo1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (IF NO TO EVBRKDWN, ASK EVMHP) Have you ever felt you had a
mental health problem? B. (IF YES TO EVMHP ASK:) What did you do about
it? (PROBE: Anything else? until R says no.) Which of these things did
you do first; second; third? (INTERVIEWER: Sequence list; read back if
needed.) B1. What R. did about the mental illness – First mention.

## Values

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
