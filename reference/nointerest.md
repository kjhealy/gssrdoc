# How often felt no interest in doing things in past 2 weeks

nointerest

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by the
following problems: Little interest or pleasure in doing things.

## Values

- `[1]` not at all

- `[2]` several days

- `[3]` more than half the days

- `[4]` nearly every day

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
