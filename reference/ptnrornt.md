# Recent partner's sexual orientation

ptnrornt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question To the best of your knowledge, which of the following best
describes how your most recent sexual partner thinks of themselves?

## Values

- `[1]` gay, lesbian, or homosexual

- `[2]` bisexual

- `[3]` heterosexual or straight

- `[4]` i have never had a sexual partner

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
