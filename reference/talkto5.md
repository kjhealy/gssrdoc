# How often does r talk to person \#5?

talkto5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking about how often you usually talk to (NAME), on the
average, Do you talk to (him/her) almost every day, at least Once a
week, at least once a month, or less than once a month? ASK FOR EACH
NAME. 5. Talks to Fifth Person

## Values

- `[1]` almost every day

- `[2]` at least once a week

- `[3]` at least once a month

- `[4]` less than once a month

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
