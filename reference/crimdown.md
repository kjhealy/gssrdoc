# What if more restrictions reduce crime

crimdown

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Suppose research proves that more restrictions on handguns
would reduce violent crime. Which of these two reactions would be closer
to your position?

## Values

- `[1]` would change mind and support more restrictions

- `[2]` would still oppose more restrictions

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
