# Better for man to work, woman tend home

fefam

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to read several more statements. As I read each
one, please tell me whether you strongly agree, agree, disagree, or
strongly disagree with it. For example, here is the statement: D. It is
much better for everyone involved if the man is the achiever outside the
home and the woman takes care of the home and family.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` disagree

- `[4]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
