# Most hrs/week worked in past month

mosthrs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In the last month, what is the greatest number of hours you
worked in a week, at all paid jobs? Please consider all hours, including
any extra hours, overtime, work you did at home for your job, and time
you spent on work that may not have been directly billable or
compensated. HOURS:
