# Believe doctors can control my pain

termpain

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, I would like to talk about concerns you may have when
considering what may happen at the end of your life. please tell me how
much you agree or disagree with each of these statements: D. I believe
that the doctors will be able to control my pain.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
