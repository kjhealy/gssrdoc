# Frequency of internet use over past 12 months

intrnetuse

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question During the last 12 months, how often, if at all, did you
typically use the Internet on any device whether for work or personal
use? If you are uncertain about your usage, please select the answer
that comes closest to your use.

## Values

- `[1]` almost all the time

- `[2]` several times a day

- `[3]` once a day

- `[4]` several times a week

- `[5]` several times a month

- `[6]` less often

- `[7]` never

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
