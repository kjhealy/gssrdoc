# Politics and public life

imppol

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question On these cards are various aspects of life. We would like to
know how important each of these aspects of life is for you. On each of
these Cards you see on the right hand side a scale with seven points.
The lowest point with number 1 indicates that this aspect of life is
unimportant to you. Point 7 at the top indicates that particular aspect
of life is very important to you. The numbers in between indicate
varying degrees of importance. Please take a look at all the cards
first. Then, tell me for each card its letter and the number you've
decided on. SHUFFLE THE SET OF BLUE CardS BEFORE HANDING TO RESPONDENT.
G. Politics and public life.

## Values

- `[1]` unimportant

- `[7]` very important

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
