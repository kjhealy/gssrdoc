# Govt should aid women seeking higher ed

fehlpcol

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are three things the government might do. Some people are
in favor of them while other people are against them. Please circle one
number for each statement to show how you feel. B. The government should
increase opportunities for women to go to college.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly in favor

- `[2]` in favor

- `[3]` neither

- `[4]` against

- `[5]` strongly against

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
