# What is r's race 1st mention

racecen1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What is your race? Indicate one or more races that you consider
yourself to be. A. First mention As of 2024, RACECEN1 has been recoded
to combine Native Hawaiian, Guamanian or Chamorro, and Samoan into the
"Other Pacific Islander" category. This has been retroactively applied
to all previous years of RACECEN1. The original RACECEN1 containing data
from 2000 to 2022 has been renamed RACECEN1_0022.
