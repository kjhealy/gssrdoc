# How far in advance know work schedule (2022 version)

knowschd1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How far in advance do you usually know what days and hours you
will need to work?

## Values

- `[1]` one week or less

- `[2]` between 1 and 2 weeks

- `[3]` between 3 and 4 weeks

- `[4]` 4 weeks or more

- `[5]` my schedule never changes

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
