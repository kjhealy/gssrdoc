# Too diff to do anything about envir

toodifme

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with each of these
statements? A. It is just too difficult for someone like me to do much
about the environment.

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
