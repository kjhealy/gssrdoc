# Science breaks down ideas of right & wrong

scimoral

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some things that have been said about science. Would
you tell me if you tend to agree or disagree with them? D. One of the
bad effects of science is that it breaks down people”s ideas of right
and wrong.
