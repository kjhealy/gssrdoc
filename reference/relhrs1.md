# Hours religious act in home

relhrs1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. In the past month, about how many hours have you spent doing
religious activities in your home (such as time spent praying,
meditating, reading religious books, listening to religious broadcasts,
etc.)?
