# Benefit of nanotechnology outweigh harmful results

nanowill

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. Nanotechnology works at the molecular level atom by atom to
build new structures, materials, and machines. People have frequently
noted that new technologies have produced both benefits and harmful
results. Do you think the benefits of nanotechnology will outweigh the
harmful results or the harmful results will outweigh the benefits?

## Values

- `[1]` benefits greater

- `[2]` about equal (if volunteered)

- `[3]` harmful results greater

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
