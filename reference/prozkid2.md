# Give kid prozac if is not paying attention

prozkid2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How likely would you be to give doctor prescribed Prozac to
your child or a child you were responsible for in the following
situations (A child would be considered anyone between the age of 8 and
15). B. ...because s/he is not paying attention at school, does not
follow through with school work and chores,

has difficulty organizing activities, is easily distracted, talks
excessively, and seems to run around or fidget constantly.
