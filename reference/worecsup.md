# Do you worry about losing economic support

worecsup

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question To what extent, if at all, do you worry about the possibility
of losing this main economic support?              (Would you say...)

## Values

- `[1]` i worry a great deal

- `[2]` i worry to some extent

- `[3]` i worry a little

- `[4]` i don't worry at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
