# How much info does r have on race issues

racinfo

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. How much information do you have about the race relations
issue. Do you have all the information you need, most of the
information, some information, or very little information?

## Values

- `[1]` all info r needs

- `[2]` most info

- `[3]` some info

- `[4]` very little info

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
