# R accept others even when they do things wrong

accptoth

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question The following are things that you may experience in your daily
life. Please tell me how often these occur. B. I accept others even when
they do things I think are wrong.

## Values

- `[1]` many times a day

- `[2]` every day

- `[3]` most days

- `[4]` some days

- `[5]` once in a while

- `[6]` never or almost never

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
