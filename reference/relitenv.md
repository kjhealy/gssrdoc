# Strength of affiliation (with volunteered response on web)

relitenv

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Would you call yourself a strong (PREFERENCE NAMED IN RELIG) or
a not very strong (PREFERENCE NAMED IN RELIG)?

## Values

- `[1]` strong

- `[2]` not very strong

- `[3]` somewhat strong (phone mode only: volunteered)

- `[4]` no religion

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
