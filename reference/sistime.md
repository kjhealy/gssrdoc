# Travel time to sister

sistime

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How many sisters age 18 or older do you have? (We mean sisters
who are still alive; please include step-sisters, half-sisters, and
adopted sisters.) B. About how long would it take you to get to where
your sister lives? Think of the time it usually takes door to door.

## Values

- `[1]` lt 15 min

- `[2]` 15-30 min

- `[3]` 30 min-1 hr

- `[4]` 1-2 hr

- `[5]` 2-3 hr

- `[6]` 3-5 hr

- `[7]` 5-12 hr

- `[8]` 12+ hr

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
