# R favors women as priests

fepriest

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. IF CATHOLIC RESPONDENT, ASK FEPRIEST. OTHERS GO TO FECLERGY.
As I read this statement, will you tell me whether you agree strongly,
agree somewhat, disagree somewhat, or disagree strongly? It would be a
good thing if women were allowed to be ordained as priests.

## Values

- `[1]` agree strongly

- `[2]` agree somewhat

- `[3]` disagree somewhat

- `[4]` disagree strongly

- `[8]` no opinion

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
