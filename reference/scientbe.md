# Scientists want to make life better for avg person

scientbe

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question G. Most scientists want to work on things that will make life
better for the average person. Do you strongly agree, agree, disagree,
or strongly disagree?

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` disagree

- `[4]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job

  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
