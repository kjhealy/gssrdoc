# In what order check into a mental hospital

ormntlhs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are the things you said NAME should do. INTERVIEWER: READ
"YES" ANSWERS AND ASK: In what order should s/he do them? J. Check into
a mental hospital.
