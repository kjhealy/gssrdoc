# R relation to r 5th sibling

sbrel5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I have some questions about your sisters and brothers.
These include your full, adopted, step, or half siblings. Let's start
with the oldest and work down to the youngest. (RECORD THIS INFORMATION
FOR UP TO THE NINE OLDEST SIBLINGS IN TABLE TO RIGHT). D5. Relation to
R. of fifth sibling

## Values

- `[1]` full

- `[2]` adopted

- `[3]` step

- `[4]` half

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
