# X s situation caused by: bad character

mhchrctr

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Is it very likely, somewhat likely, not very likely, or not at
all likely that (NAME)”s situation is caused by… A. (His/Her) own bad
character.

## Values

- `[1]` very likely

- `[2]` somewhat likely

- `[3]` not very likely

- `[4]` not at all likely

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
