# Test of knowledge about probablity2

odds2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, think about this situation. A doctor tells a couple that
their genetic makeup means that they've got one in four chances of
having a child with an inherited illness. B. Does this mean that each of
the couple's children will have the same risk of suffering from the
illness?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
