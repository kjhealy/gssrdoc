# R agrees immoral person corrupts society

rotapple

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please consider the following statements and tell me whether
you agree strongly, agree somewhat, disagree somewhat or disagree
strongly with each statement. C. Immoral actions by one person can
corrupt society in general.

## Values

- `[1]` agree strongly

- `[2]` agree somewhat

- `[3]` disagree somewhat

- `[4]` disagree strongly

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
