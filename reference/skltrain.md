# R had job skill training in thepast 12 months

skltrain

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In addition to your main job, do you do any other work for
additional income? IF YES F. Over the past 12 months, have you had any
training to improve your job skills?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
