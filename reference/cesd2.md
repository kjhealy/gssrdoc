# How much time sleep was restless in past wk

cesd2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (HAND CARD N1) (Using this card, please tell me how much of the
time during the past week...) ...your sleep was restless?

## Values

- `[1]` none or almost none of the time

- `[2]` some of the time

- `[3]` most of the time

- `[4]` all or almost all of the time

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
