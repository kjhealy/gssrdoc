# Has r figured out problems with software on own

doonown

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question When you want to learn how to do something new with your
software and you need advice on how to do it, which of the following do
you do? (Name as many as are correct.) (PROMPT: Anything else?) G.
Figure it out yourself?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
