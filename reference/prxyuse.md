# Likelihood r ask someone to use the internet on their behalf in near future

prxyuse

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If you needed to do something on the Internet in the near
future, how likely is it that someone will do it for you?

## Values

- `[1]` very likely

- `[2]` rather likely

- `[3]` rather unlikely

- `[4]` very unlikely

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
