# Who in household does housework

sharehhw

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which of the following best applies to the sharing of household
work between you and your spousepartfill?

- `[2]` r does a bit more than fair share

- `[3]` r does roughly fair share

- `[4]` r does a bit less than fair share

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
