# Image of god as spouse

spouse

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question When you think about God, how likely are each of these images
to come to your mind? Would you say extremely likely, somewhat likely,
not too likely, or not likely at all? (HAND CARD AD. READ EACH ITEM.)
(CIRCLE ONE NUMBER FOR EACH WORD) H. Spouse

## Values

- `[1]` extremely likely

- `[2]` somewhat likely

- `[3]` not too likely

- `[4]` not likely at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
