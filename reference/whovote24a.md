# Voting intention in 2024 election (post july 25 2024)

whovote24a

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In the November election for President, do you think you will
probably vote for Donald Trump, Kamala Harris, someone else, or will you
probably not vote?
