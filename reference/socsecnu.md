# Social security phase out or continue

socsecnu

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you think it would be better to phase out Social Security
and require people to take what they and their employers now pay into
Social Security and invest it in mandatory private retirement accounts,
or do you think Social Security should continue much as it is now?

## Values

- `[1]` phase out social security+

- `[2]` continue as is

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
