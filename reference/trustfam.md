# Would trust my family

trustfam

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People sometimes are incapable of making decisions about their
care and medical treatment at the end of life. If you were incapable,
how much trust would you put in the following people to do what was best
for you? READ RESPONSE CATEGORIES A. I would trust my family.

## Values

- `[1]` completely

- `[2]` a great deal

- `[3]` somewhat

- `[4]` only a little

- `[5]` not at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
