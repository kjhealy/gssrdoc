# Aim of art

aimofart

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question I'm going to read two statements about what works of art should
Do. Please tell me which statement is closest to your opinion.

## Values

- `[1]` art works should celebrate what is most beautiful about the
  world and the human spirit

- `[2]` art works should freely express an artist’s deepest thoughts and
  emotions, good or bad
