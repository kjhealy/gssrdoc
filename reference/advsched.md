# How far in advance do you schedule work

advsched

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How far in advance do you usually know what days and hours you
will need to work? (HAND CARD L2)

## Values

- `[1]` one day or less in advance

- `[2]` 2 to 3 days in advance

- `[3]` 4 to 7 days in advance

- `[4]` between 1 and 2 weeks in advance

- `[5]` between 3 and 4 weeks in advance

- `[6]` more than 4 weeks in advance

- `[7]` my schedule never changes

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
