# How important: research takes place in a laboratory

sciimp8

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to read you some statements about science and
scientists. Please look at Card B19. How important are each of the
following in making something scientific? H. The research takes place in
a laboratory. (Would you say this is very important, pretty important,
not too important, or not important at all in making something
scientific?)

## Values

- `[1]` very important

- `[2]` pretty important

- `[3]` not too important

- `[4]` not important at all
