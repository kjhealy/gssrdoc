# Rich buy better health care than poor

richhlth

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Is it just or unjust - right or wrong - that people with higher
incomes can: A. Buy better health care than people with lower incomes

## Values

- `[1]` very just, definitely right

- `[2]` somewhat just, right

- `[3]` neither just nor unjust, mixed feelings

- `[4]` somewhat unjust, wrong

- `[5]` very unjust, definitely wrong

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
