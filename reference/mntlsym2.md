# 2nd mention, what mental illness is, symptoms

mntlsym2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Of course, everybody hears a good deal about physical illness
and disease, but now, what about the ones we call mental or nervous
illness?...When you hear someone say that a person is "mentally-ill,"
what does that mean to you? (PROBES: How would you describe a person who
is mentally-ill? What do you think a mentally-ill person is like? What
does a person like this do that tells you he is mentally-ill? How does a
person like this act?) 2. Symptoms or manifestations of mental illness –
Second mention.
