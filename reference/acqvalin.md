# Number of linda in voluntary association

acqvalin

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Next, we are going to ask about people that you are acquainted
with through schools, clubs, associations, or places of worship. B. How
many of the people you are acquainted with through schools, clubs,
associations, or places of worship are named Linda?

## Values

- `[1]` 0

- `[2]` 1

- `[3]` 2 - 5

- `[4]` 6 - 10

- `[5]` more than 10

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
