# Close relative marry no. white

marno

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What about having a close relative marry a Jewish person? Would
you be very in favor of it happening, somewhat In favor, neither in
favor nor opposed to it happening, somewhat opposed, or very opposed to
it happening? E. A white raised in the North? (INTERVIEWER: READ
OPPOSITE REGION - SOUTH = CENSUS REGIONS - SOUTH ATLANTIC, SOUTH EAST,
CENTRAL, SOUTH WEST CENTRAL; NORTH = EVERYWHERE ELSE)

## Values

- `[1]` strongly favor

- `[2]` favor

- `[3]` neither favor nor oppose

- `[4]` oppose

- `[5]` strongly oppose

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
