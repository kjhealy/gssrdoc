# Position of pet questions in questionnaire

petsseq

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Position of pet questions in questionnaire NOTE: This variable
originates on the GSS Next follow-on, and should not be analyzed without
the GSS Next-specific weights WTSSPS_NEXT or WTSSNRPS_NEXT

## Values

- `[1]` beginning

- `[2]` middle

- `[3]` end

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
