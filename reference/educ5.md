# Education level of person \#5

educ5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question This card (CARD BH) lists general levels of education. As far
as you know, what is (NAME)”s highest level of education? PROBE: Your
best guess. RECORD VERBATIM IF NOT CODEABLE. ASK FOR EACH NAME. 5.
Education of Fifth Person

## Values

- `[0]` 1-6 years

- `[1]` 7-9 years

- `[2]` 10-12 years

- `[3]` h.s. grad

- `[4]` some college

- `[5]` asso. degree

- `[6]` bach. degree

- `[7]` grad or prof

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
