# Salience, scientific community

salsci

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How often would you say that you and your friends think about
topics we've been discussing during the interview? would you say that
you and your friends think about (READ EACH ITEM A-E) very often,
sometimes, or almost never? E. The scientific community
