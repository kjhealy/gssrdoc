# Financial situation compared to last year

yrfnce

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Compared to 12 months ago, how do you think the financial
situation of your household has changed? Would you say the current
situation is...

## Values

- `[1]` much better

- `[2]` somewhat better

- `[3]` about the same

- `[4]` somewhat worse

- `[5]` much worse

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
