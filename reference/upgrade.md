# Refuse vols with no basic skills?

upgrade

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Many people who want to volunteer for service in the armed
forces Do not have the necessary basic skills like reading, writing and
arithmetic. Do you think the armed forces should refuse to accept such
volunteers, or should they accept them and give them the necessary
education?
