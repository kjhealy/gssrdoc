# Times r sought job information from friends

jobfrnds

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'd like to ask you about how you navigate around the Web.
How frequently do you use each of the following ways of getting to the
web sites you visit: Click on a link in an email message from a friend,
relative, or co-worker. F. Friends outside of work or relatives?

## Values

- `[0]` not at all

- `[1]` 1 or 2 times

- `[2]` 3-5 times

- `[3]` 6 or more times

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
