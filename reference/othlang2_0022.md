# What other languages does r speak

othlang2_0022

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What other language(s) do you speak? Second response. As of
2024, OTHLANG2 has been recoded to only account for seven language
categories. This has been retroactively applied to all previous years of
OTHLANG2. The original OTHLANG2 containing data from 2000 to 2022 has
been renamed OTHLANG2_0022.
