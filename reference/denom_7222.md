# Specific denomination

denom_7222

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What is your religious preference? Is it Protestant, Catholic,
Jewish, some other religion, or no religion? A. What specific
denomination is that, if any? As of 2024, DENOM has been recoded to only
account for seven broad denomination categories. This has been
retroactively applied to all previous years of DENOM. The original DENOM
containing data 1972 to 2022 has been renamed DENOM_7222.
