# Pro-athletes giving thanks to god

godsport

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you strongly approve of, somewhat approve of, neither
approve of nor oppose, somewhat oppose, or strongly oppose the following
public expressions of religion: B. Pro-athletes giving thanks to God
during sports events?

## Values

- `[1]` strongly approve

- `[2]` somewhat approve

- `[3]` not apprve/oppse

- `[4]` somewhat oppose

- `[5]` strongly oppose

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
