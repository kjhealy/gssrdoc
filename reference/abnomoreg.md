# Married–wants no more children (grid on web)

abnomoreg

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (Pleae indicate whether or not you think it should be possible
for a pregnant woman to obtain a legal abortion . . .) If she is married
and does not want any more children?
