# Currently work for military or dod?

milwrknw

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you ever had a civilian job with the armed forces or the
Defense Department? A. Do you have a job now with the armed forces or
with the Defense Department?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
