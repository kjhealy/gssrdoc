# Percentage of some college educ in occ10 based on acs 2010

sei10educ

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Percentage of those who had some college or more education
among those who had the same occupation as in OCC10 in American
Community Survey between 2010 and 2012 (25-64 years old only). This
percentage is not from the GSS but it is used to calculate SEI10.
