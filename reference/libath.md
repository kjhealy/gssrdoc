# Allow anti-religious book in library

libath

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question There are always some people whose ideas are considered bad or
dangerous by other people. For instance, somebody who is against all
churches and religion . . . C. If some people in your community
suggested that a book he wrote against churches and religion should be
taken out of your public library, would you favor removing this book, or
not?
