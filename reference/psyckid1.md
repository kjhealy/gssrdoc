# Will give kid med because kid is hostile

psyckid1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How likely would you be to give doctor-prescribed psychiatric
medication to your child or a child you were responsible for in the
following situations (A child would be considered anyone between the
ages of 8 and 15). A....because s/he is hostile, often loses his/her
temper, often argues with adults, actively defies authority and seems
spiteful or vindictive.
