# Aged should be able to support family

supfam1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People differ in their ideas about what it takes for a young
person to become an adult these days. How important is it for them to
be... Be capable of supporting a family financially A. By what age
should this normally occur?
