# You train employees safe use equipment

supduty1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here is a card listing 7 different kinds of oversight
activities. Which ones do you do as part of your supervisory
responsibilities? Please mention all that apply: A. Train employees in
the safe use of equipment or tools.
