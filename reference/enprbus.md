# Most important environment problem

enprbus

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question D. Here is a list of some different environmental problems.
Which problem, if any, do you think is the most important for America as
a whole?

## Values

- `[1]` air pollution

- `[2]` chemicals and pesticides

- `[3]` water shortage

- `[4]` water pollution

- `[5]` nuclear waste

- `[6]` domestic waste disposal

- `[7]` climate change

- `[8]` genetically modified foods

- `[9]` using up our natural resources

- `[10]` none of these

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
