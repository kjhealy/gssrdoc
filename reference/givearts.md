# Contributions to arts culture humanities

givearts

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question During the last year, approximately how much money did you and
the other family members in your household contribute to each of the
following: D. Were any of your contributions to organizations involved
in the arts, culture, or the humanities?
