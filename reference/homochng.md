# Homosexuality: inherent or choice?

homochng

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you think being homosexual is something people choose to be,
or do you think it is something they cannot change?

## Values

- `[1]` something people choose to be

- `[2]` something they cannot change

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
