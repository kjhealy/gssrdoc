# General happiness - follow-on version

happy_next

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Taken all together, how would you say things are these
days—would you say that you are very happy, pretty happy, or not too
happy? NOTE: This variable originates on the GSS Next follow-on, and
should not be analyzed without the GSS Next-specific weights WTSSPS_NEXT
or WTSSNRPS_NEXT

## Values

- `[1]` very happy

- `[2]` pretty happy

- `[3]` not too happy

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
