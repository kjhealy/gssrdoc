# Asked relatives friends colleagues to help

askhelp

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking about the last 12 months, have you done any of the
following in order to find a job? F. Asked relatives, friends, or
colleagues to help you find a job?

## Values

- `[1]` no

- `[2]` yes, once or twice

- `[3]` yes, more than twice

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
