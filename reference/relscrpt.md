# R has read religious text outside of a service in the past year

relscrpt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question During the last 12 months, have you read or listened to the
reading of any holy scripture such as the Bible, Buddhist sutra, Koran,
Sruti, Torah, or other religious scripture, not counting any reading
that happened during a worship service?
