# Children attend christian school

chrissch

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do/did all of your children attend public school for all of
their elementary and high school education? A. Which types of school
do/did your children attend? 3. Christian school (not Catholic)

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
