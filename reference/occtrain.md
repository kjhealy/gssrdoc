# Gone thru specialized training

occtrain

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. Have you gone through any specialized training in school to
obtain a vocational certificate, diploma, degree, or license for this
occupation?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable

  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
