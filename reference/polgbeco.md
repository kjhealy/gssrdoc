# What is your view on politics in us & world

polgbeco

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (HAND CARD C5) Here are two options about what affects policies
in America. Which of them comes closest to your view?

## Values

- `[1]` policies in america depend on what is happening in the world
  economy, rather than who is in government

- `[2]` policies in america depend more on who is in government, rather
  than what is happening in the world economy

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
