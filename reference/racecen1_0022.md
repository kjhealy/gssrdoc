# What is r's race, first mention

racecen1_0022

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What is your race? Indicate one or more races that you consider
yourself to be. A. First mention

As of 2024, RACECEN1 has been recoded to coarsen certain sensitive
categories. This has been retroactively applied to all previous years of
RACECEN1. The original RACECEN1 containing data from 2000 to 2022 has
been renamed RACECEN1_0022.
