# Med just prevent family from working out problem

pryntfam

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, I'd like to ask you for your opinions on several issues.
For each question, please tell me if you strongly agree, agree somewhat,
disagree somewhat or strongly disagree with the statement. G.
Medications for behavior problems just prevent families from working out
problems themselves.
