# Don't have any say about what the government does

poleff11

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with each of the following
statements? PLEASE CHECK ONE BOX ON EACH LINE. A. People like me don't
have any say about what the government does

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
