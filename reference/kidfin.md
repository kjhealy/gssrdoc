# Better not to have kids due to \$ burden

kidfin

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you agree or disagree ... E. It is better not to have
children because they are such a heavy financial burden.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
