# Hubby or wife earns more dollars

earnshh

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. Considering all sources of income, between you and your
spouse/partner, who has the higher income?

## Values

- `[1]` spouse has no income

- `[2]` i have much higher income

- `[3]` i have higher income

- `[4]` we have about the same income

- `[5]` spouse has a higher income

- `[6]` spouse partner has much higher income

- `[7]` i have no income

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
