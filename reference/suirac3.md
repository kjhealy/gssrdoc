# Race of suicide victim 3rd closest to r

suirac3

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question We would like to know a few other things about that person. C.
What was that person”s race? Was it black, white, hispanic, or other?

- `[1]` black

- `[2]` white

- `[4]` other
