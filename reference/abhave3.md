# Have abortion if couldn't afford child

abhave3

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (WOMEN): Would you yourself have an abortion if… (MEN): Would
you yourself advise the woman you were married to have an abortion if…
C. You did not think you could afford another child?
