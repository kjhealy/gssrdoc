# R's insurance plan has limits

insrlmts

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Does your health insurance plan have restrictions, for example
limits on getting tests and getting care from specialists?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job

  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
