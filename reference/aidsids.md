# Require aids victims to wear id tags

aidsids

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you support or oppose the following measures to deal with
AIDS? C. Require people with the AIDS virus to wear identification tags
that look like those carried by people with allergies or diabetes

## Values

- `[1]` support

- `[2]` oppose

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
