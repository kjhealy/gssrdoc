# How much pay dependent on supervising others

paysup

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In deciding how much people ought to earn, how important should
each of these things be, in your opinion: C. Whether the job requires
supervising others (how important should that be in deciding pay)?

## Values

- `[1]` essential

- `[2]` very important

- `[3]` fairly important

- `[4]` not very important

- `[5]` not important at all

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
