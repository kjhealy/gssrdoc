# Desire to be closer to god

uniongod

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question The following questions deal with possible daily spiritual
experiences. To what extent can you say you experience the following: D.
I desire to be closer to or in union with God.

## Values

- `[1]` many times a day

- `[2]` every day

- `[3]` most days

- `[4]` some days

- `[5]` once in a while

- `[6]` never/almst nevr

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
