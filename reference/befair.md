# How often do you think people take advantage

befair

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How often do you think that people would try to take advantage
of you if they got the chance and how often would they try to be fair?

## Values

- `[1]` try to take advantage almost all of the time

- `[2]` try to take advantage most of the time

- `[3]` try to be fair most of the time

- `[4]` try to be fair almost all of the time

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
