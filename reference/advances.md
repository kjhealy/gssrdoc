# Have you advanced, same level or lost

advances

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Since your first full-time job with this organization, have you
advanced rapidly, made steady advances, stayed at about the same level
or lost some ground?

## Values

- `[1]` advanced rapidly

- `[2]` steady advance

- `[3]` samelevel

- `[4]` lost some ground

- `[7]` 1st job with org

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
