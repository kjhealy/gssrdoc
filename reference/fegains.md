# Best way for women to improve position

fegains

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People have different ideas about the best way for women to
improve their position in American life and politics. Some people think
that the best way for women to improve their position is through women's
rights groups, they would be at point 1 on this card. Other people think
that the best way for women to improve their position is for each
individual women to become better trained and more qualified. They would
be located at point 7. And other people have opinions somewhere in
between. Where would you place yourself on this scale?
