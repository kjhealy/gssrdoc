# Influence of children's programs on public tv

infpubtv

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Children are exposed to many influences in their daily lives.
What kind of influence does each of the following have on children: Very
positive, positive, neutral, negative, very negative?: B. Children”s
programs on television.

## Values

- `[1]` very positive

- `[2]` positive

- `[3]` neutral

- `[4]` negative

- `[5]` very negative

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
