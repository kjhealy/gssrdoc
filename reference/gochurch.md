# Importance of attending church regularly

gochurch

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People have many differing views about what makes a person a
good Christian or Jew. Please tell me how important is each of the
following to you. Once again, please indicate where you would place your
feelings on a scale from 1 to 5. How important is it … A. To attend
regularly religious services at church or synagogue.

## Values

- `[1]` very important

- `[5]` not very important

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
