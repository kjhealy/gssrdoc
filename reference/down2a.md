# Who does r turn to second for help when depressed

down2a

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now suppose you felt just a bit down or depressed, and you
wanted to talk about it. B. And who would you turn to second if you felt
a bit down or depressed and wanted to talk about it?
