# Close relative marry jew

marjew

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What about having a close relative marry a Jewish person? Would
you be very in favor of it happening, somewhat In favor, neither in
favor nor opposed to it happening, somewhat opposed, or very opposed to
it happening? A. A Jewish person?

## Values

- `[1]` strongly favor

- `[3]` neither favor nor oppose

- `[4]` oppose

- `[5]` strongly oppose

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
