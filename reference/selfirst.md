# Take care of yourself first before others

selfirst

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question As I read each of the following statements, please tell me
whether you strongly agree, agree, neither agree or disagree, disagree,
or strongly disagree with it. A. You have to take care of yourself
first, and if you have any energy left over, then help other people.
