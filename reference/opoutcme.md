# Should amer promote eq oppty or eq outcomes?

opoutcme

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people think America should promote equal opportunity for
all, that is, allowing everyone to compete for jobs and wealth on a fair
and even basis. Other people think America should promote equal
outcomes, that is, insuring that everyone has a decent standard of
living and that there are only small differences in wealth and income
betweenthe top and bottom in society. Which do you favor: promoting
equal opportunity or promoting equal outcomes?
