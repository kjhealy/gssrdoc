# Wife should help husbands career first

fehelp

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to read several more statements. As I read each
one, please tell me whether you strongly agree, agree, disagree, or
strongly disagree with it. For example, here is the statement: B. It is
more important for a wife to help her husband's career than to have one
herself.
