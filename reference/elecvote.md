# How honest last natl election: counting of votes

elecvote

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking of the last national election in America, how honest
was it regarding the counting and reporting of the votes?

## Values

- `[1]` very honest

- `[2]` somewhat honest

- `[3]` neither honest nor dishonest

- `[4]` somewhat dishonest

- `[5]` very dishonest

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
