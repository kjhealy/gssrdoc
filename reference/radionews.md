# Reliability of news on podcasts, radio

radionews

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In general, how reliable would you rate the news on political
matters found in the following sources?… Radio and podcasts (including
Internet radio or access via Internet).

## Values

- `[1]` very unreliable

- `[2]` somewhat unreliable

- `[3]` somewhat reliable

- `[4]` very reliable

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
