# Days of poor mental health past 30 days

mntlhlth

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. Now thinking about your mental health, which includes
stress, depression, and problems with emotions, for how many days during
the past 30 days was your mental health not good?
