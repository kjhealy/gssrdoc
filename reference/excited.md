# Felt excited or interested in something?

excited

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to read a list of different feelings that people
sometimes have. After each one, I would like you to tell me on how many
days you have felt this way during the past 7 days. On how many days in
the past 7 days have you... (SPECIFY NUMBER OF DAYS) (IF RESPONDENT
ASKS, SAY THAT FEELING THIS WAY AT ANY TIME DURING A DAY MAKES THAT DAY
"COUNT.") G. Felt excited about or interested in something?
