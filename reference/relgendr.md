# What is your relationship situation

relgendr

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which of the following best describes your current relationship
situation?

## Values

- `[1]` i am in a steady relationship with a woman and we live together

- `[2]` i am in a steady relationship with a man and we live together

- `[3]` i am in a steady relationship with a woman and we do not live
  together

- `[4]` i am in a steady relationship with a man and we do not live
  together

- `[5]` i am not in a steady relationship right now

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
