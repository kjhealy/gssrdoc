# Who makes decision about how to bring up children

deckids

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Who usually makes/made the decisions about how to bring up your
children?

## Values

- `[1]` mostly me

- `[2]` mostly my spouse

- `[3]` sometimes me or sometimes my spouse

- `[4]` we decide together

- `[5]` someone else

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable

  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
