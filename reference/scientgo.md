# Scientists work for good of humanity

scientgo

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question C. Scientific researchers are dedicated people who work for the
good of humanity. Do you strongly agree, agree, disagree, or strongly
disagree?

## Values

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` disagree

- `[4]` strongly disagree
