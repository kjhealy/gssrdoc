# Religious tradition age 16

reltrad16

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question RELTRAD16 is constructed from variables RELIG16, DENOM16 and
OTH16. The missing values coded as ".z: Variable-specific reserve code"
represent OTH16 denominations that are not originally covered by the
Steensland et al. (2000) article (i.e., OTHER values larger than 148).
Please see Appendix F in the codebook for more information.

## Values

- `[1]` evangelical protestant

- `[2]` mainline protestant

- `[3]` black protestant

- `[4]` catholic

- `[5]` jewish

- `[6]` other faith

- `[7]` nonaffiliated

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
