# Should semi-auto gun sale be limited to military

semiguns

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Should semi-automatic, assault weapons or semi-automatic guns
known as assault rifles be sold to the general public or should their
sales be limited to the military and police?

## Values

- `[1]` sold to the general public

- `[2]` sales be limited to the military and police

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
