# Almost everything we do harms envir

harmsgrn

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question And please check one box for each of these statements to show
how much you agree or disagree with it. B. Almost everything we do in
modern life harms the environment.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
