# Does employee ownership matter when r thinks about buying from a company

buyesop

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If you were thinking of buying from a company, would you be
influenced by whether the company was employee-owned? PLEASE READ THE
DEFINITION OF EMPLOYEE-OWNED: Employee-owned is when a company is owned
by all of its employees, with each employee having a share of ownership.

## Values

- `[1]` i would be less likely to buy from an employee-owned company

- `[2]` i would be neither more nor less likely to buy from an
  employee-owned company

- `[3]` i would be more likely to buy from an employee-owned company

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
