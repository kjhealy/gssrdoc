# Felt in touch with someone far away

esp

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How often have you had any of the following experiences? READ
EACH ITEM AND CIRCLE ONE CODE FOR EACH. B. Felt as though you were in
touch with someone when they were far away from you.

## Values

- `[1]` never in my life

- `[2]` once or twice

- `[3]` several times

- `[4]` often

- `[8]` cannot answer

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
