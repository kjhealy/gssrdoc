# Should people be permitted to sell a kidney

sellorgn

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A body organ that is much in need and that people may
contribute are kidneys. Most people can live with only one kidney,
though their chances of survival are better if they have two. Do you
believe that people with two healthy kidneys should be permitted to sell
a kidney to a hospital or organ center to use for transplants?
