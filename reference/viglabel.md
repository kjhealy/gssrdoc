# How likely x is experiencing vignettes

viglabel

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your opinion, how likely is it that NAME is experiencing
(READ A-E) – very likely, somewhat likely, not very likely, or not at
all likely? (IN PART E, READ ONLY THE PHRASE FOLLOWING THE LETTER OF THE
VIGNETTE) E. REFER TO VIGNETTE ID, CHECK BELOW AND READ ONLY 1 PHRASE OF
THE FOLLOWING: Vignette A: alcohol dependence Vignette B: a major
depression Vignette C: schizophrenia Vignette D: a drug problem Vignette
E: GO TO DECTREAT

## Values

- `[1]` very likely

- `[2]` somewhat likely

- `[3]` not very likely

- `[4]` not at all likely

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
