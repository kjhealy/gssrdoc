# Eth minorities never fit in am mainstream

ethnofit

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some opinions some people have expressed in connection
with ethnic issues in the United States. To what extent do you agree or
disagree with each one? B. Ethnic minority groups will never really fit
in with mainstream American culture.
