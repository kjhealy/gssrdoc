# Murderers should get death penalty

deathpen

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some measures to deal with crime. Some people are in
favor of them while other people are against them. Do you agree or
disagree that. . . B. People convicted of murder should be subject to
the death penalty.
