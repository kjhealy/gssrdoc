# Who in household does small repair's

repairs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your household who does the following things . . . B. Makes
small repairs around the house.

## Values

- `[1]` always the woman

- `[2]` usually the woman

- `[3]` about equal or both together

- `[4]` usually the man

- `[5]` always the man

- `[6]` is done by a third person

- `[7]` not married

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
