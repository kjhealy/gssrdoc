# R's self ranking of social position (2021)

rank1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In our society there are groups which tend to be towards the
top and groups which tend to be towards the bottom. On the handcard is a
scale that runs from top to bottom. Where would you put yourself now on
this scale?

## Values

- `[1]` 1 - top

- `[10]` 10 - bottom

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
