# Everyone is like x but x's case is just a bit more extreme and ongoing

morextrm

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Let’s continue talking about (Name) for a minute. Tell me how
much you agree or disagree with the following statement. Basically, we
are all a little like (Name). In (Name)’s case it is just a bit more
extreme and ongoing.
