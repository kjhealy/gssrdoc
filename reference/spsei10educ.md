# Percentage of some college educ in spocc10 based on acs 2010

spsei10educ

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Percentage of those who had some college or more education
among those who had the same occupation as in SPOCC10 in American
Community Survey between 2010 and 2012 (25-64 years old only). This
percentage is not from the GSS but it is used to calculate SPSEI10.
