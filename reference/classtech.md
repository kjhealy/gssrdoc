# Are poor or rich benefiting more from digital tech

classtech

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you think that in America rich people or poor people are
benefitting more, or both equally, or neither, from the use of the
Internet and digital technology?

## Values

- `[1]` rich people are benefitting more than poor people

- `[2]` rich and poor people are benefitting equally

- `[3]` poor people are benefitting more than rich people

- `[4]` neither rich people nor poor people are benefitting

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
