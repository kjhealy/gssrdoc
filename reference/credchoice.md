# R's credit score has to do with choices or things out of your control

credchoice

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Would you say your current credit score has more to do with
choices you’ve made or things out of your control? NOTE: This variable
originates on the GSS Next follow-on, and should not be analyzed without
the GSS Next-specific weights WTSSPS_NEXT or WTSSNRPS_NEXT

## Values

- `[1]` entirely choices you've made

- `[2]` mostly choices you've made

- `[3]` both choices you've made and things out of your control

- `[4]` mostly things out of your control

- `[5]` entirely things out of your control

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
