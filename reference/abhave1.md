# Have abortion if probable defect

abhave1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (WOMEN): Would you yourself have an abortion if… (MEN): Would
you yourself advise the woman you were married to have an abortion if…
A. There is a strong chance of serious defect in the baby?
