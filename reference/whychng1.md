# Religious change experience1

whychng1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Open-ended questions for how people who had undergone spiritual
or religious change described their experiences. A. First Mention: What
led to, caused, or triggered the relgious or spiritual change?
