# How well r speaks the language in betrlang

spklangw

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question C. How well do you speak that language?

## Values

- `[1]` very well

- `[2]` well

- `[3]` not well

- `[4]` poorly/hardly at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
