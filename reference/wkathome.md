# How often do you work from home

wkathome

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (HAND CARD D8) And how often... ...do you work at home during
your usual working hours? Would you say...

## Values

- `[1]` always

- `[2]` often

- `[3]` sometimes

- `[4]` hardly ever

- `[5]` never

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
