# Postsecondary institution attended for credit

voednme1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In what year did you receive the (COLLEGE NAME) degree? F. IF
YES: What institution was that?

## Values

- `[1]` jail/prison

- `[2]` high school

- `[3]` company/business

- `[4]` vocational school/trades/technical (not high school, not
  college)

- `[5]` secretarial school

- `[6]` adult education/school

- `[7]` medical/nursing/health/dental/hospital

- `[8]` union

- `[9]` armed forces

- `[10]` college/university

- `[11]` art/design

- `[12]` business college (not business school at a university)

- `[13]` home study/correspondence

- `[14]` job corp

- `[15]` beauty school/cosmetology

- `[16]` insurance, real estate, finance, executive management

- `[17]` police/fire academy; training

- `[18]` development center

- `[19]` career center

- `[20]` foreign college/university

- `[30]` other

- `[40]` unable to code response, unclear, not identifiable

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
