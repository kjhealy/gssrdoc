## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question There are different opinions as to what it takes to be a good
citizen. As far as you are concerned personally on a scale of 1 to 7,
where 1 is not at all important and 7 is very important, how important
is it… F. To try to understand the reasoning of people with other
opinions
