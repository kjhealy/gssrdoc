# Opinion of how people get ahead

getahead

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people say that people get ahead by their own hard work;
others say that lucky breaks or help from other people are more
important. Which do you think is most important?

## Values

- `[1]` hard work most important

- `[2]` hard work, luck equally important

- `[3]` luck most important

- `[4]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
