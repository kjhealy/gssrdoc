# How oft r & frnds think abt women's rgts

fethink

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. How often would you say that you and your friends think
about women's rights? Would you say that you and your friends think
about women's rights very often, fairly often, occasionally, or almost
never?

## Values

- `[2]` fairly often

- `[3]` occasionally

- `[4]` almost never

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
