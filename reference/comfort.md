# Religion helps people to gain comfort in times of trouble and sorrow

comfort

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you agree or disagree that… C. ...that practicing a religion
helps people to gain comfort in times of trouble and sorrow?

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
