# Ballotformwtnr

ballotformwtnr

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Non-response person weight for years 2002, 2010, 2012, 2016,
and 2018 due to an assignment error of ballot and form for these years.
For more information please see Methodological Report \#134 at
https://gss.norc.org/us/en/gss/get-documentation/methodological-reports.html.
