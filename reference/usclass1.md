# Traditional class divisions still remain

usclass1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are different opinions about social differences in this
country. Please tell me for each one whether you strongly agree,
somewhat, agree, somewhat disagree, or strongly disagree. READ EACH
STATEMENT. CIRCLE ONE CODE FOR EACH. A. In the United States traditional
divisions between owners and workers still remain. A person's social
standing depends upon whether he/she belongs to the upper or lower
class.
