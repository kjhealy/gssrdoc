# How often does r visit father

pavisit1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question And now some questions about your father. How often do you see
or visit your father?

## Values

- `[1]` he lives in the same household as i do

- `[2]` daily

- `[3]` at least several times a week

- `[4]` at least once a week

- `[5]` at least once a month

- `[6]` several times a year

- `[7]` less often

- `[8]` never

- `[9]` my father is no longer alive

- `[10]` i dont know where father lives

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
