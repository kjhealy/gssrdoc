# More trade, less jobs in usa

moretrde

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Suppose two friends of yours are talking about trade with other
countries. One friend thinks that increased trade has created a lot of
jobs in the United States for people with jobs like yours. The other
friend thinks that increased trade has taken away a lot of jobs in the
United States for people with jobs like yours. Which view is closer to
your own?

## Values

- `[1]` created more jobs

- `[2]` about the same

- `[3]` taken them away

- `[4]` not relevant for my job

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
