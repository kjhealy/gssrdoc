# Allow racists book in library y

libracy

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (Or consider a person who believes that Black people are
genetically inferior…)If some people in your community suggested that a
book this person wrote which said Black people are inferior should be
taken out of your public library, would you favor removing this book, or
not?
