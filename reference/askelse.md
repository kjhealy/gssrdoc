# R asked someone else for help

askelse

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question When you want advice or assistance with surfing on the Web,
finding a Web site, downloading software or some other online service,
or getting your Internet connection to work, which of the following do
you do? (Name as many as are correct.) E. Ask someone else you know for
help (like a friend or family member)

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
