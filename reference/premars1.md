# Is premarital sex wrong?

premars1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. Do you think it is wrong or not wrong if a man and a woman
have sexual relations before marriage?

## Values

- `[1]` always wrong

- `[2]` almost always wrong

- `[3]` wrong only sometimes

- `[4]` not wrong at all

- `[5]` other
