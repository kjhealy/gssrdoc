# Allow anti-religious book in library y

spkracy

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Or consider a person who believes that Blacks are genetically
inferior… If such a person wanted to make a speech in your community
claiming that Blacks are inferior, should this person be allowed to
speak, or not?

## Values

- `[1]` yes, allowed to speak

- `[2]` not allowed

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
