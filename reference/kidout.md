# Govt intervene: child stays out late

kidout

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Sometimes public authorities intervene with parents in raising
their children. Please indicate in each of the following cases how far
you think authorities should go in dealing with a 10 year old child and
his or her parents. Public authorities should... C. The parents
regularly let the child stay out late at night without knowing where the
child is.
