# Number of siblings (including step- and adopted)

sibs_7222

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How many brothers and sisters did you have? Please count those
born alive, but no longer living, as well as those alive now. Also
include stepbrothers and stepsisters, and children adopted by your
parents.

As of 2024, SIBS has been recoded to only account for 0 to 6+ siblings.
This has been retroactively applied to all previous years of SIBS. The
original SIBS containing data from 1972 to 2022 has been renamed
SIBS_7222.
