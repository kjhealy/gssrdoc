# Amt of household income coming from this business

owninc

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In which of these groups did your earnings from this business
fall in contributing towards your household income For last year? IF R
HAS MORE THAN ONE BUSINESS/FARM/PARTNERSHIP, ASK ABOUT THE PRIMARY
BUSINESS THEY ARE INVOLVED IN.
