# What sort of jew is r's partner currently

cojew

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Does (s/he) consider (her/himself) Orthodox, Conservative,
Reform or none of these?

## Values

- `[1]` orthodox

- `[2]` conservative

- `[3]` reform

- `[4]` none of these

- `[5]` other
