# Geographic mobility since age 16

mobile16

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In what state or foreign country were you living when you were
16 years old? REFER TO REGION CODES BELOW AND ENTER CODE NUMBERS IN BOX.
A. When you were 16 years old, were you living in this same
(city/town/county)?

## Values

- `[1]` same state, same city

- `[2]` same state, different city

- `[3]` different state

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
