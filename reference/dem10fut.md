# How well will democracy work in america in ten yrs

dem10fut

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question On the whole, on a scale of 0 to 10 where 0 is very poorly and
10 is very well. C. And how about 10 years from now? How well do you
think democracy will work in America then?

## Values

- `[0]` very poorly

- `[10]` very well

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
