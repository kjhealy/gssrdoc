# R's confidence in the existence of god

god

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please look at this card and tell me which statement comes
closest to expressing what you believe about God. I don”t believe in
God.....................1 I don”t know whether there is a God and I
don”t believe there is any way to find
out..................................2 I don”t believe in a personal
God, but I do believe in a Higher Power of some
kind........................3 I find myself believing in God some of the
time, but not at others........4 While I have doubts, I feel that I do
believe in God….............................5 I know God really exists
and I have no doubts about it...........................6

## Values

- `[1]` don't believe

- `[2]` don't know, no way to find out

- `[3]` higher power

- `[4]` believe sometimes

- `[5]` believe with doubts

- `[6]` no doubts

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
