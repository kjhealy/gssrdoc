# Environmental threats exaggerated

grnexagg

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with each of these
statements? C. Many of the claims about environmental threats are
exaggerated.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
