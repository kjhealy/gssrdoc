# Contact with other people

jobpeop

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question On these cards there are various aspects of jobs. How important
Do you personally consider these job characteristics? Using the scale
from one to seven for your answers again, tell me for each card its
letter and the number you've decided on. SHUFFLE THE PINK CARDS BEFORE
HANDING TO RESPONDENT. I. A lot of contact with other people

## Values

- `[1]` unimportant

- `[7]` very important

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
