## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question H. In general, do you think that a rise in the world's
temperature caused by climate change is . . .

## Values

- `[2]` very dangerous

- `[3]` somewhat dangerous

- `[4]` not very dangerous

- `[5]` not dangerous at all
