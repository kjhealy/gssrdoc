# Borrow money \#1

borrow1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Suppose you needed to borrow a large sum of money. A. Who would
you turn to first for help? B. Who would you turn to second? PLEASE
CIRCLE ONLY ONE FIRST CHOICE NUMBER AND ONE SECOND CHOICE NUMBER. A.
First Choice
