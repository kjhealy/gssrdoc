# R's religious identity

religid

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. When it comes to your religious identity, would you say you
are a fundamentalist, evangelical, mainline, or liberal Protestant or do
none of these describe you?

## Values

- `[1]` fundamentalist

- `[2]` evangelical

- `[3]` mainline

- `[4]` liberal

- `[5]` none

- `[6]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
