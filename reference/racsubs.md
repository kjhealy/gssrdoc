# Favor attempts to integrate suburbs

racsubs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some religious and business groups have set up programs to
encourage (Negro/Black) people to buy houses in white suburbs. Do you
favor or oppose these voluntary programs to integrate white suburbs?
