# How much responsibility r had for this person

mhresoth

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Let's turn away from (NAME) now. I have a few questions about
your general experiences that I would like to ask you. Leaving yourself
aside, have you personally ever known someone who has received treatment
for a mental health situation? D. How much responsibility did you have
for making day-to-day decisions for this person? Would you say…

## Values

- `[1]` a great deal

- `[2]` quite a bit

- `[3]` a little

- `[4]` not at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
