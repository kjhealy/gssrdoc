# Proportion of same race as r among all acquaintances

acqmyrac

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Are the people that you are acquainted with…

## Values

- `[1]` almost all the same race as you

- `[2]` mostly the same race as you

- `[3]` about evenly divided

- `[4]` mostly a different race than you

- `[5]` almost all a different race than you

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
