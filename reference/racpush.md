# Blacks shouldn't push

racpush

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some opinions other people have expressed in
connection with (Negro/Black)-white relations. Which statement on the
card comes closest to how you, yourself, feel? The first one is . . . A.
(Negroes/Blacks/African-Americans) shouldn't push themselves where
they're not wanted.
