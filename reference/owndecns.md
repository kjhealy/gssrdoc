# Best if every nation had its own country

owndecns

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking in general about nations and countries all over the
world, to what extent do you agree or disagree with the following
statement?… It would be best if every nation had its own country where
it could make its own decisions.

## Values

- `[1]` agree strongly

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` disagree strongly

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
