# How often does r read traditional newspaper

newsprnt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How often do you read a traditional newspaper (i.e. one that is
printed with ink on paper and NOT counting what you read online) – every
day, a few times a week, once a week, less than once a week, or never?

## Values

- `[1]` every day

- `[2]` a few times a week

- `[3]` once a week

- `[4]` less than once a week

- `[5]` never

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
