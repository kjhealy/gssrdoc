# How often contacted about work when not working

wkcontct

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some workers must respond to coworkers, supervisors, managers,
customers, or clients about work-related matters when they are not
working. These contacts may be by phone, text, instant message, email,
etc. How often are you contacted about work-related matters when you are
not working?

## Values

- `[1]` never

- `[2]` less than once a month

- `[3]` once or twice a month

- `[4]` once a week

- `[5]` several times a week

- `[6]` once a day

- `[7]` two or more times a day

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
