# Govt camera surveillance at work

camwrk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Protecting secrets is a continuing concern of the government. I
am going to ask you about various security measures that the government
might apply to individuals with a SECRET or TOP SECRET clearance. Do you
(strongly agree, agree, neither agree nor disagree, disagree, or
strongly disagree) that people with SECRET or TOP SECRET clearance
should be subject to: H. Video camera surveillance in the workplace

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
