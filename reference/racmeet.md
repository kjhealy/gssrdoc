# Allowed to hold pub meeting for racist

racmeet

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Should people prejudiced against any racial or ethnic group be
allowed to hold public meetings?

- `[2]` should probably be allowed

- `[3]` should probably not be allowed
