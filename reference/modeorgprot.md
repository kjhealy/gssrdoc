# Has r organized a protest in last 12 months

modeorgprot

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some different forms of political and social action
that people can take. Please indicate, for each one, whether you have
done any of these things in the last 12 months offline only, online
only, or both offline and online… Organized or helped to organize a
demonstration or political protest.

## Values

- `[1]` only offline

- `[2]` only online

- `[3]` both offline and online

- `[4]` neither offline nor online

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
