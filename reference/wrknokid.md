# Shld mom work aftr marriage, before kids?

wrknokid

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you think that women should work outside the home full-time,
part-time or not at all under these circumstances: A. After marrying and
before there are children.

## Values

- `[1]` work full-time

- `[2]` work part-time

- `[3]` stay home

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
