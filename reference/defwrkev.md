# Ever work for military supplier?

defwrkev

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you ever worked for a company where a major part of their
business was selling supplies or services to the armed forces?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
