# Employers' right to hire by cancertests

genecany

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. Suppose workers will be exposed to some cancer-causing
materials on the job. In that case, should employers have the right not
to hire workers whose tests show they have an inherited tendency to
develop certain forms of cancer, or should they not have that right?

## Values

- `[1]` should

- `[2]` should not

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
