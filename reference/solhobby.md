# Hobby club tries to solve problems

solhobby

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do (EACH "YES" IN MEMFRAT-MEMNUM) to which you belong do
anything to try to solve individual or community problems? I. Hobby or
garden clubs

- `[2]` no
