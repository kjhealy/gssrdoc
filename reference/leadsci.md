# Shld pay attntn only to theory accepted by leading scientists

leadsci

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question I'm going to read to you some statements like those you might
find in a newspaper or magazine article. For each statement, please tell
me if you strongly agree, agree, disagree, or strongly disagree. F.
Government decision makers should pay attention only to those scientific
theories that have been accepted by most leading scientists.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` disagree

- `[4]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
