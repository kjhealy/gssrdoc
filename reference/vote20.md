# Remember if voted in 2020 election

vote20

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In 2020, you remember that Joe Biden ran for President on the
Democratic ticket against Donald Trump for the Republicans. Do you
remember for sure whether or not you voted in that election?

## Values

- `[1]` voted

- `[2]` did not vote

- `[3]` ineligible

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
