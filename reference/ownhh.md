# Young should not live with parents

ownhh

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People differ in their ideas about what it takes for a young
person to become an adult these days. How important is it For them to
be... No longer living in their parents' household

## Values

- `[1]` extremely important

- `[2]` quite important

- `[3]` somewhat important

- `[4]` not too important

- `[5]` not at all important

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
