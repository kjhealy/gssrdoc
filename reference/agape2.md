# Not happy unless the one s happiness is before r

agape2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you agree strongly, agree somewhat, neither agree nor
disagree, disagree somewhat, or strongly disagree with the following
statements? B. I cannot be happy unless I place the one I love”s
happiness before my own.
