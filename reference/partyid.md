# Political party affiliation

partyid

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Generally speaking, do you usually think of yourself as a
Republican, Democrat, Independent, or what?

## Values

- `[0]` strong democrat

- `[1]` not very strong democrat

- `[2]` independent, close to democrat

- `[3]` independent (neither, no response)

- `[4]` independent, close to republican

- `[5]` not very strong republican

- `[6]` strong republican

- `[7]` other party

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
