# What peo or org have 2nd most inf

gvinflu2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (HAND CARD C4) (Here is a list of people and organizations that
can influence government actions.) And which do you think has the second
most influence on the actions of the American government?
