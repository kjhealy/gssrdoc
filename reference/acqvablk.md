# Number of black in voluntary association

acqvablk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Next, we are going to ask about people that you are acquainted
with through schools, clubs, associations, or places of worship. G. How
many are Black or African-American?

## Values

- `[1]` 0

- `[2]` 1

- `[3]` 2 - 5

- `[4]` 6 - 10

- `[5]` more than 10

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
