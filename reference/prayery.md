# Bible prayer in schools-version y

prayery

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. What are your views on the reading of the Lord's Prayer or
Bible verses in public schools? Do you think it should be required in
all public schools, not allowed in any public schools, or that it should
be up to each state or local community to decide?
