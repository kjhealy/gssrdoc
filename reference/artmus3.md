# Visit museum after learning on web

artmus3

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question F. Visit an art museum or gallery as a result of what you found
on its Web site?

## Values

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
