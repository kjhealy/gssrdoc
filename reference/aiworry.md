# R worried that ai will take over many jobs done by humans

aiworry

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Overall, how worried, if at all, are you that in the next 10
years machines, computer programs, and Artificial Intelligence (AI) will
take over many of the jobs done by humans?

## Values

- `[1]` very worried

- `[3]` neither worried nor unworried

- `[4]` not very worried

- `[5]` not at all worried
