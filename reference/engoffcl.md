# Favor law making english the official language

engoffcl

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you favor a law making English the official language of the
United States, meaning government business would be conducted in English
only, or do you oppose such a law?

## Values

- `[1]` favor

- `[3]` neither favor nor oppose

- `[5]` oppose

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
