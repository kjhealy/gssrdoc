# Imp of church teachings to make decision

decchurh

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Can you tell me how important is each of the following in
helping you to make decisions about your life. Please indicate your
answer by stating where you would place your feelings on a scale from 1
to 5 as shown on this hand Card. C. The teachings of your church or
synagogue

## Values

- `[1]` very important

- `[5]` not very important

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
