# Wrong for woman to get abortion if low income?

abpoorw

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you personally think it is wrong or not wrong for a woman to
have an abortion. . . B. If the family has a very low income and cannot
afford any more children.

## Values

- `[1]` always wrong

- `[2]` almost always wrong

- `[3]` wrong only sometimes

- `[4]` not wrong at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
