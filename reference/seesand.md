# Which picture shows what she should use for the 2nd pot

seesand

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please look at Card I-4. A gardener has an idea that a plant
needs sand in the soil for healthy growth. In order to test her idea she
uses two pots of plants. She sets up one pot of plants as shown on the
top part of the card. Which one of the pictures on the bottom part of
the card shows what should she use for the second pot?
