# Could r find a job (employed r's)

findwork

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If you lost your job for any reason, and were looking actively
for another one, how easy or difficult do you think it would be for you
to find an acceptable job?

## Values

- `[1]` very easy

- `[2]` fairly easy

- `[3]` neither easy nor difficult

- `[4]` fairly difficult

- `[5]` very difficult

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
