# People elected to congress try to keep promises

poleff16

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with each of the following
statements? PLEASE CHECK ONE BOX ON EACH LINE. F. People we elect to
Congress try to keep the promises they have made during the election

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
