# Favor busing 10 yrs ago

busing10

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. Now, thinking about ten years ago, that is in 1972, did you
then favor or oppose the busing of (Negro/Black) and white school
children from one school district to another?

## Values

- `[1]` favor

- `[2]` oppose

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
