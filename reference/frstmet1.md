# R first met the named alter in the group

frstmet1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Did you first meet (NAME) in one of the groups in the previous
question, or did you first meet somewhere else? A. Did you meet the
first name in one of these groups or did you meet somewhere else?

## Values

- `[1]` yes, one of these groups

- `[2]` no, somewhere else

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
