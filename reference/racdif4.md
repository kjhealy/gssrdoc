# Differences due to lack of will

racdif4

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question On the average (Negroes/Blacks/African-Americans) have worse
jobs, income, and housing than white people.Do you think these
differences are . . . D. Because most (Negroes/Blacks/African-Americans)
just don't have the motivation or will power to pull themselves up out
of poverty?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
