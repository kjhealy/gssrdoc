# Opinion on teaching classes only in english

engteach

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question There are several different ideas about how to teach children
who don't speak English when they enter our public schools. Please look
at the card and tell me which statement best describes how you feel: All
classes should be conducted only in English so that children have to
learn English right from the start…………………………………………………….…………………1 Children
who don't know English should have classes in their native language just
for a year or two until they learn English………………...……….….....…………… 2
Students who want to keep up with their native languages and cultures
should be able to take many of the classes in Spanish or other languages
All the way through high school……......3

## Values

- `[1]` all classes in english

- `[2]` 1-2 yrs in native lang

- `[3]` native lang thru hs
