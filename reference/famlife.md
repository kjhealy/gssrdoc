# How successful in family life

famlife

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question The next few questions are about your success in different
areas of life. First, how successful do you feel in your family life? Do
you feel...

## Values

- `[1]` not at all successful

- `[2]` not very successful

- `[3]` somewhat successful

- `[4]` very successful

- `[5]` completely successful

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
