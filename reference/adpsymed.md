# Would r have child on med by psychiatrists ad

adpsymed

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If (John/Mary) was your child, would you... Go to a
psychiatrist for help? A. If the psychiatrist said your child should be
on medication, how likely would you be to do that?

## Values

- `[1]` very likely

- `[2]` somewhat likely

- `[3]` not very likely

- `[4]` not at all likely

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
