# Was r's starting pay higher/lower/same as earning in previous job

pastpay

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question When you went to work for your current employer, was your
starting pay higher, lower or about the same as what you were earning in
your previous (full time) job?

## Values

- `[1]` higher

- `[2]` about the same

- `[3]` lower

- `[4]` current employer is first job

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
