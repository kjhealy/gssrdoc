# Need educated parents to get ahead

oppared

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please show for each of these how important you think it is for
getting ahead in life . . . B. Having well educated parents?

## Values

- `[1]` essential

- `[2]` very important

- `[3]` fairly important

- `[4]` not very important

- `[5]` not important at all

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
