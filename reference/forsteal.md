# Foreigner's stealing u.s. tech more threat 10 y

forsteal

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Are the following threats to the United States greater, about
the same, or less today than they were 10 years ago? E. Stealing U.S.
advanced technology and trade secrets by foreigners
