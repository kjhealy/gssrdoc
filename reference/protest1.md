# Allow public meetings protesting govt

protest1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question There are many ways people or organizations can protest against
a government action they strongly oppose. Please show which you think
should be allowed and which should not be allowed by circling a number
after each question. A. Organizing public meetings to protest against
the government.

## Values

- `[1]` definitely allowed

- `[2]` probably allowed

- `[3]` probably not allowed

- `[4]` definitely not allowed

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
