# Spending right amount on arts

natarts

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Earlier we talked about problems in this country, and whether
we are spending too much money on them, too little money, or about the
right amount. I have one more problem to ask you about: the arts . . .
are we spending too much, too little, or about the right amount on that?

## Values

- `[1]` too little

- `[2]` about right

- `[3]` too much

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
