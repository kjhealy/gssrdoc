# Would you rather sell bedroom furniture

sellbed

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question I'd like you to imagine that you want to sell each of the
following items. Would you rather sell these items to someone with whom
you have had some family or social relationship, or to a buyer with whom
you had no prior personal contact? Please tell me the number that best
represents your feeling with respect to each of the sales listed. A.
Bedroom furniture.

## Values

- `[1]` strong preference no contact

- `[3]` dont care

- `[5]` strong preference contact

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
