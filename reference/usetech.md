# Percentage of time use tech

usetech

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question During a typical week, about what percentage of your total time
at work would you normally spend using different types of electronic
technologies (such as computers, tablets, smart phones, cash registers,
scanners, GPS devices, robotic devices, and so on)?
