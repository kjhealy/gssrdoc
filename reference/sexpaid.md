# Ever had sex with a person you paid or who paid you for sex

sexpaid

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you ever had sex with a person you paid or who paid you
for sex? As of 2024, SEXPAID has been slightly adjusted to help protect
confidentiality. Please see the 2024 codebook for more information.
