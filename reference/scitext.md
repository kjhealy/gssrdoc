# What it means to r to study scientifically

scitext

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your own words, could you tell me what it means to study
something scientifically?

## Values

- `[1]` formulation of theories, test hypotheses

- `[2]` do experiments, control group

- `[3]` rigorous, systematic comparison

- `[4]` measurement

- `[5]` classification

- `[6]` redundancies/incorrect

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
