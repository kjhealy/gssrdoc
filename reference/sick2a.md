# Who does r turn to second for help when sick

sick2a

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now we would like to ask you how you would get help in
situations that anyone could find herself or himself in. First, suppose
you had the 'flu and had to stay in bed for a few days and needed help
around the house, with shopping and so on. B. And who would you turn to
second if you had the flu and needed help around the house?
