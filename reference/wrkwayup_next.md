# Black people overcome prejudice without favors - follow-on version

wrkwayup_next

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you agree strongly, agree somewhat, neither agree nor
disagree, disagree somewhat, or disagree strongly with the following
statements? Irish, Italian, Jewish and many other minorities overcame
prejudice and worked their way up. Black people should do

NOTE: This variable originates on the GSS Next follow-on, and should not
be analyzed without the GSS Next-specific weights WTSSPS_NEXT or
WTSSNRPS_NEXT

## Values

- `[1]` agree strongly

- `[2]` agree somewhat

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree somewhat

- `[5]` disagree strongly

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
