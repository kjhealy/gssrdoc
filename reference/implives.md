# Way of life more important than being catholic

implives

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please tell me whether you strongly agree, agree, disagree, or
strongly disagree with the following statements: B. How a person lives
is more important than whether or not he or she is Catholic.
