# R has stock in r's company

ownstock

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you own any shares of stock in the company where you now
work, either directly or through some type of retirement or stock plan?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no, i work for a company that does not have stock

- `[3]` no, my company has stock but i do not own any

- `[4]` no, i do not own and do not know if my company has stock

- `[5]` i do not know if i own stock in my company

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
