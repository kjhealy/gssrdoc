# America benefits from being a member of nafta?

nafta2a

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. How strongly do you agree or disagree with the following
statement? (Please, select one response) Generally speaking, would you
say that America benefits or does not benefit from being a member of
NAFTA?

## Values

- `[1]` greatly benefits

- `[2]` largely benefits

- `[3]` somewhat benefits

- `[4]` benefits only a little

- `[5]` does not benefit at all

- `[7]` have never heard of nafta

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
