# How likely to mention problems to relatives

transrel

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now imagine that one of your relatives sees the car and wants
to buy it. How likely would you be to mention the car's history of
transmission problems (if this person did not ask)?

## Values

- `[1]` definitely would not mention it

- `[2]` probably would not

- `[3]` probably would

- `[4]` definitely would mention it

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
