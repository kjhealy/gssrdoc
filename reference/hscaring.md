# School subjects: concern for others

hscaring

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some things that might be taught in school. How
important is it that schools teach each of these to 15-year-olds? H.
Concern for minorities and the poor.

## Values

- `[1]` essential

- `[2]` very important

- `[3]` fairly important

- `[4]` not very important

- `[5]` not important at all
