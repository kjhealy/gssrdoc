# R accepts a person from a different religion or view from r's marrying relative

relmarry

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People belong to different religions and have different
religious views. A. Would you accept a person from a different religion
or with a very different religious view from yours marrying a relative
of yours? Would you say…

## Values

- `[1]` definitely accept

- `[2]` probably accept

- `[3]` probably not accept

- `[4]` definitely not accept

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
