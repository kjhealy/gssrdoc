# How likely to get best treatment avail in us

medbest

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How likely is it that if you become seriously ill, you would
get or not get the best treatment available in the United States?

## Values

- `[1]` it's certain r would get

- `[2]` it's likely r would get

- `[3]` equal chance of getting or not getting

- `[4]` it's likely r would not get

- `[5]` it's certain r would not get

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
