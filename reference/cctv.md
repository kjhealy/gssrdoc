# Civil liberties - video surveillance

cctv

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I have some questions about civil liberties and public
security. Do you think that the American government should or should not
have the right to do the following? Keep people under video surveillance
in public areas?  Would you say...

## Values

- `[1]` definitely should have the right

- `[2]` probably should have the right

- `[3]` probably should not have the right

- `[4]` definitely should not have the right

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
