# Best way for blacks to improve position

blkgains

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People have different ideas about the best way for blacks to
improve their position in American life and politics. Some people think
that the best way for blacks to improve their position is through civil
rights groups, they would be at point 1 on this card. Other people think
that the best for way blacks to improve their position is for each
individual black to become better trained and more qualified. They would
be located at point 7. And other people have opinions somewhere in
between. Where would you place yourself on this scale?
