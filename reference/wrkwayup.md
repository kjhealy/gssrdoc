# Black people overcome prejudice without favors

wrkwayup

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. Do you agree strongly, agree somewhat, neither agree nor
disagree, disagree somewhat, or disagree strongly with the following
statement (HAND CARD TO RESPONDENT): Irish, Italians, Jewish and many
other minorities overcame prejudice and worked their way up. Black
people should do the same without special favors.

## Values

- `[1]` agree strongly

- `[2]` agree somewhat

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree somewhat

- `[5]` disagree strongly

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
