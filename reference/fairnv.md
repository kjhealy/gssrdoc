# People fair or try to take advantage (no volunteered response on web)

fairnv

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you think most people would try to take advantage of you if
they got a chance, or would they try to be fair?

## Values

- `[1]` would take advantage of you

- `[2]` would try to be fair

- `[3]` depends (phone mode only: volunteered)

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
