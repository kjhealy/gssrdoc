# Number of employees: r's entire firm

totalnum

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question About how many people are employed in the entire business,
corporation or organization for which you work? (Count part-time as well
as full-time employees. (If you work for the government, how many people
are employed in your whole division or agency?)

## Values

- `[1]` 1-9

- `[2]` 10-49

- `[3]` 50-99

- `[4]` 100-499

- `[5]` 500-999

- `[6]` 1,000-1,999

- `[7]` 2,000 - 9,999

- `[8]` 10,000+

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
