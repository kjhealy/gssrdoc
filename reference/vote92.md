# Did r vote in 1992 election

vote92

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question G. In 1992, you remember that Clinton ran for President on the
Democratic ticket against Bush for the Republicans and Perot as an
Independent. Do you remember for sure whether or not you voted in that
election?

## Values

- `[1]` voted

- `[2]` did not vote

- `[3]` not eligible

- `[4]` refused

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
