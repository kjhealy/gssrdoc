# Relationship with this person as a result of mental health problem

mhreloth

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Let's turn away from (NAME) now. I have a few questions about
your general experiences that I would like to ask you. Leaving yourself
aside, have you personally ever known someone who has received treatment
for a mental health situation? F. As a result of the mental health
problem, would you say that your relationship with this person…
