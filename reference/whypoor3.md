# Not enough jobs

whypoor3

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I will a list of reasons some people give to explain why
there are poor people in this country. Please tell me whether you feel
each of these is very important, somewhat important, or not important in
explaining why there are poor people in this country. C. Failure of
industry to provide enough jobs

## Values

- `[1]` very important

- `[2]` somewhat important

- `[3]` not important

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
