# How many religious preferences mentioned

switnum

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question I'd like to go over your religious preferences since you were
raised as a (RELIGION AND/OR DENOMINATION MENTIONED IN RELIG16/DENOM16).
Have you ever had another religious preference besides being (RELIGION
AND/OR CHURCH DENOMINATION MENTIONED IN RELIG16/DENOM16).
Yes................(ASK A) No..............(GO TO MARELIG) B. RECORD
NUMBER OF RELIGIONS MENTIONED

## Values

- `[4]` four or more

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job

  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
