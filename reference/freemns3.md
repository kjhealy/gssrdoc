# What does freedom mean to you

freemns3

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question The next questions are about freedom in American today. Freedom
means many different things to people. When you think about freedom,
what comes to mind? Can you tell me in a couple of sentences what does
freedom mean to you? C. Third distinct subject mentioned
