## Source

## Details

Question What other language(s) do you speak? First response. As of
2024, OTHLANG1 has been recoded to only account for seven language
categories. This has been retroactively applied to all previous years of
OTHLANG1. The original OTHLANG1 containing data from 2000 to 2022 has
been renamed OTHLANG1_0022.
