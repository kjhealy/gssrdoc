# Natural environments should be left alone

natrecon

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with the following
statements: C. Natural environments that support scarce or endangered
species should be left alone, no matter how great the economic benefits
to your community from developing them commercially might be.

## Values

- `[1]` agree strongly

- `[2]` agree somewhat

- `[3]` disagree somewhat

- `[4]` disagree strongly

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
