# R seek treat working less carefully

treat6

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Next, I'm going to read you some descriptions of health
problems people sometimes experience. Some people might go to a doctor,
others might not. F. Not working or doing other activities as carefully
as usual as a result of any emotional problems such as feeling depressed
or anxious?

## Values

- `[1]` definitely go

- `[2]` probably go

- `[3]` probably not go

- `[4]` definitely not go

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
