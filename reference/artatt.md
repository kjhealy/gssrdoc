# Attended exhibit alone or with others

artatt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question You said that in the last 12 months you attended an exhibit.
I'd like to ask you a few questions about the last time you did this
type of activity. Did you attend this exhibit alone, with one other
person, or with two or more other people?

## Values

- `[1]` alone

- `[2]` with one other person

- `[3]` with two or more other people

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
