# Who would r have voted for-1976 election

if76who

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question C. In 1976, you remember that Carter ran for President on the
Democratic ticket against Ford for the Republicans. Do you remember for
sure whether or not you voted in that election? 2. IF DID NOT VOTE OR
INELIGIBLE: Who would you have voted for, for President, if you had
voted?

## Values

- `[1]` carter

- `[2]` ford

- `[3]` other

- `[4]` refused

- `[5]` wouldnt vote

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
