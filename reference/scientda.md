# Happy if daughter scientist

scientda

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. If you had a daughter, how would you feel if she wanted to
be a scientist"would you feel happy, unhappy, or would you not care one
way or the other?

- `[3]` unhappy
