# Did spouse live at home at age 16?

spfam16

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Was your (husband/wife) living with both (his/her) own mother
and father around the time (s/he) was 16? (IF NO: with whom was (he/she)
living around that time?) IF HUSBAND/WIFE MARRIED OR LEFT HOME BY AGE
16, PROBE FOR BEFORE THAT.

## Values

- `[0]` other

- `[1]` both own mother and father

- `[2]` father and stepmother

- `[3]` mother and stepfather

- `[4]` father-no mother or stepmother

- `[5]` mother-no father or stepfather

- `[6]` some other male relative -no female head-

- `[7]` some other female relative -no male head-

- `[8]` other, with both male and female relatives

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
