# Intl orgs take away much power from american govt

powrorgs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with the following
statements? (Please, select one response) D. International organizations
are taking away too much power from the American government.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
