artists

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question I'm going to read several statements. After I read each one,
please tell me whether you strongly agree, agree, disagree, or strongly
disagree with it. For example . . . B. Artists and performers who are
truly excellent will be able to support themselves by selling artworks
or tickets to performances.
