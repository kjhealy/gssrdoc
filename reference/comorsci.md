# Antarctica should be reserved or open

comorsci

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people think that Antarctica should be reserved primarily
for scientific purposes. Others think it should be open for tourism,
fishing, exploration for oil, and other commercial purposes. Please look
at Card B23. Which statement best describes your view on this issue?

## Values

- `[1]` strongly support reserving antarctica

- `[2]` somewhat support reserving antarctica

- `[3]` dont lean one way or the other on this issue

- `[4]` somewhat support opening antarctica

- `[5]` strongly support opening antarctica
