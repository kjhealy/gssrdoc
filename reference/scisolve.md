# Science will solve social problems

scisolve

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some things that have been said about science. Would
you tell me if you tend to agree or disagree with them? A. Science will
solve our social problems like crime and mental illness.

- `[1]` agree
