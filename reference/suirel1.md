# R's relationship to closest suicide

suirel1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking about the person you knew best that died by suicide,
please tell me the letter of the category on the card which most clearly
describes your relationship to that person?                         
(HAND CARD I1)
