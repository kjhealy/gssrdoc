# How often your family interfered with your job

jobvshme

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In the last few years, how often would you say that your family
or household responsibilities have interfered with your ability to
devote full attention to your job? Does this happen frequently,
sometimes, seldom or never?

## Values

- `[1]` frequently

- `[2]` sometimes

- `[3]` seldom

- `[4]` never

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
