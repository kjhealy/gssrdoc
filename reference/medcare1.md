# Who r thinks should be most responsible for cost of xs medical care

medcare1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your opinion, who should be most responsible for paying the
cost of NAME's medical care, including mental health care and treatment?

## Values

- `[1]` name, him-herself

- `[2]` his-her family

- `[3]` government

- `[4]` insurance

- `[5]` private charity

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
