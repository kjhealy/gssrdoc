# Jobs created are better than r's current job

newjobs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question On average, do you think the jobs that have been created in the
United States for people with jobs like yours are better than your job
(with higher pay and better benefits) or worse?

## Values

- `[1]` better

- `[2]` about the same

- `[3]` worse

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
