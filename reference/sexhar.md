# R experienced sexual harassment

sexhar

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Sometimes at work people find themselves the object of sexual
advances, propositions, or unwanted sexual discussions from co-workers
or supervisors. The advances sometimes involve physical contact and
sometimes just involve sexual conversations. Has this ever happened to
you?
