# Fem opportunities better in military?

femilop

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. And what about women? Would you say that their treatment and
opportunities are better in the military, better in civilian employment,
or that there isn”t any difference these days?

## Values

- `[1]` military better

- `[2]` civilian better

- `[3]` no difference

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
