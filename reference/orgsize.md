# How many people employed in the entire org

orgsize

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question D. About how many people are employed in the entire business,
corporation, or organization for which you work? (Count part-time as
well as full-time employees.)

## Values

- `[1]` 1 - 9

- `[2]` 10 - 49

- `[3]` 50-99

- `[4]` 100-499

- `[5]` 500-999

- `[6]` 1 000-1 999

- `[7]` 2 000-9 999

- `[8]` 10 000 or more

- `[98]` not sure

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
