# Willing to sacrifice wish to let the one achieve

agape3

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you agree strongly, agree somewhat, neither agree nor
disagree, disagree somewhat, or strongly disagree with the following
statements? C. I am usually willing to sacrifice my own wishes to let
the one I love achieve his/hers.
