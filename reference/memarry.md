# Way of life recommendation for man

memarry

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If you were advising a young man, which of the following ways
of life would you recommend?

## Values

- `[1]` live alone

- `[2]` live with posslq

- `[3]` cohabitate, then marry

- `[4]` marry

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
