# Confidence in pharmaceutical companies y

conpharvacy

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some institutions in this country. As far as the
people running these institutions are concerned, would you say you have
a great deal of confidence, only some confidence, or hardly any
confidence at all in them?

Pharmaceutical companies working to create safe vaccines to protect the
public against COVID-19

## Values

- `[1]` a great deal

- `[2]` only some

- `[3]` hardly any

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job

  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
