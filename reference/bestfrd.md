# Number of other close friends

bestfrd

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now think about your best friend, the friend you feel closest
to (but not your partner). Is this best friend ...

## Values

- `[1]` a male relative

- `[2]` a female relative

- `[3]` a man who is not a relative

- `[4]` a woman who is not a relative

- `[5]` i dont have a close friend

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
