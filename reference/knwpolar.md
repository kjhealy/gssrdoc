# Level of r's information: the north and south poles

knwpolar

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question We want to ask about how much information you have on various
topics. Please look at Card B21. For each of the following areas, please
indicate whether you are very informed, somewhat informed, neither
informed nor uninformed, somewhat uniformed, or very uninformed about
the issues. E. The North and South poles

## Values

- `[1]` very informed

- `[2]` somewhat informed

- `[3]` neither informed nor uninformed

- `[4]` somewhat uninformed

- `[5]` very uninformed

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
