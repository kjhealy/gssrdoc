# Should penalty for illegal gun sale be tougher than drug

gunsdrug

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Should penalties for illegally selling guns be tougher, less
tough, or about as tough as penalties for illegally selling drugs?

## Values

- `[1]` tougher

- `[2]` less tough

- `[3]` about as tough
