# Problem effect family most

enprbfam

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question E. Which problem, if any, affects you and your family the most?

## Values

- `[1]` air pollution

- `[2]` chemicals and pesticides

- `[3]` water shortage

- `[4]` water pollution

- `[5]` nuclear waste

- `[6]` domestic waste disposal

- `[7]` climate change

- `[8]` genetically modified foods

- `[9]` using up our natural resources

- `[10]` none of these

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
