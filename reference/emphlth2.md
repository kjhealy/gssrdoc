# Employers coverage

emphlth2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which of the following statements best describes your
employer's coverage:

## Values

- `[1]` only individual employees are covered

- `[2]` employees and their spouses are covered but domestic partners
  are not covered

- `[3]` employees and their spouses are covered and domestic partners
  are covered

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
