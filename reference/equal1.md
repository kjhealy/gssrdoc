# Everyone should look out for self

equal1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question SHUFFLE THE GREY CARDS. READ STATEMENTS IN ORDER OF CARDS TO
RESPONDENT. CIRCLE APPROPRIATE CODE FOR EACH STATEMENT. A. In our
society everyone must look out for himself. It is of little use to unite
with others and fight for one's goals in politics or in unions.
