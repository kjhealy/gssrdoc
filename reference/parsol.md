# R's living standard compared to parents

parsol

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. Compared to your parents when they were the age you are now,
Do you think your own standard of living now is much better, somewhat
better, about the same, somewhat worse, or much worse than theirs was?

## Values

- `[1]` much better

- `[2]` somewhat better

- `[3]` about the same

- `[4]` somewhat worse

- `[5]` much worse

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
