# How many personal messages received per day

emgetp

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking now about all your home, work or other computers, D.
About how many of these messages are personal one-to-one, rather than
ones you receive as a member of a group or e-list?

## Values

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
