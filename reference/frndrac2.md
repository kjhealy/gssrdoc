# What is friend 2s race

frndrac2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Is (NAME) Asian, Black, Hispanic, White or something else? 2.
Race of Second Friend

## Values

- `[1]` asian

- `[2]` black

- `[3]` hispanic

- `[4]` white

- `[5]` something else

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
