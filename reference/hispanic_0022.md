# R hispanic specific

hispanic_0022

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question IF R IS FEMALE, READ LATINA; IF MALE, READ LATINO. Are you
Spanish, Hispanic, or Latino/Latina? Yes . . . . . (ASK A) No . . . . .
. (GO TO NEXT QUESTION) IF YES: Which group are you from? Mexican,
Mexican American, Chicano/Chicana Puerto Rican Cuban Other As of 2024,
HISPANIC has been recoded to remove certain sensitive categories. This
has been retroactively applied to all previous years of HISPANIC. The
original HISPANIC containing data from 2000 to 2022 has been renamed
HISPANIC_0022.
