# R primary identity as ethnic or american

ethid1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question C. Well, if you had to choose, which would you think of
yourself most of the time–mainly as a member of a particular ethnic,
racial, or nationality group, or mainly as just an American?

## Values

- `[1]` just an american

- `[2]` some part. ethnic grp

- `[3]` 2 or more ethnic grps

- `[4]` it depends

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
