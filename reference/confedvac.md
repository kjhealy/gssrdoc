## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some institutions in this country. As far as the
people running these institutions are concerned, would you say you have
a great deal of confidence, only some confidence, or hardly any
confidence at all in them?

The federal government ensuring the safety of vaccines to protect the
public against serious diseases
