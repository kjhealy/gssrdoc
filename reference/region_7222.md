# Region of interview

region_7222

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question REGION OF INTERVIEW As of 2024, REGION has been recoded to only
account for four categories. This has been retroactively applied to all
previous years of REGION. The original REGION containing data from 1972
to 2022 has been renamed REGION_7222.
