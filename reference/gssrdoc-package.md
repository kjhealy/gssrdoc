# gssrdoc: Document General Social Survey Variables

The General Social Survey (GSS) is a long-running, mostly annual survey
of US households. It is administered by the National Opinion Research
Center (NORC). This package contains the a tibble with information on
the survey variables, together with every variable documented as an R
help page. For more information on the GSS see <http://gss.norc.org>.
