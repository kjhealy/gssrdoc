# Was r's work part-time or full-time?

partfull

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'd like to ask you about last year. In (the previous year)
how many weeks did you work either full-time or part-time not counting
work around the house–include paid vacations and sick leave? A. When you
worked in (the previous year), was it usually full or part time?

## Values

- `[1]` full-time

- `[2]` part-time

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
