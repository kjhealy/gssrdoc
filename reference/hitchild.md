# Stranger had hit mans child

hitchild

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Are there any situations that you can imagine in which you
would approve of a man punching an adult male stranger? C. Would you
approve of a man punching a stranger who had hit the man”s child after
the child accidentally damaged the stranger”s car?
