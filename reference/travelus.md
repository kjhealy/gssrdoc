# Transportation r took when travelling to us

travelus

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking about the most recent time you traveled to the United
States from another country"please tell me which box shows the kind of
transportation you took: A, B, or C.

## Values

- `[1]` box a

- `[2]` box b

- `[3]` box c

- `[4]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
