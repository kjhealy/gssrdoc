# Web make r attend a political meeting

meeting

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you ever taken any of the following actions because of
something you learned about or discussed on the Web? C. Attended a
political meeting or demonstration?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job

  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
