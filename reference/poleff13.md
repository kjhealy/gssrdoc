# Have a pretty good understanding of issues

poleff13

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with each of the following
statements? PLEASE CHECK ONE BOX ON EACH LINE. C. I feel that I have a
pretty good understanding of the important political issues facing our
country

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
