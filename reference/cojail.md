# Jail draft resister's?

cojail

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Even though they are no longer drafted for military service,
young men are still required by law to register for the draft when they
become 18 years old. If a young man refuses to register for the draft,
do you think he should be punished in any way? A. If a young man refuses
to register for the draft, would you approve or disapprove of sending
him to jail?

## Values

- `[2]` disapprove
