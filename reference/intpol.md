# How interested in politics is r

intpol

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How interested are you in politics and national affairs? Are
you very interested, somewhat interested, only slightly interested, or
not at all interested?

## Values

- `[1]` very interested

- `[2]` somewhat int

- `[3]` slightly int

- `[4]` not interested

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
