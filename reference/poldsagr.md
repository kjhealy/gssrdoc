# Visit political sites r disagrees with

poldsagr

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Is it VERY TRUE for you, SOMEWHAT TRUE, or NOT TRUE that you:
C. Visit sites that challenge your own opinions and provide different
perspectives on political affairs?

## Values

- `[1]` very true

- `[2]` somewhat true

- `[3]` not true

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
