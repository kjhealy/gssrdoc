# Total family income

income

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In which of these groups did your total family income, from all
sources, fall last year before taxes, that is? Just tell me the letter.

## Values

- `[1]` under \$1,000

- `[2]` \$1,000 to \$2,999

- `[3]` \$3,000 to \$3,999

- `[4]` \$4,000 to \$4,999

- `[5]` \$5,000 to \$5,999

- `[6]` \$6,000 to \$6,999

- `[7]` \$7,000 to \$7,999

- `[8]` \$8,000 to \$9,999

- `[9]` \$10,000 to \$14,999

- `[10]` \$15,000 to \$19,999

- `[11]` \$20,000 to \$24,999

- `[12]` \$25,000 or more

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
