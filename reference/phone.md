# Does r have telephone

phone

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thank you very much for your time and help. Do you have a
telephone? May I have your name and (one of your) telephone number(s)
just in case I have left something out in this interview? ENTER NAME AND
PHONE NUMBER. IF NO PHONE OR REFUSED, CIRCLE APPROPRIATE CODE NUMBER.

## Values

- `[1]` no phone number

- `[2]` refused phone number

- `[3]` home phone

- `[4]` phone elsewhere

- `[5]` phone location not given

- `[6]` cell phone

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
