# Universe began with a big explosion: true or false

bigbang1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question According to astronomers, the universe began with a huge
explosion. (Is that true or false?)

## Values

- `[1]` true

- `[2]` false

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
