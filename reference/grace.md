# Had powerful religious experience

grace

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How often have you had any of the following experiences? READ
EACH ITEM AND CIRCLE ONE CODE FOR EACH. E. Felt as though you were very
close to a powerful, spiritual force that seemed to lift you out of
yourself.
