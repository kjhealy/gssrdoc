# Mostly men or women at the workplace

genderwk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Are the people who work at this location mostly men or women?
Please just tell me the number on the card?

## Values

- `[1]` all women

- `[2]` almost all women

- `[3]` mostly women

- `[4]` half men/women

- `[5]` mostly men

- `[6]` almost all men

- `[7]` all men

- `[8]` not sure

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
