# Are women or men benefiting more from digital tech

gendtech

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In all countries, there are differences between social groups.
Do you think that in America women or men are benefitting more, or both
equally, or neither from the use of the Internet and digital technology?

## Values

- `[1]` women are benefitting more than men

- `[2]` women and men are benefitting equally

- `[3]` men are benefitting more than women

- `[4]` neither men nor women are benefitting

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
