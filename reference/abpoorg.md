# Low income–cant afford more children (grid on web)

abpoorg

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (Pleae indicate whether or not you think it should be possible
for a pregnant woman to obtain a legal abortion . . .) if the family has
a very low income and cannot afford any more children?
