# Household members 6 thru 12 yrs old

preteen_7222

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Household Size and Composition (see Appendix D: Recodes, for
information about these variables). B. NUMBER OF MEMBERS 6 TO 12 YEARS
OLD: As of 2024, PRETEEN has been recoded to only account for 0 to 2+
children ages 6 to 12 in the household. This has been retroactively
applied to all previous years of PRETEEN. The original PRETEEN
containing data from 1972 to 2022 has been renamed PRETEEN_7222.

## Values

- `[0]` 0 household members between 6 to 12

- `[8]` 8 or more

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
