# Relations between management and workers

unmanrel

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In general how would you describe relations in your work place
between management and employees? Would you say they are very good,
quite good, neither good nor bad, quite bad, or very bad?

## Values

- `[1]` very

- `[2]` quite good

- `[3]` neither good nor bad

- `[4]` quite bad

- `[5]` very bad

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
