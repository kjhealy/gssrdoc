# Ever drink too much?

drunk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you ever have occasion to use any alcoholic beverages such
as liquor, wine, or beer, or are you a total abstainer? A. Do you
sometimes drink more than you think you should?
