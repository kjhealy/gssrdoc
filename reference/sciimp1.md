# How important: done by scientists in univer'sity settings

sciimp1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to read you some statements about science and
scientists. Please look at Card B19. How important are each of the
following in making something scientific? A. It is done by scientists
employed in a university setting. Would you say this is very important,
pretty important, not too important, or not important at all in making
something scientific?

## Values

- `[1]` very important

- `[2]` pretty important

- `[3]` not too important

- `[4]` not important at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
