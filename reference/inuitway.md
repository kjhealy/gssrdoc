# Effect of global warming: inuit no longer follow traditional way of life

inuitway

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please look at Card B24. Scientists predict that global warming
may soon have big effects on the polar regions. I will describe some of
these possible effects and, for each one, please say whether it would
bother you a great deal, some, a little, or not at all if it actually
happened. E. Inuit and other native peoples may no longer be able to
follow their traditional way of life.

## Values

- `[1]` a great deal

- `[2]` some

- `[3]` a little

- `[4]` not at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
