# What kind of house r's family lived at birth

bornhome

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking back to the time when you were born, tell me which box
shows the kind of house your family lived in at your work: A, B, or C?
We just want you to choose one box.

## Values

- `[1]` box a

- `[2]` box b

- `[3]` box c

- `[4]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
