# Should people obey laws without exceptn?

obeylaw

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In general, would you say that people should obey the law
without exception, or are there exceptional occasions on which people
should follow their consciences even if it means breaking the law?
(CIRCLE ONE ANSWER)

## Values

- `[2]` follow conscience on occassions
