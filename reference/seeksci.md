# Probable source of information about scientific issues

seeksci

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If you wanted to learn about scientific issues such as global
warming or biotechnology, where would you get information (" newspapers,
magazines, the Internet, books or other printed materials, TV, radio,
government agencies, family, friends, colleagues, or some other source)?
