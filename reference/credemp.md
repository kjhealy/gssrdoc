# Use credit report - employers

credemp

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (It’s not only lenders that use credit reports to make
decisions. How fair or unfair do you think it is for each of the
following to use credit reports?) Employers deciding who to hire. NOTE:
This variable originates on the GSS Next follow-on, and should not be
analyzed without the GSS Next-specific weights WTSSPS_NEXT or
WTSSNRPS_NEXT

## Values

- `[1]` very fair

- `[2]` somewhat fair

- `[3]` neither fair nor unfair

- `[4]` somewhat unfair

- `[5]` very unfair

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
