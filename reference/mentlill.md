# How likely x is experiencing mental illness

mentlill

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your opinion, how likely is it that NAME is experiencing
(READ A-E) – very likely, somewhat likely, not very likely, or not at
all likely? (IN PART E, READ ONLY THE PHRASE FOLLOWING THE LETTER OF THE
VIGNETTE) C. A mental illness

## Values

- `[1]` very likely

- `[2]` somewhat likely

- `[3]` not very likely

- `[4]` not at all likely

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
