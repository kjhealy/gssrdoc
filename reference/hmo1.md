# Hmo improves the quality of care

hmo1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question The health care system is changing rapidly with more and more
people being treated in Health Maintenance organizations (or HMOs) and
in managed care plans. How much do you agree or disagree with how the
HMOs and managed care plans are working? A. They improve the quality of
care.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly disagree

- `[2]` disagree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` agree

- `[5]` strongly agree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
