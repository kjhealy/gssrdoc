# Did spouse work with young children

spwkbaby

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What about your spouse/partner at that time – did he or she
work outside the home full-time, part-time, or not at all… B. And what
about when a child was under school age?

## Values

- `[1]` worked full-time

- `[2]` worked part-time

- `[3]` stayed home

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
