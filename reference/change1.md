# Help regarding important change \#1

change1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Suppose you needed advice about an important change in your
life – for example, about a job or moving to another part of the
country? A. Who would you turn to first for help? B. Who would you turn
to second? Please CIRCLE ONLY ONE FIRST CHOICE NUMBER AND ONE SECOND
CHOICE NUMBER. A. First Choice
