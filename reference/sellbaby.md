# Surrogate mothers should be permitted

sellbaby

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Recently, some married couples who are unable to have children
have paid women, called "surrogate mothers," to bear a child for them.
When the child is born, the couple becomes its adoptive parents and the
surrogate mother receives a fee. Do you think that this practice should
be permitted or forbidden under the law?
