# Concerned about environment

grncon

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question C. Generally speaking, how concerned are you about
environmental issues? Please tell me what you think, where 1 means you
are not at all concerned and 5 means you are very concerned.

## Values

- `[1]` 1 - not at all concerned

- `[5]` 5 - very concerned

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
