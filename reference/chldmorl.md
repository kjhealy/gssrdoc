# Moral-religious training improving?

chldmorl

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Compared with 10 years ago, would you say that American
children today are much better off, better off, about the same, worse
off, or much worse off regarding: D. Their moral or religious training.

## Values

- `[1]` much better

- `[2]` better

- `[3]` about the same

- `[4]` worse

- `[5]` much worse

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
