# How often r's friends or family were angry with r

upset

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking about the important people in your life, such as your
spouse or partner, your family members, or close friends, how often in
the past 4 weeks did any of these people act angry or upset with you?
