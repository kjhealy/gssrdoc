# Help for household tasks \#1

chores1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question First, there are some household and garden jobs you can”t
really Do alone –for example, you may need someone to hold a ladder, or
to help you move furniture. A. Who would you turn to first for help? B.
Who would you turn to second? Please CIRCLE ONLY ONE FIRST CHOICE NUMBER
AND ONE SECOND CHOICE NUMBER. A. First Choice
