# How often visit closest friend

frivisit

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking now of close friends - not your husband or wife or
partner or family members - but people you feel fairly close to… D. How
often do you see or visit with your friend (the friend you feel closest
to)?

## Values

- `[1]` lives in hshld

- `[2]` daily

- `[3]` several x week

- `[4]` once a week

- `[5]` once a month

- `[6]` several x year

- `[7]` less often

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
