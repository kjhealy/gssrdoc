# In what order go to a spiritual or a natural healer

orhealer

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are the things you said NAME should do. INTERVIEWER: READ
"YES" ANSWERS AND ASK: In what order should s/he do them? F. Go to a
spiritual or a natural healer for help.
