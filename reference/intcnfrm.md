# How often r tries to confirm information with other sources

intcnfrm

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question When looking for news or information about political matters
online, how often, if at all, do you… Try to confirm information you
find by searching online for another source?

## Values

- `[1]` very often

- `[2]` often

- `[3]` sometimes

- `[4]` rarely

- `[5]` never

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
