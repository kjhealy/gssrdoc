# Control r feels over own life

cntrlife

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people feel they have completely free choice and control
over the way their lives turn out, and other people feel that what they
themselves do has no real effect on what happens to them. Please use
this scale to indicate how much freedom of choice and control you feel
you have over the way your life turns out.

## Values

- `[1]` no choice and control

- `[4]` some choice and control

- `[7]` a great deal of choice and control

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
