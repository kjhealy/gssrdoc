# R involved in any task force for decision-making

empinput

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some companies have organized workplace decision-making in ways
to get more employee input and involvement. Are you personally involved
in any group, team, committee, or task force that addresses issues such
as product quality, cost cutting, productivity, health and safety, or
other workplace issues?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
