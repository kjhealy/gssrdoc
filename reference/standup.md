# Stand up for your own opinion

standup

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people think that it's important to stand up for your own
opinion even if it makes others around you uncomfortable. Others think
that it's better to keep your views to yourself if they would make
others around you uncomfortable. Which position comes closer to your
view?
