# Percent of people who shopped at the supermarket during childhood who were asian

chldshop_asn

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Think about the supermarket where your family shopped. What
percentage of the people who shopped at the supermarket belong to each
of the following categories? - Asian
