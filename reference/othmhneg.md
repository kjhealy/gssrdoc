# R's other acquaintences negative attitudes about mh problems

othmhneg

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking about other people you know personally outside of your
family, to what extent do they hold negative attitudes about people with
mental health problems?

## Values

- `[1]` very much

- `[2]` somewhat

- `[3]` not very much

- `[4]` not at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
