# Husb better if bad marrge ended by div

divhubby

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question And when a marriage is troubled and unhappy, is it generally
better for the husband if the couple stays together or gets divorced?

## Values

- `[1]` much better to div

- `[2]` better to divorce

- `[3]` worse to divorce

- `[4]` much worse to div

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
