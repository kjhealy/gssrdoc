# You organize schedules or tasks, not supervise

othduty3

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (On the card you have are/Here is a card listing) 7 different
kinds of work activities. Which ones do you do with employees whom you
do not officially supervise? Please mention all that apply: C. Organize
schedules or tasks.

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
