# Can find out where you will be working

findout

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question On any given day, is there some place where you find out where
you will be working?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
