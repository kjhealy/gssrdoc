# Did r vote in 1972 election

vote72

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. In 1972, you remember that McGovern ran for President on the
Democratic ticket against Nixon for the Republicans. Do you remember for
sure whether or not you voted in that election?

## Values

- `[1]` voted

- `[2]` did not vote

- `[3]` not eligible

- `[4]` refused

- `[6]` wldnt vt-relig

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
