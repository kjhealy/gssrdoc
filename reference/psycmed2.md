# People should stop taking if symptoms gone

psycmed2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please tell me how much you agree or disagree with the
following statements about medicines prescribed by doctors to help
people who are having problems with their emotions, nerves or their
mental health: B. If symptoms are no longer present, people should stop
taking these medications.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
