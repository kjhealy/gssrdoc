# Are cities or countryside benefiting more from digital tech

urbantech

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you think that in America people who live in the countryside
or people who live in cities are benefitting more, or both equally, or
neither, from the use of the Internet and digital technology?

## Values

- `[1]` people who live in the countryside are benefitting more than
  people who live in cities

- `[2]` people who live in the countryside and people who live in cities
  are benefitting equally

- `[3]` people who live in cities are benefitting more than people who
  live in the countryside

- `[4]` neither people from the countryside nor from cities are
  benefitting

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
