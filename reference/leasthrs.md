# Fewest hrs/week worked in past month

leasthrs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In the last month, what is the fewest number of hours you
worked in a week, at all paid jobs? Please do not include weeks in which
you missed some or all hours because of illness, vacation, or other
personal obligations. HOURS:
