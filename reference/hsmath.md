# The highest level of math r completed in high school

hsmath

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, let me ask you to think about the courses you took in high
school. What was the highest level of math that you completed in high
school?

## Values

- `[0]` no math in hs; didn't go to hs

- `[1]` general math, business, or vocational math

- `[2]` pre-algebra

- `[3]` one year of algebra

- `[4]` two years of algebra

- `[5]` geometry (plane or solid or both)

- `[6]` trigonometry/linear programming/analysis

- `[7]` pre-calculus

- `[8]` calculus

- `[9]` statistics/probability

- `[10]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
