# Expect u.s. in war within 10 years (with volunteered response on web)

uswaryv

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you expect the United States to fight in another world war
within the next ten years?

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[3]` undecided (phone mode only: volunteered)

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
