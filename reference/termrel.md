# My religious community very helpful

termrel

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, I would like to talk about concerns you may have when
considering what may happen at the end of your life. please tell me how
much you agree or disagree with each of these statements: B. My
religious community would be very helpful if I were terminally ill.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
