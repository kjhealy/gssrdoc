# How many people seen at church or group meeting

meetings

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Of these\_\_\_\_friends and relatives, about how many do you
stay in contact with by: D. Seeing them at meetings or events related to
church, clubs, or other groups?

## Values

- `[1]` 0

- `[2]` 1 - 2

- `[3]` 3 - 5

- `[4]` 6 - 10

- `[5]` 11 - 15

- `[6]` 16-25

- `[7]` 26-50

- `[8]` 50+

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
