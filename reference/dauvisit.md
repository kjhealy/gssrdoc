# How often visit with daughter

dauvisit

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How many daughters age 18 or older do you have? (We mean
daughters who are still alive; please include step-daughters,
half-daughters and adopted daughters.) A. How often do you see or visit
with your daughter?

## Values

- `[1]` lives in hshld

- `[2]` daily

- `[3]` several x week

- `[4]` once a week

- `[5]` once a month

- `[6]` several x year

- `[7]` less often

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
