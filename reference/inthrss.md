# Has r or someone they know been harassed online

inthrss

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people report experiences of harassment on the Internet.
Have you or someone you know personally ever experienced online
harassment or online hate speech?

## Values

- `[1]` yes, me myself

- `[2]` yes, someone i know personally

- `[3]` yes, me and someone else i know personally

- `[4]` no

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
