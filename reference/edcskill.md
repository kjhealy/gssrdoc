# How important school in developing skills

edcskill

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now think about the skills that you actually use in your job.
How important would you say each of the following was in developing
these skills? A. School, college, or university

## Values

- `[1]` very important

- `[2]` important

- `[3]` not un/important

- `[4]` not important

- `[5]` not important at all

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
