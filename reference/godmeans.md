# Life meaningful because god exists

godmeans

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you agree or disagree with the following. . . C. To me, life
is meaningful only because God exists.

## Values

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree
