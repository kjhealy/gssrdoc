# Nothing inherently wrong with prostitution

sellsex

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with the following
statements: A. There is nothing inherently wrong with prostitution, so
long as the health risks can be minimized. If consenting adults agree to
exchange money for sex, that is their business.
