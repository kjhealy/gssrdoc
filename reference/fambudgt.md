# How couples monitor budget

fambudgt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How do you and your spouse/partner organize the income that one
or both of you receive? Please choose the option that comes closest.

## Values

- `[1]` i manage, give prtnr share

- `[2]` prntnr manages, gives me share

- `[3]` pool all money, take out what we need

- `[4]` pool some money, keep some separate

- `[5]` keep money separate

- `[6]` not married

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
