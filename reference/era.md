# Does r favor or oppose era?

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you heard or read about the Equal Rights Amendment? B. Do
you strongly favor, somewhat favor, somewhat oppose, or strongly oppose
this amendment?
