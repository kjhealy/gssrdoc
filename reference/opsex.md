# Need to be right sex to get ahead

opsex

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please show for each of these how important you think it is for
getting ahead in life . . . L. Being born a man or a woman – how
important is that for getting ahead in life?

## Values

- `[1]` essential

- `[2]` very important

- `[3]` fairly important

- `[4]` not very important

- `[5]` not important at all
