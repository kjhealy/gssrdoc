# Number of years r attended church school

churhsch

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Did you get any of your grade or high school education in
parochial schools or other schools run by religious groups?
Yes................(ASK A) No..............(GO TO SUNSCH16) A. How many
years did you attend religious schools?

## Values

- `[0]` never went or less than half year

- `[95]` one or more year

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
