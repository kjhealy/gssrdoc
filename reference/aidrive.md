# R's comfort with driverless car

aidrive

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Various human actions can be done by robots or Artificial
Intelligence (AI). For each of the following, please indicate, using a
scale from 0 to 10, how you would personally feel about it. On this
scale, '0' means that you would feeltotally uncomfortable and '10' means
that you would feeltotally comfortable with this situation... Being
driven in a driverless car.

## Values

- `[0]` totally uncomfortable with this situation

- `[10]` totally comfortable with this situation

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
