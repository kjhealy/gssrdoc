# R credit report

credrep

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In general, would you say that your credit record is poor,
fair, good, very good, or excellent? NOTE: This variable originates on
the GSS Next follow-on, and should not be analyzed without the GSS
Next-specific weights WTSSPS_NEXT or WTSSNRPS_NEXT

## Values

- `[1]` poor

- `[2]` fair

- `[3]` good

- `[4]` very good

- `[5]` excellent

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
