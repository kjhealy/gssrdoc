# Percent of children you knew (friends, classmates) during childhood who were some other race

chldchld_oth

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Not including your family, what percentage of the children you
knew (friends, classmates belonged to each of the following
categories? - Some other race
