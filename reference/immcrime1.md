# Immigrants increase crime rates

immcrime1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question There are different opinions about immigrants from other
countries living in America. By “immigrants” we mean people who come to
settle in America. How much do you agree or disagree with each of the
following statements?… Immigrants increase crime rates.
