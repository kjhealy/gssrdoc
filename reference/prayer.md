# Bible prayer in public schools

prayer

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. The United States Supreme Court has ruled that no state or
local government may require the reading of the Lord's Prayer or Bible
verses in public schools. What are your views on this–do you approve or
disapprove of the court ruling?
