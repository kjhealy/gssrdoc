# Who provides for old people

prvdold

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (HAND CARD C3) (People have different opinions on who should
provide services in America. Who do you think should primarily
provide...) Care for old people?  (Should it be?)

## Values

- `[1]` government

- `[2]` private companies/for-profit organizations

- `[3]` non-profit organizations/charities/cooperatives

- `[4]` religious organizations

- `[5]` family, relatives or friends

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
