# R's children under 18 living with r

kidnow1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Are your biological or adopted children under age 18 living
with you now at least some of the time?

## Values

- `[1]` yes, all of them live with me at least some of the time

- `[2]` some live with me at least some of the time

- `[3]` none live with me

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
