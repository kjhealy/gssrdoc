# How important training in developing skills

jobskill

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now think about the skills that you actually use in your job.
How important would you say each of the following was in developing
these skills? B. Training or experience gained in my present or a
previous job

## Values

- `[1]` very important

- `[2]` important

- `[3]` not impt/unimp

- `[4]` not important

- `[5]` not imprt at all

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
