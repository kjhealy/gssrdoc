# Trend of online contact with politically different

intoppo

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In the last 12 months, if at all, has your online contact with
the following people increased, decreased or remained the same?… People
with different political views from your own.

## Values

- `[1]` greatly decreased

- `[2]` somewhat decreased

- `[3]` remained the same

- `[4]` somewhat increased

- `[5]` greatly increased
