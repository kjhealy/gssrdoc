# Job opportunities for women

fejobop

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How about job opportunities for women – do you think they are,
in general, better or worse than job opportunities for men with similar
education and experience?

## Values

- `[1]` much bettr for women

- `[2]` better for women

- `[3]` no difference

- `[4]` worse for women

- `[5]` much wrse for women

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
