# How committed to group is r

caregrp

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Personally, how committed do you feel to this organization? Not
committed at all, slightly committed, moderately committed, strongly
committed, or very strongly committed.

## Values

- `[1]` not committed

- `[2]` slightly comm

- `[3]` moderately comm

- `[4]` strongly comm

- `[5]` very strongly comm

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
