# Paid what you need

socdif2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are different opinions about social differences in this
country. Please tell me for each one whether you strongly agree,
somewhat agree, somewhat disagree, or strongly disagree. B. Personal
income should not be determined solely by one's work. Rather, everybody
should get what he/she needs to provide a decent life to his/her family.

## Values

- `[1]` agree strongly

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` disagree

- `[4]` disagree strongly

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
