# Aged should be financially independent

finind1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People differ in their ideas about what it takes for a young
person to become an adult these days. How important is it for them to
be... Financially independent from their parents/guardians A. By what
age should this normally occur?
