# I am inclined to feel i am a failure

afailure

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Indicate your agreement with each of the following statements
by selecting the number that comes closest to your answer: B. All in
all, I'm inclinded to feel I'm a failure.
