# Thinking us how often feel frustrated?

usfrustr

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I want to ask some questions about how you feel when you
think about this country, the United States. When you think about the
United States: C. When you think about the United States, how often do
you feel FRUSTRATED? Do you feel frustrated:

## Values

- `[1]` always

- `[2]` most of the time

- `[3]` some of the time

- `[4]` rarely

- `[5]` never

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
