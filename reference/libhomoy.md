# Allow homosexuals book in library y

libhomoy

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If somebody in your community suggests that a book the gay
person wrote in favor of homosexuality should be taken out of your
public library, would you favor removing it, or not?

## Values

- `[2]` not remove
