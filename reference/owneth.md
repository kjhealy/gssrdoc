# Do ethic politicians serve ethnic groups better

owneth

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you feel that people are best represented in politics by
leaders from their own racial or ethnic background, or doesn't the
leader's background make very much difference?

## Values

- `[1]` leaders from own background

- `[2]` leaders background no matter

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
