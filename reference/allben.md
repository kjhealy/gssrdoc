# Everyone benefit from traditional family

allben

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In many married couples, women take the main responsibility for
the care of the home and children, while men take the main
responsibility for supporting the family financially. Who benefits from
this: men, women, children, everyone, or no one? PROBE: Anyone else?
UNTIL R SAYS NO. D. Everyone
