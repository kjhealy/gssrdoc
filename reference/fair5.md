# People fair or not

fair5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B2. Generally speaking, do you think that most people would try
to take advantage of you if they got the chance, or would they try to be
fair? Please tell me what you think, where 1 means most people would try
to take advantage of you and 5 means that most people would try to be
fair.

## Values

- `[1]` most people would try to take advantage

- `[5]` most people would try to be fair

- `[6]` 6 can't choose

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
