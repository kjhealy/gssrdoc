# Country of spouse's family origin

spethnic

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question From what countries or part of the world did your
(wife”s/husband”s) ancestors come? A. IF MORE THAN ONE COUNTRY NAMED:
Which one of these countries does your (wife/husband) feel closer to? A.
SUMMARY ETHNICITY OF SPOUSE
