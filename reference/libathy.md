# Allow anti-religionist to teach y

libathy

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (There are always some people whose ideas are considered bad or
dangerous by other people. For instance, somebody who is against all
churches and religion…) If some people in your community suggested that
a book this person wrote against churches and religion should be taken
out of your public library, would you favor removing this book, or not?
