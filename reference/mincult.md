# Ethnic minorities should be given gov assistance

mincult

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with the following
statements? B. Ethnic minorities should be given government assistance
to preserve their customs and traditions

## Values

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` disagree strongly
