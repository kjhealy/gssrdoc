# Who should pay for costs of care for under school age child

carecost

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Who do you think should primarily cover the costs of childcare
for children under school age?

## Values

- `[1]` the family

- `[2]` the government/public funds

- `[3]` the employers

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job

  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
