# If self employed, number of other employees

numemps_0422

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Are/were you self-employed or do/did you work for someone else?
(If "Self-employed") Do/did you have any employees? (If "Yes") How many
employees do/did you have?

As of 2024, NUMEMPS has been recoded to ranges of employees. This has
been retroactively applied to all previous years of NUMEMPS. The
original NUMEMPS containing data from 2004 to 2022 has been renamed
NUMEMPS_0422.

## Values

- `[97]` 97 or more

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
