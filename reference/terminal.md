# Have you or close friends relative faced with terminal illness

terminal

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now in this section, I will ask you some questions about the
end of life. Have you or any close friend or family members been faced
with a terminal or life-threatening illness?

## Values

- `[1]` yes myself

- `[2]` yes friend

- `[3]` yes immed reltve

- `[4]` yes othr relatve

- `[5]` no

- `[6]` self and one

- `[7]` two or more

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
