# Who has the greatest right to keep their jobs?

layoffs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question From time to time companies must lay-off some of their
employees. If some have to be laid-off, who, in your opinion, has the
greatest right to keep their jobs?

## Values

- `[1]` those who have worked longest

- `[2]` those who have the greatest needs

- `[3]` those who are the best workers

- `[4]` difficulty in finding a new job

- `[5]` union members

- `[6]` older employees

- `[7]` younger employees

- `[8]` men

- `[9]` women

- `[10]` whites

- `[11]` minorities-non-whites

- `[12]` other -please specify

- `[13]` owners,bosses

- `[14]` essential employees

- `[15]` newest employees

- `[16]` most loyal employees

- `[17]` production. hands-on employees

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
