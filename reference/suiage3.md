suiage3

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question We would like to know a few other things about that person. B.
How old was that person? Was (he/she)…

- `[1]` 10 years or under

- `[2]` 11-20

- `[3]` 21-40

- `[4]` 41 year or older
