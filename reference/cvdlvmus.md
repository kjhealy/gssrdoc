# Change in in-person events since first year of covid

cvdlvmus

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question On March 13th, 2020, federal authorities declared a national
emergency due to the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic. For the next
questions, please consider March 2020 to March 2021 as the first year of
the COVID-19 pandemic. The following question is about arts activities
you may be doing now. Compared to the first year of the COVID-19
pandemic (March 2020 to March 2021), are you now attending in-person
live arts events more often, at the same rate, less often, or are you
not attending in-person live arts at all? (This may include music,
theater, or dance performances.)

NOTE: This variable originates on the NEA follow-on, and should not be
analyzed without the NEA-specific weights WTSSPS_NEA or WTSSNRPS_NEA

## Values

- `[1]` more often

- `[2]` at the same rate

- `[3]` less often

- `[4]` r not attending these activities

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
