# Govt should track sick people

mobilsurv

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (Do you think the United States government should or should not
have the right to do the following at times of severe epidemics?) Use
digital (mobile phone) surveillance to track infected people.

## Values

- `[1]` definitely should have the right

- `[2]` probably should have the right

- `[3]` probably should not have the right

- `[4]` definitely should not have the right

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
