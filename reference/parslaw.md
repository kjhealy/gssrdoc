# How often does r contact parents-in-law

parslaw

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now some questions about your contact with other relatives.
Please indicate how often you have been in contact with any of the
following types of relatives in the last four weeks. C. Parents-in-law

## Values

- `[1]` more than twice in last 4 weeks

- `[2]` once or twice in last 4 weeks

- `[3]` not at all in last 4 weeks

- `[4]` i have no living relative of this type

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
