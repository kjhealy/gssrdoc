# R send or receive to or from church members

emchurch

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking now about all your home, work or other computers, J.
Members of your church?

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
