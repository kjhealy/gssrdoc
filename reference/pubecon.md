# Should media publish economic plans?

pubecon

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. Now suppose the confidential government papers were about
economic plans. Should the newspaper be allowed to publish the papers?
Or should the government have the power to prevent publication?

## Values

- `[1]` allow publication

- `[2]` stop publication

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
