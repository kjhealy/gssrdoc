# Should govt maintain secrecy over technology

sectech

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In order to maintain America's leadership in the world, the
government should maintain a high level of secrecy surrounding
technology with military uses. Do you . . .

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
