# In past 12 months, r has participated in charitable or religious volunteer orgs

partvol

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In the past 12 months, how often, if at all, have you taken
part in the activities? Of charitable or religious organisations that do
voluntary work?

## Values

- `[1]` once a week or more

- `[2]` one to three times a month

- `[3]` several times a year

- `[4]` once in the past year

- `[5]` never

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
