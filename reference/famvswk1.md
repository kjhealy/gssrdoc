# How often difficult to conc at work because family resp

famvswk1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How often has each of the following happened to you during the
past three months? D. You have found it difficult to concentrate at work
because of your family responsibilities.

## Values

- `[0]` doesn't apply/no job

- `[1]` several times a week

- `[2]` several times a month

- `[3]` once or twice

- `[4]` never

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
