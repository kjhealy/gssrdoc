# Business leaders best for country

bizbstnk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please look at Card E16. When making policy recommendations
about nuclear power, to what extent do you think the following groups
would support what is best for the country as a whole or what serves
their own narrow interests? FI INSTRUCTION: IF ASKED WHAT NARROW
INTEREST MEANS: Well, someone might gain financially if a certain policy
were adopted or it might advance his or her career. C. Business leaders
