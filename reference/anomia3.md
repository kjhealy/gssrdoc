# No right & wrong ways to make money

anomia3

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to read you several more statements. Some people
agree with a statement, others disagree. As I read each one, tell me
whether you more or less agree with it, or more or less disagree. C. To
make money, there are no right and wrong ways any more, only easy and
hard ways.
