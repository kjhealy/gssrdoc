# How satisfied were you with the work

satfixes

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How satisfied were you with the work that was done for you?

## Values

- `[1]` not too satisfied

- `[2]` pretty satisfied

- `[3]` extremely satisfied

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
