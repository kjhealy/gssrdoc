# Scientists pry into things they oughtn't

scipry

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some things that have been said about science. Would
you tell me if you tend to agree or disagree with them? C. Scientists
always seem to be prying into things that they really ought to stay out
of.
