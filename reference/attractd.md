# At what age were you first sexually attracted to same sex

attractd

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question At about what age were you first sexually attracted to someone
of the same sex?

## Values

- `[1]` under age 14

- `[2]` age 14-18

- `[3]` age 18-25

- `[4]` age 26-35

- `[5]` age 36-45

- `[6]` age 46-55

- `[7]` age 56-65

- `[8]` age 66 or older

- `[9]` i have never told anyone i have never been sexually attracted to
  someone of the same sex

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
