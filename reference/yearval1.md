# Estimate of dollar value of payments in that year

yearval1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What was the approximate total dollar value of these various
payments in that year? NOTE: All responses who give valid answers to
EXTRAYR are asked YEARVAL.
