# Opinion of political influence of women

feinflu

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People have different opinions about the amount of influence
that various groups have in American life and politics. Do you think
that women have far too much influence, too much influence, about the
right amount of influence, too little influence, or do they have far too
little influence?

## Values

- `[1]` far too much

- `[2]` too much

- `[3]` right amount

- `[4]` too little

- `[5]` far too little

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
