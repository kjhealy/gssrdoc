# Do whites get proper govt attention

whtgovt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. Do you think that white people in this country get much more
attention from government than they deserve, more attention than they
deserve, about the right amount of attention, less attention than they
deserve, or much less attention from government than they deserve?

- `[1]` much more

- `[2]` more

- `[3]` about right

- `[4]` less

- `[5]` much less
