# Stranger was beating up a woman

hitbeatr

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Are there any situations that you can imagine in which you
would approve of a man punching an adult male stranger? D. Was beating
up a woman and the man saw it?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
