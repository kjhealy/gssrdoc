# Income alone is enough

rincblls

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you feel that the income from your job alone is enough to
meet your family's usual monthly expenses and bills?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
