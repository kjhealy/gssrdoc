# My own previous knowledge of the firm

fixera

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Think about the most recent time you've paid for home
maintenance service – e.g. a roofer, a painter, a plumber or something
like that. What made you choose the person or business that did the
work? (Choose all that apply.) A. My own previous knowledge of the firm
or workers.

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
