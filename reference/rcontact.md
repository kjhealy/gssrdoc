# Do you have personal contact at work

rcontact

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (HAND CARD D7) (For each of these statements about your (main)
job, please tell me how much you agree or disagree with it as it applies
to your job.) In my job, I have personal contact with other people.
(Would you say...)

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
