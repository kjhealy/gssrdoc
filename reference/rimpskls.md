# Job gives r chance to improve skills

rimpskls

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question For each of these statements about your (main) job, please tell
me how much you agree or disagree with it as it applies to your job. A.
My job gives me a chance to improve my skills

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
