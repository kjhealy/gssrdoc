# Your relationship to the real estate agent

relagent

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which of the following best describes your relationship to the
real estate agent with whom you worked in your purchase of the home or
to the owner of the real estate agency? Please indicate what your
closest relationship was.

## Values

- `[1]` a relative including in-laws

- `[2]` a friend or acquaintance

- `[3]` a friend of a friend-relative

- `[4]` had previous business dealings

- `[5]` no prior relationship

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
