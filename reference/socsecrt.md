# Social security will be better

socsecrt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question When you retire, do you think Social Security benefits will be
much better than those now paid of retirees, somewhat better, about the
same, somewhat worse, or much worse?

## Values

- `[1]` much better

- `[2]` somewhat better

- `[3]` about the same

- `[4]` somewhat worse

- `[5]` much worse

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
