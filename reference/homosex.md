# Homosexual sex relations

homosex

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What about sexual relations between two adults of the same
sex–do you think it is always wrong, almost always wrong, wrong only
sometimes, or not wrong at all?

## Values

- `[1]` always wrong

- `[2]` almost always wrong

- `[3]` wrong only sometimes

- `[4]` not wrong at all

- `[5]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
