# Frequency r has work/school internet access

intprfssnl

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How often, if at all, do you typically use the Internet on any
device at the following locations for any reason?… At your work-place/at
school/ at university?

## Values

- `[1]` almost always

- `[2]` often

- `[3]` sometimes

- `[4]` hardly ever

- `[5]` never

- `[6]` no access

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
