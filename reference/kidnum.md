# Number of children age 18 and older

kidnum

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now some questions about your children who are aged 18 and
older. How many children age 18 and older do you have? (We mean children
who are still alive. Please include step-children and adopted children.)
If you have no children age 18 and older, enter '0'.

## Values

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
