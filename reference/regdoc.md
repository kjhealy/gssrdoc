# R has regular doctor or clinic

regdoc

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question This section is about physicians telling patients about how
health insurance and managed care policies affect health care. First, I
will ask you about your health care. Later, you will hear a recorded
conversation between a doctor and patient and I'll ask some questions
about the conversation.Do you have a regular doctor or clinic?
