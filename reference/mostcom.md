# Have most in common with which group

mostcom

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Of these groups"whites, blacks, Jews, Hispanics and Latin
Americans, Asian Americans, if you had to say, which One (other than
your own) do you feel you have the most in common with?

## Values

- `[1]` whites

- `[2]` blacks/african-americans

- `[3]` jews

- `[4]` hispanics

- `[5]` asians

- `[6]` equal in common to all

- `[7]` nothing in common with any

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
