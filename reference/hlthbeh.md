# Suffer health prob from behavior

hlthbeh

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Severe health problems may have many causes. How much do you
agree or disagree with the following statements? People suffer from
severe health problems... Because they behaved in ways that damaged
their health.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree
