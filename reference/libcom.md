# Allow communists book in library

libcom

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, I should like to ask you some questions about a man who
admits he is a Communist. C. Suppose he wrote a book which is in your
public library. Somebody in your community suggests that the book should
be removed from the library. Would you favor removing it, or not?

- `[2]` not remove
