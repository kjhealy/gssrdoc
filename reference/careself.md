# Those in need have to take care of themselves

careself

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please tell me whether you strongly agree, agree, neither agree
nor disagree, disagree, or strongly disagree with the following
statements: B. Those in need have to learn to take care of themselves
and not depend on others

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
