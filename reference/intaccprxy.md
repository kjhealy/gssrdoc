# How often r asked someone to use the internet on their behalf

intaccprxy

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In the last 12 months how often, if at all, did you ask someone
to do something for you on the Internet on your behalf, such as sending
an email, getting information or shopping online?

## Values

- `[1]` not at all

- `[2]` once or twice

- `[3]` more often

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
