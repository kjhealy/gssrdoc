# If less welfare more will work

lessfare

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some proposals about welfare. I would like to know if
you favor or oppose them. (READ "A") - Would you say that you strongly
favor it, favor it, neither favor nor oppose it, oppose it, or strongly
oppose it? B. Second, reducing welfare benefits to make working for a
living more attractive.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly favor

- `[2]` favor

- `[3]` neither favor nor oppose

- `[4]` oppose

- `[5]` strongly oppose

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
