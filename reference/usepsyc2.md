# Will take medicine because cant cope w stress

usepsyc2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How likely would you be to take doctor-prescribed psychiatric
medication in the following situations? B. ...because you didn”t know
how to cope anymore with the stresses of life.

## Values

- `[1]` very likely

- `[2]` somewhat likely

- `[3]` mixed

- `[4]` somewht unlikely

- `[5]` very unlikely

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
