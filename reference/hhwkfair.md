# Sharing of hh work between r and spouse

hhwkfair

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which of the following best applies to the sharing of household
work between you and your spouse/partner?

## Values

- `[1]` i do much more than my fair share of hh work

- `[2]` i do a bit more than my fair share of hh work

- `[3]` i do roughly my fair share of hh work

- `[4]` i do a bit less than my fair share of hh work

- `[5]` i do much less than my fair share of hh work

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
