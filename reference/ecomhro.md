# Hours per week using other computer hsehld mngm

ecomhro

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question For about how many minutes or hours a week do you use (this
other computer/these other computers) for each of the following: A.
Shopping, paying bills and other household management? 2. Hours per
week?

## Values

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
