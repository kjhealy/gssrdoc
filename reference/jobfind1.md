# How easy for r to find a same job

jobfind1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How easy would it be for you to find a job with another
employer with approximately the same income and fringe benefits as you
have now?

## Values

- `[1]` very easy to find similar job

- `[2]` somewhat easy to find similar job

- `[3]` not easy at all to find similar job

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
