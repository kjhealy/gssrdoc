# Effect of mental health problem on relation with

mntlrel

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question I want you to think about the person with a mental health
problem with whom you have had the most contact. As a result of the
mental health problem, would you say that your relationship with this
person...

## Values

- `[1]` became stronger

- `[2]` was unchanged

- `[3]` became worse

- `[4]` ended as a result of the problem

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
