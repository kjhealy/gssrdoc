# R active in any other group

actother

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you ever done any active work for (EACH "YES" IN
MEMFRAT-MEMOTHER)? I mean been a leader, helped organize meetings, been
an officer, or given time or money? P. Any other groups

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
