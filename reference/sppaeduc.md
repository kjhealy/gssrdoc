# Highest schl grade spouse's father completed

sppaeduc

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. What is the highest grade or year of regular school that
your (husband's/wife's) father ever completed? CODE EXACTGRADE.

- `[1]` 1st grade
