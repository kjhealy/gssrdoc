# Patient was self

relmhsp1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Was this a relative, a close friend, or just someone you didn't
know very well? (CIRCLE ALL THAT APPLY) PROBE UNTIL "NO": ANYONE ELSE?
A. Respondent.

## Values

- `[1]` self

- `[2]` not self
