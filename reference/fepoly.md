# Men or women better suited for politics

fepoly

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. Would you say that most men are better suited emotionally
for politics than are most women, that men and women are equally suited,
or that women are better suited than men in this area?

## Values

- `[1]` men

- `[2]` equal

- `[3]` women
