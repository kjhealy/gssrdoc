# Number of sex partner's last 5 years

partopn5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, think about the past five years–the time since
February/March 1986, and including the past 12 months, how many sex
partners have you had in that five year period?

## Values

- `[990]` -
