# Allow racist to speak

allowrac

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question E. Or, consider a person who believes that Blacks are
genetically inferior. If such a person wanted to speech in your
community claiming that Blacks are inferior, should he be allowed to
speak, make a or not?
