# Next to health, money is most important

anomia1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to read you several more statements. Some people
agree with a statement, others disagree. As I read each one, tell me
whether you more or less agree with it, or more or less disagree. A.
Next to health, money is the most important thing in life.
