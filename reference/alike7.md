# How alike: fly & tree

alike7

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'd like to turn to something a bit different. This section
is about reasoning abilities, that is, about how people think. Most
people take tests of their reasoning abilities and there is a lot of
interest in how these abilities may change over time. What I'd like you
to do is tell me how two things are alike. G. In what way are a fly and
a tree alike?
