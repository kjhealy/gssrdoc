# Was problem of narrow or wide concern

locself

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you ever personally gone to see, or spoken to, or written
to – some member of local government or some other person of influence
in the community about some needs or problems? A. Was this need or
problem primarily of concern to you, your friends and family, or was it
an issue of wider concern?
