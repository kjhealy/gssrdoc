# Man and woman do things together-separate

sharesep

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Next, we have some questions about the kind of relationship you
would like with a spouse or partner. It doesn't matter whether you are
now married or living with someone. For each pair of items on the card,
which type of relationship would you prefer? In pair A, would you prefer
the type of relationship described in statement 1 or statement 2? How
about in pair B? Pair C? B.
