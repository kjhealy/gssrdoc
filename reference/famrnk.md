# Rank in society of r

famrnk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now we would like to know where you and other people fit in.
Please look at the diagram you think best describes America today, the
one you chose first: A. Where would you say you and your family actually
are - at the top, somewhere in the middle, or toward the bottom?

## Values

- `[1]` top

- `[7]` bottom

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
