# Help for depression \#1

down1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now suppose you feel just a bit down or depressed, and you
wanted to talk about it. A. Who would you turn to first for help? B. Who
would you turn to second? PLEASE CIRCLE ONLY ONE FIRST CHOICE NUMBER AND
ONE SECOND CHOICE NUMBER. A. First Choice
