# English official language of u.s.

engoff1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you favor a law making English the official language of the
United States, or do you oppose such a law?

## Values

- `[1]` favor

- `[2]` oppose

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
