# What percentage of population is of mixed heritage

usmixed

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People may come from one racial group such as being White,
Black/African-American, Hispanic, Asian, or American Indian, or people
may come from two or more of these groups. What percent of people in the
United States today do you think have parents or their ancestors from
two or more racial groups?
