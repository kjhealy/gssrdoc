# R relationship to the salesperson

reldealr

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which of the following best describes your relationship to the
salesperson from whom you purchased the car or to the owner of the auto
dealership. Please indicate what your closest relationship was.

## Values

- `[1]` a relative , including in-laws

- `[2]` a friend or acquaintance

- `[3]` a friend of a friend-relative

- `[4]` previous business dealings

- `[5]` no prior relationship

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
