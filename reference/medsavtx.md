# Govt programs for mental health treatment will

medsavtx

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, I'd like to ask you for your opinions on several issues.
For each question, please tell me if you strongly agree, agree somewhat,
disagree somewhat or strongly disagree with the statement. E. In the
long-run, government programs that provide mental health treatment to
children will save taxpayers money.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree somewhat

- `[3]` disagree somewhat

- `[4]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
