# Pay differences needed for motivation

incentiv

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people earn a lot of money while others do not earn very
much at all. In order to get people to work hard, do you think large
differences in pay are . . .

## Values

- `[1]` absolutely neccessary

- `[2]` probably neccessary

- `[3]` probably not necc

- `[4]` definitely not necc

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
