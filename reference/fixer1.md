# What was the most important reason?

fixer1

## Source

## Details

Question Think about the most recent time you've paid for home
maintenance service – e.g. a roofer, a painter, a plumber or something
like that. What made you choose the person or business that did the
work? (Choose all that apply.) G. Most important reason from above
