# Give kid prozac if is hostile

prozkid1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How likely would you be to give doctor prescribed Prozac to
your child or a child you were responsible for in the following
situations (A child would be considered anyone between the age of 8 and
15). A. ...because s/he is hostile, often loses his/her temper, often
argues with adults, actively defies authority and seems spiteful or
vindictive.
