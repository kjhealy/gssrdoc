# Can husband refuse to sire children

mebear

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If the wife in a family wants children, but the husband decides
that he does not want any children, is it all right for the husband to
refuse to have children.

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
