# Should child be forced by law to admit to a hosp

forcehos

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question C. Do you think that parents of children like (John/Mary)
should be forced by law to admit (John/Mary) to a hospital for
treatment?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no
