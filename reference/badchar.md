# Situation caused by own bad character

badchar

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. Now, I want to ask you some specific questions about
(John/Mary). In your opinion, how likely is it that (John/Mary)'s
situation MIGHT be caused by (his/her) own bad character–very likely,
somewhat likely, not very likely, or not at all likely?

## Values

- `[1]` very likely

- `[2]` somewhat likely

- `[3]` not very likely

- `[4]` not at all likely

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
