# Is r concerned about privacy?

privacy

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How concerned are you about threats to your personal privacy in
America today? Would you say you are very concerned, somewhat concerned,
only a little concerned, or not concerned at all?

## Values

- `[1]` very concerned

- `[2]` somewhat

- `[3]` only a little

- `[4]` not concerned

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
