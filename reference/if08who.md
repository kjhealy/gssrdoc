# Who you would have voted for

if08who

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question K. In 2008, you remember that Obama ran for President on the
Democratic ticket against McCain for the Republicans. Do you remember
for sure whether or not you voted in that election? 2. IF DID NOT VOTE
OR INELIGIBLE: Who would you have voted for, for President, if you had
voted?

## Values

- `[1]` obama

- `[2]` mccain

- `[3]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
