# Woman's health seriously endangered

abhlth

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please tell me whether or not you think it should be possible
for a pregnant woman to obtain a legal abortion if. . .READ EACH
STATEMENT, AND CIRCLE ONE CODE FOR EACH. C. If the woman”s own health is
seriously endangered by the pregnancy?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
