# Pol party encourage ppl to be active in politics in am

polactve

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking now about politics in America, to what extent do you
agree or disagree with the following statements? A. Political parties
encourage people to become active in politics

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
