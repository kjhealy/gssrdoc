# Elected officials how much influence

polinfnk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some say that the government should expand the use of nuclear
power to generate electricity in the U.S. Others oppose any expansion in
the use of nuclear power in this country. Please look at Card E15. How
much influence should each of the following groups have in deciding
whether to expand the use of nuclear power? Elected officials. (Would
you say...)

## Values

- `[1]` a great deal of influence

- `[2]` a fair amount

- `[3]` a little influence

- `[4]` none at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
