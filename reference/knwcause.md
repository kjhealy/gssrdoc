# Know causes of environment issues

knwcause

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question F1. How much do you feel you know about the causes of these
sorts of environmental problems? Please tell me what you think, where 1
indicates you feel you know nothing at all and 5 indicates you feel you
know a great deal.

## Values

- `[1]` know nothing at all

- `[5]` know a great deal

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
