# Would local officials listen to problem

loccare

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If you had some complaint about a local government activity and
took that complaint to a member of the local government council, would
you expect him or her to pay a lot of attention to what you say, some
attention, very little attention, or none at all?

## Values

- `[1]` lot of attention

- `[2]` some

- `[3]` very little

- `[4]` none

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
