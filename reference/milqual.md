# Quality of persons in military

milqual

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question As you know, this country stopped the military draft in 1972.
Since that time we have relied on volunteers. Now I'd like to ask you a
few questions about our armed forces. A. How would you rate the quality
of the men and women now serving in the armed forces? Would you say the
quality of personnel is excellent, good, not so good, or poor? (Just
your own opinion based on what you've heard or read.)
