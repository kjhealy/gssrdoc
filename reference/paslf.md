# Was father self-employed?

paslf

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here is a list of different types of jobs. Which type did your
father have when you were 16. (If your father did not have a job then,
please give the job he used to have.) A. Was your father self-employed,
or did he work for someone else?

## Values

- `[1]` self-employed

- `[2]` someone else

- `[3]` no dad-never had job

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
