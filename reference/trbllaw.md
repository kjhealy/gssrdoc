# Child with med get into less trouble with the

trbllaw

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, I”d like to ask you for your opinions on several issues.
For each question, please tell me if you strongly agree, agree somewhat,
disagree somewhat or strongly disagree with the statement. D. Children
who receive medical treatment for behavior problems are less likely to
get into trouble with the law later in life.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree somewhat

- `[3]` disagree somewhat

- `[4]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
