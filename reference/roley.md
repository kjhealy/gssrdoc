# Acts like boy-acts like girl-version y

roley

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. Which three qualities listed on this card would you say are
the most desirable for a child to have? B. Which one of these three is
the most desirable of all? C. All of the qualities listed on this card
may be desirable, but could you tell me which three you consider least
important? D. And which one of these three is least important of all? 7.
That he acts like a boy or she acts like a girl

## Values

- `[1]` 1 most desirable

- `[2]` 3 most desirable

- `[3]` not mentioned

- `[4]` 3 least desirable

- `[5]` 1 least desirable

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
