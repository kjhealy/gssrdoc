# Can wife refuse to have children

febear

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If the husband in a family wants children, but the wife decides
that she does not want any children, is it all right for the wife to
refuse to have children?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
