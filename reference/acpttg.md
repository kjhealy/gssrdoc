# Accepting people

acpttg

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you think American society has gone too far, about far
enough, or not far enough in accepting people (whose sex differs from
the one they were assigned at birth)? Question edits are in compliance
with federal administration directives released in January 2025.

## Values

- `[1]` too far

- `[2]` about far enough

- `[3]` not far enough

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
