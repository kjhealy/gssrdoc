# Time r would spend on friends

timefrnd

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Suppose you could change the way you spend your time, spending
more on some things and less on others. Which of these things on the
following list would you like to spend more time on, which would you
like to spend less time on, and which would you like to spend the same
amount of time as now? D. Time with your friends?

## Values

- `[1]` spend much more

- `[2]` spend a bit more

- `[3]` spend same

- `[4]` spend a bit less

- `[5]` spend much less

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
