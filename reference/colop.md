# Should chances for college be changed?

colop

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How do you feel about opportunities for young people to go to
college. Should opportunities be...

## Values

- `[1]` increased a lot

- `[2]` increased a little

- `[3]` kept the same

- `[4]` reduced a little

- `[5]` reduced a lot

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
