# In life, r has ever been harassed on job bc r was believed to be gay/les/bi

evharjb

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you ever in your life been harassed verbally or in writing
on the job by a co-worker, supervisor, or boss because you were believed
to be gay, lesbian, or bisexual?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
