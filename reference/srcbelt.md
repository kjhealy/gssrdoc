# Src beltcode

srcbelt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question C. SRC (SURVEY RESEARCH CENTER, UNIVERSITY OF MICHIGAN) NEW
BELT CODE

## Values

- `[1]` central city of 12 largest smsas

- `[2]` central city of the remainder of the 100 largest smsas

- `[3]` suburbs of 12 largest smsas

- `[4]` suburbs of the remaining 100 largest smsas

- `[5]` other urban (counties having towns of 10,000 or more)

- `[6]` other rural (counties having no towns of 10,000 or more)

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
