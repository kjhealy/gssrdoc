# Type of place lived in when 16 yrs old

res16

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which of the categories on this card comes closest to the type
of place you were living in when you were 16 years old?

## Values

- `[1]` in open country but not on a farm

- `[2]` farm

- `[3]` in a small city or town (under 50,000)

- `[4]` in a medium-size city (50,000-250,000)

- `[5]` in a suburb near a large city

- `[6]` in a large city (over 250,000)

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
