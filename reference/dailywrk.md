# How r's daily work is organized

dailywrk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which of the following statements best describes how your daily
work is organized?

## Values

- `[1]` i am free to decide how my daily work is organized

- `[2]` i can decide within certain limits

- `[3]` i am not free to decide how my daily work is organized

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
