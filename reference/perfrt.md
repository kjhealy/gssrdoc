# Performance rating last year

perfrt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If you received a performance rating from your organization in
2021, which statement best describes the rating you received?

## Values

- `[1]` below expectations

- `[2]` meets expectations

- `[3]` above expectations

- `[4]` r didn't receive a performance rating

- `[5]` none of the statements match the performance rating r received

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
