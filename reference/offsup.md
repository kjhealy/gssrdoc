# Do you supervise the work of other employees

offsup

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question As an official part of your main job, do you supervise the work
of other employees or tell other employees what work to do?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
