# Should govt aid black people?

helpblk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now look at CARD BF. Some people think that Black people have
been discriminated against for so long that the government has a special
obligation to help improve their living standards. Others believe that
the government should not be giving special treatment to Black people.A.
Where would you place yourself on this scale, or haven't you made up
your mind on this?

## Values

- `[1]` government should help

- `[3]` agree with both

- `[5]` no special treatment

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
