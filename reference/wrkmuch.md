# How much you compare to people do same job

wrkmuch

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Compared to other people who do the same or similar kind of
work that you do, how much work would you say you do? Would you say that
you do much more, somewhat more, about the same, somewhat less or much
less?

## Values

- `[1]` much more

- `[2]` somewhat more

- `[3]` about same

- `[4]` somewhat less

- `[5]` much less

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
