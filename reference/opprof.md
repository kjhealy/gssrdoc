# Child of profssnl has better opportunity

opprof

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please indicate whether you agree or disagree with each of the
following statements. B. A person whose father is a professional has
much better chance of getting ahead in life than a person whose father
is a factory worker.

## Values

- `[1]` agree strongly

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
