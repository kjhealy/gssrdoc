# R's relationship to closest suicide

suiwho

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Think about the person you knew best that has committed
suicide. Please tell me the letter of the category on the card which
best describes your relationship to that person.

## Values

- `[1]` husband or wife

- `[2]` partner or lover

- `[3]` son or daughter

- `[4]` other relative

- `[5]` friend

- `[6]` neighbor

- `[7]` co-worker

- `[8]` acquaintance

- `[9]` patient

- `[10]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
