# How easy your supervisor know how good you are

quality

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How easy or hard would you say it is for you supervisor to
evaluate the quality of the work that is done by a person in a job like
yours? Would you say very easy, somewhat easy, somewhat hard, or very
hard?

## Values

- `[1]` very easy

- `[2]` somewhat easy

- `[3]` somewhat hard

- `[4]` very hard

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
