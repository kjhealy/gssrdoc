# Unable to care for a sick child-relative?

nonurse

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your (present/most recent) job, (have you ever done/did you
ever do) any of the following because of your responsibilities to the
job? B. Been unable to care for a sick child or relative?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
