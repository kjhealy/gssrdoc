# R's family income when 16 yrs old

incom16

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking about the time when you were 16 years old, compared
with American families in general then, would you say your family income
was–far below average, below average, average, above average, or far
above average? (PROBE: Just your best guess.)
