# R sought health information past year

hlthinfo

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In the past year, that is, between (INSERT CURRENT MONTH)
1999/2001/2003 and (INSERT CURRENT MONTH) 2000/02"have you looked for
information about a health concern or medical problem?

## Values

- `[0]` not at all

- `[1]` 1 or 2 times

- `[2]` 3-5 times

- `[3]` 6 or more times

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
