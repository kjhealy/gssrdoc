# Does r follow public affairs?

civic

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people seem to follow what's going on in government and
public affairs most of the time, whether there's an election going on or
not. Others aren't that interested. Would you say you follow what's
going on in government and public affairs most of the time, some of the
time, only now and then, or hardly at all?
