polviewy

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. We hear a lot of talk these days about liberals and
conservatives. I'm going to show you a seven-point scale on which the
political views that people might hold are arranged from extremely
liberal–point 1–to extremely conservative–point 7. Where would you place
yourself on this scale, or haven't you thought much about this?
