# In what order go to a therapist, or counselor

ormntlot

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are the things you said NAME should do. INTERVIEWER: READ
"YES" ANSWERS AND ASK: In what order should s/he do them? E. Go to a
therapist, or counselor, like a psychologist, social worker, or other
mental health professional for help.

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
