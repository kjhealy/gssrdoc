# Who would r have voted for-1996 election

if96who

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question H. In 1996, you remember that Clinton ran for President on the
Democratic ticket against Dole for the Republicans and Perot for the
Reform Party. Do you remember for sure whether or not you voted in that
election? 2. IF DID NOT VOTE OR INELIGIBLE: Who would you have voted
for, for President, if you had voted?

## Values

- `[1]` clinton

- `[2]` dole

- `[3]` perot

- `[4]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
