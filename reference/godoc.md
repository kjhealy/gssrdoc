# Last week went to see a doctor?

godoc

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to ask you about things you did during the last
seven days. I'm only interested in what you did during the last seven
days. From last (DAY OF WEEK) to today did you… A. Go to see a doctor or
receive medical treatment at a clinic or hospital?

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
