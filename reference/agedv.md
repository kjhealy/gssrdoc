# Should aged live with their children (with volunteered response on web)

agedv

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question As you know, many older people share a home with their grown
children. Do you think this is generally a good idea or a bad idea?

## Values

- `[1]` a good idea

- `[2]` a bad idea

- `[3]` depends (phone mode only: volunteered)

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
