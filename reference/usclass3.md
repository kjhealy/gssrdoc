# Achievement depends on educ and ability

usclass3

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are different opinions about social differences in this
country. Please tell me for each one whether you strongly agree,
somewhat, agree, somewhat disagree, or strongly disagree. READ EACH
STATEMENT. CIRCLE ONE CODE FOR EACH. C. America has an open society.
What one achieves in life no longer depends on one's family background,
but on the abilities one has and the education one acquires.
