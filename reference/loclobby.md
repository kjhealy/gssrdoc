# Has r lobbied local officials

loclobby

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you ever personally gone to see, or spoken to, or written
to – some member of local government or some other person of influence
in the community about some needs or problems?

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no
