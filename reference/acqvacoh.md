# Number of cohabiting ppl in voluntary association

acqvacoh

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Next, we are going to ask about people that you are acquainted
with through schools, clubs, associations, or places of worship. O. How
many women that you are acquainted with through schools, clubs,
associations, or places of worship are you pretty certain live in a
romantic relationship with a man to whom they are not married?
