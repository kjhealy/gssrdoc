# Attend some other type of religious event

othrel

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question During the last seven days did you do the following: C. Attend
some other type of religious event or meeting (e.g. prayer breakfasts,
Bible study groups, choir practices, church sponsored lectures, adult
fellowship meetings)?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
