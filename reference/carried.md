# R carried a stranger's belongings

carried

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question During the past 12 months, how often have you done each of the
following things: I. Carried a stranger”s belongings, like groceries, a
suitcase, or shopping bag

## Values

- `[1]` more than once a week

- `[2]` once a week

- `[3]` once a month

- `[4]` at least 2 or 3 times in the past year

- `[5]` once in the past year

- `[6]` not at all in the past year

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
