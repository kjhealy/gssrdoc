# R learn something new on web about arts

artsnew

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I would like you to think about the last time you used the
Web to get information or exchange views about music, film, the visual
arts, dance, theater, museums, literature, or related topics. A. On that
occasion, did you come across or learn about anything new with regard to
any of these topics?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[8]` cant recall

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
