# Born on date with a vietnam war draft lottery number

vietdraft

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Likelihood of respondent born on a date with a Vietnam War
draft lottery number

## Values

- `[0]` draft lottery number made draft unlikely

- `[1]` draft lottery number made draft likely

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(f)]` missing birthdate information `[NA(i)]`
  iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no
  answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on
  web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable `[NA(x)]` not available in this release
  `[NA(y)]` not available in this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
