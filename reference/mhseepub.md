# How often r sees someone with mental health problem in public places

mhseepub

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you frequently, occasionally, rarely, or never see someone
who seems to have a serious mental health problem in a public place?

## Values

- `[1]` frequently

- `[2]` occasionally

- `[3]` rarely

- `[4]` never

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
