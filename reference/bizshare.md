# Percentage ownership r holds

bizshare

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What percent ownership do you currently hold in this business?
IF R HAS MORE THAN ONE BUSINESS/FARM/PARTNERSHIP, ASK ABOUT THE PRIMARY
BUSINESS THEY ARE INVOLVED IN. ALLOW THE CAPTURE OF NON-NUMERICAL
ANSWERS LIKE "MAJORITY", "CONTROLLING INTEREST", "ONE OF THREE
PARTNERS", ETC.

## Values

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
