# Should police tail a suspect?

mantail

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, suppose the tip is about a man without a criminal record.
Please circle a number for each question to show if you think police
should be allowed without a court order to... A. Keep the man under
surveillance.

## Values

- `[1]` definitely allowed

- `[2]` probably allowed

- `[3]` probably not allowed

- `[4]` definitely not allowed

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
