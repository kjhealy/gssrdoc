# Opinion of why people get ahead

getaheay

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Which do you think is the most important reason why people get
ahead: their own hard work or lucky breaks and help from other people?

## Values

- `[1]` hard work most important

- `[2]` hard work, luck equally important

- `[3]` luck or help from other people most important

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
