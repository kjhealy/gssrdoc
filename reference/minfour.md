# Imputed poverty line for family of four

minfour

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People who have incomes below a certain level can be considered
poor. That level is called the "poverty line."What amount of weekly
income would you use as a poverty line for a family of four (husband,
wife, and two children) in this community?

## Values

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
