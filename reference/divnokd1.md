# Childless couples stay married even with problems

divnokd1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you agree or disagree… B. Even when there are no children, a
married couple should stay together even if they don”t get along.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
