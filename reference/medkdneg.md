# Medicating child will have long term negative

medkdneg

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now, I”d like to ask you for your opinions on several issues.
For each question, please tell me if you strongly agree, agree somewhat,
disagree somewhat or strongly disagree with the statement. B. Giving
medications to children with behavior problems will have long-term
negative effects on their development.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree somewhat

- `[3]` disagree somewhat

- `[4]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
