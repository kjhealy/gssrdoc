# R's religion treats men and women equally

relgeneq

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your opinion, does your religion treat men and women
equally, treat men better than women, or treat women better than men?

## Values

- `[1]` i don't belong to or follow any religion

- `[2]` treats men and women equally

- `[3]` treats men better than women

- `[4]` treats women better than men

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
