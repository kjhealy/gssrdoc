# Has r moved from one kind of place to another

geomobil

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question We are interested in the extent to which you have moved from
one kind of place to another. Which do you think is most like your
experience of life? Which of the following best applies to you?

## Values

- `[1]` i have lived in different countries

- `[2]` i have lived in different places in the same country

- `[3]` i have lived in different neighborhoods in the same place

- `[4]` i have always lived in the same neighborhood

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
