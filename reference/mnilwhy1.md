# 1st mention cause of mental illness feelings

mnilwhy1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (IF NO TO EVBRKDWN, ASK EVMHP) Have you ever felt you had a
mental health problem? A. (IF YES TO EVMHP ASK:) Could you tell me about
when you felt that way? What was it about? (PROBE: GET EXACT DATE and
complete details.) A1. The causes and sources of R's mental illness –
First mention.
