# Pay difference in companies

buseqinc

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Think of a score of 1 as meaning that major companies ought to
reduce the pay differences between employees with high pay and those
with low pay, and a score of 7 meaning that major companies should not
concern themselves with reducing pay differences.

## Values

- `[1]` should do something to reduce pay differences

- `[7]` should not concern themselves with pay differences

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
