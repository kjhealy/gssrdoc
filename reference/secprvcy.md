# Govt favor secrecy over workers privacy?

secprvcy

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question When faced with the conflict between an individual”s right to
privacy and the government”s need to collect personal background
information before giving a SECRET or TOP SECRET clearance, the
government should:

1.  Favor protecting an individual”s right to privacy by not gathering
    information.

2.  Favor the government”s need to protect security by gathering
    information.
