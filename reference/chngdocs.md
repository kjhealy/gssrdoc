# Changing official documents

chngdocs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you think the law should allow people to change their (sex)
on official documents like a driver's license to reflect (a sex that
differs from the one they were assigned at birth)? Question edits are in
compliance with federal administration directives released in January
2025.

## Values

- `[1]` yes, the law should allow

- `[2]` no, the law should not allow

- `[3]` not sure

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
