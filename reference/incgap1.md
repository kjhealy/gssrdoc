# Government's responsibility to reduce economic inequality

incgap1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking about the situation in America. To what extent do you
agree or disagree with the following statement? It should be the
government’s responsibility to reduce income differences between the
rich and the poor.
