# Branch of service

vetkind

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you ever been on active duty for military training or
service for two consecutive months or more? IF YES: What was your total
time on active duty? A. In what branch of the service was that?

## Values

- `[0]` several

- `[1]` air force guard

- `[2]` air force

- `[3]` navy

- `[4]` army

- `[5]` national guard

- `[6]` marines

- `[7]` coast guard

- `[8]` public health

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
