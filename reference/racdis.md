# How far are closest opp. race

racdis

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Are there any ("whites" for black respondents, "blacks" for
non-black respondents) living in this neighborhood now? B. How many
blocks (or miles) away do they (the (Negro/Black/African-American)
families who live closest to you) live?

## Values

- `[1]` same block

- `[2]` 1-3 blks away

- `[3]` 4-8 blks away

- `[4]` over 8 blks

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
