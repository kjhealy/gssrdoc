# Expect probs in interracial marriage

racmarpr

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please tell me whether you agree or disagree with the following
statements . . . A. You can expect special problems with marriages
between (Negroes/Blacks) and Whites.
