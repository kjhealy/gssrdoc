# America should follow decision of intl org

decsorgs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with the following
statements? (Please, select one response) C. In general, America should
follow the decisions of international organizations to which it belongs,
even if the government does not agree with them.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree
