## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you feel that your family, relatives and/or friends make too
many demands on you?

## Values

- `[1]` no, never

- `[2]` yes, but seldom

- `[3]` yes, sometimes

- `[4]` yes, often

- `[5]` yes, very often

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
