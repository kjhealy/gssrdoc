# Liking for england

england

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question You will notice that the boxes on this card go from the highest
position of "plus 5" for a country which you like very much, to the
lowest position of "minus 5" for a country you dislike very much. How
far up the scale or how far down the scale would you rate the following
countries? READ EACH ITEM. C. England
