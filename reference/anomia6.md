# Not fair to bring child into world

anomia6

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I'm going to read you several more statements. Some people
agree with a statement, others disagree. As I read each one, tell me
whether you more or less agree with it, or more or less disagree. F.
It's hardly fair to bring a child into the world with the way things
look for the future.
