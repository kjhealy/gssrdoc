# Importance formal school for you job

schoolng

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How important were each of the following as sources of the
skills that you use in your job? Would you say very important,
important, somewhat important, or not at all important? A. Formal
Schooling.

## Values

- `[1]` very important

- `[2]` important

- `[3]` somewhat important

- `[4]` not at all important

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
