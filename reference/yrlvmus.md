# Attend live music or theater in past 12 months

yrlvmus

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question First, we'd like to ask about some leisure or recreational
activities that people do during their free time. As we list each
activity, please indicate if it is something you have done in the past
12 months. During the last 12 months, did you go in person to a live
music, theater, or dance performance?

NOTE: This variable originates on the NEA follow-on, and should not be
analyzed without the NEA-specific weights WTSSPS_NEA or WTSSNRPS_NEA

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
