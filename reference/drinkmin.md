# How often at least a sip but less than one full drink

drinkmin

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question F. During the last 12 months, how often did you have at least a
sip, but less than one full drink of any kind of alcoholic beverage in a
single day, that is, beer, wine, or a drink containing liquor of any
kind. Was it:

## Values

- `[1]` never in the last 12 months

- `[2]` once in the last 12 months

- `[3]` twice in the last 12 months

- `[4]` three to six times in the last 12 months

- `[5]` seven to eleven times in the last 12 months

- `[6]` one to three times a month

- `[7]` once or twice a week

- `[8]` three or four times a week

- `[9]` every day or nearly every day

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
