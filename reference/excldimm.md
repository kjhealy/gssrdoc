# America should exclude illegal immigrants

excldimm

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with the following statement?
America should take stronger measures to exclude illegal immigrants.

## Values

- `[1]` agree strongly

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` disagree strongly

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
