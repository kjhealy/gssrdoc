# How much money contributed in informal-alone

totinfrm

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Approximately how much money or the cash equivalent of property
have you contributed in each of the fields listed above in the past
twelve months? (REPEAT FOR EACH ITEM MENTIONED IN GIVHLTH-GIVOTH) RECORD
ABOVE UNDER TOTHLTH-VALOTH. (ESTIMATED DOLLAR VALUE CONTRIBUTED
INCLUDING BOTH CASH CONTRIBUTIONS AND THE CASH-VALUE OF PROPERTY.) N.
Informal-alone-not-for-pay

## Values

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
