# R's rating of life overall in five years from 0-10

lifein5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If you were asked to rate your overall life from 0-10, where 10
represents the best possible state and 0 represents the worst possible
state, how do you think you would rate your life five years from today?

## Values

- `[0]` 0 - worst possible state

- `[10]` 10 - best possible state

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
