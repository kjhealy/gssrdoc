# Spiritually touched by beauty creation

beausprt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question The following questions deal with possible daily spiritual
experiences. To what extent can you say you experience the following: F.
I am spiritually touched by the beauty of creation.
