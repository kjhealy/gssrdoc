# Should carrying a firearm drinking alcohol be illegal

gunsdrnk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In all states it is illegal to drive while under the influence
of alcohol. Would you favor or oppose state laws making it illegal to
carry a firearm while under the influence of alcohol?

## Values

- `[1]` favor

- `[2]` oppose

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
