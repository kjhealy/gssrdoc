# R's age when 1st child born

agekdbrn_9422

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How old were you when your first child was born? As of 2024,
AGEKDBRN has been recoded to only account for first child born during
ages 18-45. This has been retroactively applied to all previous years of
AGEKDBRN. The original AGEKDBRN containing data from 1994 to 2022 has
been renamed AGEKDBRN_9422.
