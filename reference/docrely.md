# R relies on doctors knowledge

docrely

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question I will read you some statements of beliefs people have. Please
look at the card and decide which answer best applies to you. C. I
prefer to rely on my doctor's knowledge and not try to find out about my
condition on my own.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` moderately agree

- `[3]` slightly agree

- `[4]` slightly disagree

- `[5]` moderately disagree

- `[6]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
