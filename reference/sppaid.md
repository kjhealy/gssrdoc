# Regularly received alimony - chld supprt

sppaid

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Are you currently or have you ever been entitled to alimony or
child support payments from a former (husband/wife)? A. During the time
you were entitled to alimony or child support, did you receive these
payments on a regular basis, occasionally, or not at all?

## Values

- `[1]` regular basis

- `[2]` occasionally

- `[3]` not at all
