# Sex of suicide victim closest to r

suisex

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question We would like to know a few other things about that person. A.
Was that person male or female?

## Values

- `[1]` male

- `[2]` female

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
