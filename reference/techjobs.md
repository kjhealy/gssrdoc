# New techs increase number of jobs

techjobs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question New kinds of technology are being introduced more and more in
the United States: computers, robots, and so on. Do you think these new
technologies will over the next few years...

## Values

- `[1]` greatly increase

- `[2]` slight increase

- `[3]` no difference to

- `[4]` slightly reduce

- `[5]` greatly reduce

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
