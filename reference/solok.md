# How important: citizens have adequate standard of living

solok

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question There are different opinions about people's rights in a
democracy. On a scale of 1 to 7, where 1 is not at all important and 7
is very important, how important is it: A. That all citizens have an
adequate standard of living

## Values

- `[1]` not at all important

- `[7]` very important

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
