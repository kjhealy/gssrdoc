# I can think of many ways to reach my current goals

hope5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (HAND CARD G2) (Using the scale on the showcard, please select
the response that best describes how you think about yourself right
now.) I can think of many ways to reach my current goals.
