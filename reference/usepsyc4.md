# Will take medicine because intense fear

usepsyc4

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How likely would you be to take doctor-prescribed psychiatric
medication in the following situations? D. ...for no apparent reason,
you were having periods of intense fear in which you were trembling,
sweating, feeling dizzy, and feared losing control or going crazy.

## Values

- `[1]` very likely

- `[2]` somewhat likely

- `[3]` mixed

- `[4]` somewht unlikely

- `[5]` very unlikely

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
