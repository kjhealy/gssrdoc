# You evaluate job performance, not supervise

othduty6

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (On the card you have are/Here is a card listing) 7 different
kinds of work activities. Which ones do you do with employees whom you
do not officially supervise? Please mention all that apply: F. Evaluate
job performance.
