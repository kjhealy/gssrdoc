# To help others

helpoth

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question If you had to choose, which thing on this list would you pick
as the most important for a child to learn to prepare him or her for
life? (INTERVIEWER: READ CHOICES) A. Which comes next in importance? B.
Which comes third? C. Which comes fourth? 5. To help others when they
need help

- `[2]` 2nd important

- `[3]` 3rd important

- `[4]` 4th important

- `[5]` least important
