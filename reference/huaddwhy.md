# Why there is no hu at this address

huaddwhy

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. SELECT ONE FROM THE LIST BELOW WHICH BEST DESCRIBES WHY
THERE IS NO HU AT THIS ADDRESS

## Values

- `[1]` condemned

- `[2]` demolished

- `[3]` place of business without residence

- `[4]` no such address, no such hu

- `[5]` group quarters

- `[6]` vacation cabin

- `[7]` not usable as a permanent residence

- `[8]` transient use (less than one month)

- `[9]` not an hu for other reason

- `[10]` under construction

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
