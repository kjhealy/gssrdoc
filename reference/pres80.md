# Vote for carter, reagan or anderson

pres80

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question D. In 1980, you remember that Carter ran for President on the
Democratic ticket against Reagan for the Republicans, and Anderson as an
Independent. Do you remember for sure whether or not you voted in that
election?

1.  IF VOTED: Did you vote for Carter, Reagan, or Anderson?

## Values

- `[2]` reagan

- `[3]` anderson

- `[4]` other

- `[5]` refused

- `[6]` didnt vote

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
