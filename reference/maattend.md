# How often ma attended religious services

maattend

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. When you were growing up, how often did your mother (MOTHER
SUBSTITUTE) attend religious services?

## Values

- `[0]` never

- `[1]` lt once a year

- `[2]` once a year

- `[3]` sevrl times a yr

- `[4]` once a month

- `[5]` 2-3x a month

- `[6]` nrly every week

- `[7]` every week

- `[8]` more thn once wk

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
