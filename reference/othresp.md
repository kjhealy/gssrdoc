# How resp. person who you were angry at?

othresp

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Think again about the situation that made you angry or annoyed.
How responsible was the person/people who you were angry or annoyed at
for the situation? Think of a scale where 0 means he/she was/were not at
all responsible, while 10 means he/she/they was/were completely
responsible.

## Values

- `[0]` not at all responsible

- `[10]` completely responsible

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
