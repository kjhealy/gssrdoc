# How much housework r does

rhmewrk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question We're interested in knowing how much of the work around your
home is done by different people. We don't mean taking care of
children - just regular work around the house, like cooking, grocery
shopping and doing little repair jobs. A. How much of this work do you
do?

## Values

- `[1]` all

- `[2]` most

- `[3]` about half

- `[4]` some

- `[5]` very little or none

- `[6]` not married-living with partner

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
