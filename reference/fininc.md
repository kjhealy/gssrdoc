# R heard docs limit used of exp tests

fininc

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some doctors or groups may be paid more if they limit the use
of expensive tests or the use of specialists. Such arrangements are
known as financial incentives. Have you heard of such arrangements
before now?

- `[2]` no
