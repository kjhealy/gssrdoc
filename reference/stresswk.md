# Stress in past 2 wks

stresswk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question During the past two weeks, would you say that you experienced a
lot of stress, a moderate amount of stress, relatively little stress, or
almost no stress at all?

## Values

- `[1]` a lot of stress

- `[2]` a moderate amount of stress

- `[3]` relatively little stress

- `[4]` almost no stress at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
