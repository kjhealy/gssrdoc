# Have ever boycotted a product past 5 years

boycott

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Over the past 5 years have you done any of the following to
express your opinion about an issue or your support for a cause? A.
Boycotting a product

- `[2]` no
