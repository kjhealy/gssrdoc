# I hardly ever expect things to go my way

lotr4

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (HAND CARD G1) (Do you agree or disagree that . . .) I hardly
ever expect things to go my way.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly disagree

- `[2]` disagree

- `[3]` neutral

- `[4]` agree

- `[5]` strongly agree
