# In the past 7 days, r's average pain

ratepain

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question On a scale from 0 to 10, with 0 meaning no pain and 10 being
the worst imaginable pain, how would you rate your pain on average?

## Values

- `[0]` 0 - no pain

- `[10]` 10 - the worst imaginable pain
