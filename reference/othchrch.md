# Happy in other non catholic church

othchrch

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please tell me whether you strongly agree, agree, disagree, or
strongly disagree with the following statements: A. I could be just as
happy in some other church, it wouldn't have to be Catholic.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` disagree

- `[4]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
