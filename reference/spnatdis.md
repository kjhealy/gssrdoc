# Should govt spend more or less on natural disasters

spnatdis

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Would you like to see more or less government spending on
natural disasters. Remember that if you say "much more", it might
require a tax increase to pay for it. Would you say...

## Values

- `[1]` spend much more

- `[2]` spend more

- `[3]` spend the same as now

- `[4]` spend less

- `[5]` spend much less

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
