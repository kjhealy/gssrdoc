# How often does r have extra time

bored

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In general, how do you feel about your time–would you say you
always feel rushed even to do things you have to do, Only sometimes feel
rushed, or almost never feel rushed. A. How often would you say you have
time on your hands that you don't know what to do with–quite often, just
now and then, or almost never?
