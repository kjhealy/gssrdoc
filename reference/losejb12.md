# How likely r will lose job or laid off in next 1 yr

losejb12

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking now about the next 12 months, how likely do you think
it is that you will lose your job or be laid off for reasons other than
for your performance? Would you say…

## Values

- `[1]` very likely

- `[2]` fairly likely

- `[3]` not too likely

- `[4]` not at all likely

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
