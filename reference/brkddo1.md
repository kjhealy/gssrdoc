# 1st mention what r did when felt ner. break

brkddo1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you ever felt that you were going to have a nervous
breakdown? B. (IF EVER ANTICIPATED A NERVOUS BREAKDOWN) What did you do
about it? (PROBE: Anything else? until R says no.) Which of these things
did you do first; second; third? (INTERVIEWER: Sequence list; read back
if needed.) B1. What R did about the nervous breakdown - First mention.
