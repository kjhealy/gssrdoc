# Where r gets political news

polnewsfrom

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Where do you get most of your news about political matters?
Please select the most important source

## Values

- `[1]` online social media (for example facebook, instagram, tiktok, x
  (formerly twitter), youtube, snapchat)

- `[2]` television (including tv apps or access via internet)

- `[3]` newspapers (in print or access via internet)

- `[4]` radio and podcasts (including internet radio or access via
  internet

- `[5]` websites (including exclusively online news sites, news
  aggregators, portals, or search engines)

- `[6]` other

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
