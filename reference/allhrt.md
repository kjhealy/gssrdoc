# Everyone hurt from traditional family

allhrt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Who is hurt by this arrangement: men, women, children,
everyone, or no one? PROBE: Anyone else? UNTIL R SAYS NO. D. Everyone
