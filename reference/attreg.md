# Last 7 days attend a worship service

attreg

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question During the last seven days did you do the following: A. Attend
a regular, weekly worship service at a church/synagogue (e.g. mass or
Sunday morning services). Don't include watching a service on TV or
listening to one on the radio.

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
