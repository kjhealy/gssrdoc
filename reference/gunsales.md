# Should background check be required for private gun sale

gunsales

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In most states a gun owner may legally sell his or her gun
without proof that the buyer has passed a criminal history check. How
strongly do you favor or oppose a law that required private gun sales to
be subject to the same background check requirements as sales by
licensed dealers? Do you…

## Values

- `[1]` strongly favor

- `[2]` favor

- `[3]` neither favor nor oppose

- `[4]` oppose
