# Local economy depends on defense work?

resdefwk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Would you say the economy of (NAME METROPOLITAN AREA OR COUNTY)
is very dependent on defense business, somewhat dependent, or not
dependent at all on defense business?

## Values

- `[1]` very dependent

- `[2]` somewhat dependent

- `[3]` not at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
