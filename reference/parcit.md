# Were your parents citizens of america?

parcit

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. At the time of your birth, were both, one or neither of your
parents citizens of America?

## Values

- `[1]` both were citizens of america

- `[2]` only father was a citizen of america

- `[3]` only mother was a citizen of america

- `[4]` neither was a citizen of america

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
