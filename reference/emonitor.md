# Civil liberties - monitor emails

emonitor

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (Do you think that the American government should or should not
have the right to do the following?) Monitor e-mails and any other
information exchanged on the Internet?  (Would you say...)

## Values

- `[1]` definitely should have the right

- `[2]` probably should have the right

- `[3]` probably should not have the right

- `[4]` definitely should not have the right

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
