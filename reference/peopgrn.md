# How get people to protect environment

peopgrn

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question J. Which of these approaches do you think would be the best way
of getting people and their families in America to protect the
environment?

## Values

- `[1]` heavy fines for people who damage the environment

- `[2]` use the tax system to reward people who protect the environment

- `[3]` more information and education for people about the advantages
  of protecting the environment

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
