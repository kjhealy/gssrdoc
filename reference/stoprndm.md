# Should authorities have right to search ppl in the street

stoprndm

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Suppose the government suspected that a terrorist act was about
to happen. Do you think the authorities should have the right to… C.
Stop and search people in the street at random?

## Values

- `[1]` definitely should have right

- `[2]` probably should have right

- `[3]` probably should not have right

- `[4]` definitely should not have right

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
