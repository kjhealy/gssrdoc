# How difficult or easy is it for r's household to make ends meet

endsmeet

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Thinking of your household's total income, including all the
sources of income of all the members who contribute to it, how difficult
or easy is it currently for your household to make ends meet?

## Values

- `[1]` very difficult

- `[2]` fairly difficult

- `[3]` neither easy nor difficult

- `[4]` fairly easy

- `[5]` very easy

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
