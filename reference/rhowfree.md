# How much freedom does r have

rhowfree

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question And about you yourself? Would you say right now that you have
complete freedom, a great deal of freedom, a moderate degree of freedom,
not much freedom, or no freedom at all?

## Values

- `[1]` complete freedom

- `[2]` a great deal of freedom

- `[3]` a moderate degree of freedom

- `[4]` not much freedom

- `[5]` no freedom at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
