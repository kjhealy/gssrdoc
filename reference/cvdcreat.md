# Change in creating art since first year of covid

cvdcreat

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (Compared to the first year of the COVID-19 pandemic (March
2020 to March 2021), are you now...) creating or performing art of your
own more often, at the same rate, less often, or are you not creating or
performing art at all? This may have included music, theater, or dance,
creative writing, crafts or visual arts, digital art, or film or
photography done for artistic purposes

NOTE: This variable originates on the NEA follow-on, and should not be
analyzed without the NEA-specific weights WTSSPS_NEA or WTSSNRPS_NEA

## Values

- `[1]` more often

- `[2]` at the same rate

- `[3]` less often

- `[4]` r not attending these activities

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
