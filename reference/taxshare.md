# Should rich pay bigger share of taxes?

taxshare

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you think that people with high incomes should pay a larger
share of their income in taxes than those with low incomes, the same
share, or a smaller share . . .

## Values

- `[1]` much larger share

- `[2]` larger

- `[3]` the same share

- `[4]` smaller

- `[5]` much smaller share

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
