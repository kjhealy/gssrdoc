# Us harmony by ignore ethnic differences

ethignor

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are some opinions some people have expressed in connection
with ethnic issues in the United States. To what extent do you agree or
disagree with each one? A. Harmony in the United States is best achieved
by down playing or ignoring ethnic differences.
