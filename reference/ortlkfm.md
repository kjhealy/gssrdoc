# In what order talk things over with family & friends

ortlkfm

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are the things you said NAME should do. INTERVIEWER: READ
"YES" ANSWERS AND ASK: In what order should s/he do them? A. Talk things
over with family and friends.

## Values

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
