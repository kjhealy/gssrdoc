# How well local church counsels sympathetically

sympcoun

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How do you rate how your local church does each of the
following? Would you say your local church does an excellent, very good,
fair, or poor job of: C. Sympathetic counseling
