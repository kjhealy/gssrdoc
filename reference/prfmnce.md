# Did r go to a performance in last 12 months

prfmnce

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question PAY SPECIAL ATTENTION TO THE HANDCARD NUMBER INDICATED FOR THE
QUESTIONS ASKED.  DEPENDING ON THE RESPONDENT'S ANSWERS, YOU WILL NEED
TO REFER HIM/HER TO HANDCARD H1, H2, H3 OR H4.     With the exception of
elementary or high school performances, did you go to a live music,
theater, or dance performance, during the last 12 months?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job

  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
