# Number of blacks in military-version y

blnumoky

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question B. At the present time, about 33 percent of the army are black.
All things considered, do you think there are too many blacks in the
army, about the right number or should there be more blacks in the army?
RECORD UNDER "B."

## Values

- `[1]` too many

- `[2]` about right number

- `[3]` should be more

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
