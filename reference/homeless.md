# Contributed to the homeless

homeless

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People help other people in ways besides giving money, time, or
other things to organized groups. Sometimes people help needy people
directly. During the past 12 months, did you or members of your family
or household give money, food, or clothing to any of the following types
of people? A. The homeless or street-people

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no
