# Engineers work for good of humanity

enggood

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question C. Engineering researchers are dedicated people who work for
the good of humanity. Do you strongly agree, agree, disagree, or strongy
disagree?

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` disagree

- `[4]` strongly disagree
