# Has r or someone they know been a victim of online scam

intscam

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Some people receive emails or text messages from scammers from
time to time, some of them also become victims. Apart from only
receiving such messages, have you or someone you know personally ever
been a victim of an online scam, identity theft or hacking attack?

## Values

- `[1]` yes, me myself

- `[2]` yes, someone i know personally

- `[3]` yes, me and someone else i know personally

- `[4]` no

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
