# Relation to last sex partner

relatsex

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question The last time you had sex, was it with someone you were in an
on-going relationship with, or was it with someone else? Remember that
by "sex" we mean only vaginal, oral, or anal sex.

## Values

- `[1]` yes, the last time i had sex, it was with someone i was in an
  on-going relationship with

- `[2]` no, the last time i had sex, it was not with someone i was in an
  on-going relationship with

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
