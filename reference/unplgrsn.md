# Reason r reduced time spent on internet

unplgrsn

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What is the main reason why you deliberately reduced the time
you spent on the Internet?

## Values

- `[1]` i had concerns about data security or privacy issues

- `[2]` i felt it negatively impacted my physical or mental wellbeing

- `[3]` i felt overwhelmed by the amount of information

- `[4]` i was asked to spend more time in person with family or friends

- `[5]` i wanted more time for offline activities

- `[6]` i couldn't afford to use the internet

- `[7]` other reasons
