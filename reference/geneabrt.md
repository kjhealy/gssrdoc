# Abort defective fetus

geneabrt

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Suppose a test shows the baby has a serious genetic defect.
Would you (yourself want to/ want your partner to) have an abortion if a
test shows the baby has a serious genetic defect?

## Values

- `[1]` have abortion

- `[2]` no abortion

- `[7]` refused

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
