# Who decides weekend activities

wkndact

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question When you and your (spouse/partner) make decisions about
choosing shared weekend activities, who has the final say?

## Values

- `[1]` mostly me

- `[2]` mostly my spousepartfill

- `[3]` sometimes me/sometimes my spousepartfill

- `[4]` we decide together

- `[5]` someone else

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
