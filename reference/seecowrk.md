# How easy to see whether co-workers working

seecowrk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In your job how easy is it for you to see whether your
co-workers are working well or poorly? On a scale of 0 to 10 please
describe with 0 meaning not at all easy to see and 10 meaning very easy
to see.

## Values

- `[0]` not at all easy to see

- `[10]` very easy to see

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
