# Should allow art to mock or demean religion

irrelart

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Consider an artist–like a painter, sculptor, or
photographer–whose art works mock or demean religions or religious
figures. If such a person wanted to exhibit these works in your
community, should the exhibit be allowed, or not?
