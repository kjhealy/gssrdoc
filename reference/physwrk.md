# How often must r do physical work

physwrk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now some more questions about your working conditions. one code
for each item below to show how often it applies to your work. How
often... B. Do you have to do hard physical work?

## Values

- `[1]` always

- `[2]` often

- `[3]` sometimes

- `[4]` hardly ever

- `[5]` never

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
