# True-false: nanotechnology manipulate extremely small units

nanoknw1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here are a couple of true-false questions about nanotechnology.
As before, if you don't know or aren't sure, just tell me so, and we
will skip to the next question. Remember true, false, or don't know. A.
Nanotechnology involves manipulating extremely small units of matter,
such as individual atoms, in order to produce better materials. (Is that
true or false?)
