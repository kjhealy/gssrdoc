# Foreigner's should not be allowed to buy land

forland

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How much do you agree or disagree with the following
statements? E. Foreigners should not be allowed to buy land in America

- `[1]` agree strongly

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` disagree strongly
