# Mother vs father care for child

prntlk

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question People have different opinions about how well mothers and
fathers are suited to look after their children. Which of the following
statements comes closest to your opinion?

## Values

- `[1]` mothers are much better suited

- `[2]` mothers are somewhat better suited

- `[3]` mothers and fathers are equally suited

- `[4]` fathers are somewhat better suited

- `[5]` fathers are much better suited

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
