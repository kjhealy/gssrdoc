# How frightening most people are in public places

mhfright

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do you frequently, occasionally, rarely, or never see someone
who seems to have a serious mental health problem in a public place? A.
How frightening are most people that you see in public places who appear
to have a serious mental health problem? Are they…

## Values

- `[1]` very frightening

- `[2]` somewhat frightening

- `[3]` not very frightening

- `[4]` not at all frightening

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
