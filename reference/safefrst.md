# No shortcuts on worker safety

safefrst

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I”m going to read you a list of statements that might or
might not describe your main job. Please tell me whether you strongly
agree, agree, disagree, or strongly disagree with each of these
statements. L. There are no significant compromises or shortcuts taken
when worker safety is at stake

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` disagree

- `[4]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
