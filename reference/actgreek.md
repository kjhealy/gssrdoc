# R active in school fraternity

actgreek

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you ever done any active work for (EACH "YES" IN
MEMFRAT-MEMOTHER)? I mean been a leader, helped organize meetings, been
an officer, or given time or money? J. School fraternities or sororities
