# Try to make sense without relying on god

cope6

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Think about how you try to understand and deal with major
problems in your life. To what extent is each of the following involved
in the way you cope: F. I try to make sense of the situation and decide
what to do without relying on God.
