# Does r know what a computer virus is

cmpvirus

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now we'd like to ask you a few questions about terms that
people use to talk about the Internet and World Wide Web. E. Do you know
what a computer virus is?

## Values

- `[1]` yes

- `[2]` no

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
