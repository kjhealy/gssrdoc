# What is r's race, third mention

racecen3_0022

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What is your race? Indicate one or more races that you consider
yourself to be. C. Third mention

As of 2024, RACECEN3 has been recoded to coarsen certain sensitive
categories. This has been retroactively applied to all previous years of
RACECEN3. The original RACECEN3 containing data from 2000 to 2022 has
been renamed RACECEN3_0022.
