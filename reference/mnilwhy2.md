# 2nd mention cause of mental illness feelings

mnilwhy2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question (IF NO TO EVBRKDWN, ASK EVMHP) Have you ever felt you had a
mental health problem? A2. The causes and sources of R”s mental illness
– Second mention.
