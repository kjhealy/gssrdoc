## Details

(RESPONDENT'S COLLEGE DEGREE) degree? As of 2024, MAJOR1 has been
recoded to only account for 39 categories. This has been retroactively
applied to all previous years of MAJOR1. The original MAJOR1 containing
data from 2012 to 2022 has been renamed MAJOR1_1222.
