# I have a lot to say on my job

lottosay

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How true would you say each of the following statements is
about your job? Would you say very true, true, somewhat true, or not
true at all: B. I have a lot to say over what happens on my job.

## Values

- `[1]` very true

- `[2]` true

- `[3]` somewhat true

- `[4]` not true at all

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
