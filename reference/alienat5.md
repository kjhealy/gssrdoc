# Powerful people take advantage of you

alienat5

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now I want to read you some things some people have told us
they have felt from time to time. Do you tend to feel or not . . . (READ
LIST) E. Most people with power try to take advantage of people like
yourself.
