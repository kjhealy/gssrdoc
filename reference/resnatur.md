# Humans should respect naturecreated by god

resnatur

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question And please check one box for each of these statements to show
how much you agree or disagree with it. D. Human beings should respect
nature because it was created by God.

## Values

- `[1]` strongly agree

- `[2]` agree

- `[3]` neither agree nor disagree

- `[4]` disagree

- `[5]` strongly disagree

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
