# People should be allowed to publish books expressing their views

relext2

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question There are some people whose views are considered extreme by the
majority. Consider religious extremists, that is people who believe that
their religion is the only true faith and all other religions should be
considered as enemies. B. Do you think such people should be allowed to
publish books expressing their views? Would you say…
