# Only a few people can judge art

judgeart

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question I'm going to read several statements. After I read each one,
please tell me whether you strongly agree, agree, disagree, or strongly
disagree with it. For example, ... A. Only a few people have the
knowledge and ability to judge excellence in the arts.
