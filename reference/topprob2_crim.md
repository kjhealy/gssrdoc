# Next important issue (crime) - follow-on version

topprob2_crim

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Do any other issues match your answer to the previous question
(TOPPROB1_NEXT)? Crime NOTE: This variable originates on the GSS Next
follow-on, and should not be analyzed without the GSS Next-specific
weights WTSSPS_NEXT or WTSSNRPS_NEXT

## Values

- `[1]` mentioned

- `[2]` not mentioned

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
