# Number of brothers and sisters

sibs

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How many brothers and sisters did you have? Please count those
born alive, but no longer living, as well as those alive now. Also
include stepbrothers and stepsisters, and children adopted by your
parents.

## Values

- `[6]` 6 or more

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
