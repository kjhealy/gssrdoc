# R's concern about women's rights

fecare

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. How concerned are you personally about women”s rights? Are
you very concerned somewhat concerned, not very concerned, or not
concerned at all?

## Values

- `[1]` very concerned

- `[2]` somewhat concerned

- `[3]` not very concerned

- `[4]` not concerned at all

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
