# How often congress keeps promises

polefy16

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question How often do the people we elect to Congress try to keep the
promises they have made during the election: Never, rarely, sometimes,
very often, or extremely often?

## Values

- `[1]` never

- `[2]` rarely

- `[3]` sometimes

- `[4]` very often

- `[5]` extremely often

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
