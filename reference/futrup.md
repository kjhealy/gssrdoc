# Degree economy will improve in next year

futrup

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What about the next 12 months? Do you expect the national
economy to get better, get worse, or stay about the same? A. Would you
say much better or somewhat better?

## Values

- `[1]` much better

- `[2]` somewhat better

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
