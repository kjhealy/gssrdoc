# Volunteered other kinds past 12 month

volwkoth

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Have you done any voluntary activity in the past 12 months in
any of the following areas? Voluntary activity is unpaid work, not just
belonging to an organization or group. It should be of service or
benefit to other people or the community and not only to one's family or
personal friends. During the last 12 months did you do volunteer work in
any of the following areas: D. Any other kind of voluntary activities
