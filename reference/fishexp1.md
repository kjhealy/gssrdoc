## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Please look at Card I-5. What is the scientist trying to find
out from this experiment?

## Values

- `[1]` if the number of fish in the fish bowl affects the behavior of
  the fish

- `[2]` if the temperature of the fish bowl affects the behavior of the
  fish

- `[3]` if the temperature and the amount of light affect the behavior
  of the fish

- `[4]` if the number of fish, the temperature, and the amount of light
  affect the behavior of the fish

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
