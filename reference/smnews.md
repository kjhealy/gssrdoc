# Reliability of news on social media

smnews

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question In general, how reliable would you rate the news on political
matters found in the following sources?… Online social media (for
example WhatsApp, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, Truth
Social).

## Values

- `[1]` very unreliable

- `[2]` somewhat unreliable

- `[3]` somewhat reliable

- `[4]` very reliable

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
