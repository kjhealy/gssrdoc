# Highest priority for america

postmat1

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question A. Looking at the list below, please check a box next to the
one thing you think should be America”s highest priority, the most
important thing it should do. America should…

## Values

- `[1]` maintain order in the nation

- `[2]` give people more say in government decisions

- `[3]` fight rising prices

- `[4]` protect freedom of speech

- `[NA(d)]` can't choose `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
