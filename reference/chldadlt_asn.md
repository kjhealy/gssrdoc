# Percent of adults you knew (friends, care takers, neighbors) during childhood who were asian

chldadlt_asn

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Not including your family, what percentage of the adults you
knew (friends, care takers, neighbors) belonged to each of the following
categories? - Asian
