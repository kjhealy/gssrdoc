# How well elected official know risk by nuclear power

polnuke

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Here's another public issue: nuclear power. Nuclear power uses
the energy of the atom to generate electricity. some people say that
nuclear power is a safe option to meet the energy needs of the United
States without burning fossil fuels such as coal, oil, or natural gas.
Others are concerned about potential risks in the operation of nuclear
plants and about the disposal of nuclear waste. Please look at Card E13.
(On a scale of 1 to 5, where 1 means "Very Well" and 5 means Not At
All), how well do the following groups understand the likely effects of
using nuclear power to generate electricity? A. Elected officials (How
well elected officials know risk by nuclear power?)
