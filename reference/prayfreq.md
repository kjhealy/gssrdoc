# How often does r pray

prayfreq

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question Now thinking about the present, about how often do you pray?

## Values

- `[1]` never

- `[2]` less than once a year

- `[3]` about once or twice a year

- `[4]` several times a year

- `[5]` about once a month

- `[6]` 2-3 times a month

- `[7]` nearly every week

- `[8]` every week

- `[9]` several times a week

- `[10]` once a day

- `[11]` several times a day

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
  `[NA(m)]` dk, na, iap `[NA(n)]` no answer `[NA(p)]` not imputable
  `[NA(r)]` refused `[NA(s)]` skipped on web `[NA(u)]` uncodeable
  `[NA(x)]` not available in this release `[NA(y)]` not available in
  this year `[NA(z)]` see codebook
