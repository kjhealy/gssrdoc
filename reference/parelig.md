# What was father's religious preference

parelig

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question What was your father”s religious preference when you were
growing up? Was it Protestant, Catholic, Jewish, some other religion, or
no religion?

## Values

- `[1]` protestant

- `[2]` catholic

- `[3]` jewish

- `[4]` none

- `[5]` other

- `[NA(d)]` don't know `[NA(i)]` iap `[NA(j)]` I don't have a job
