# Ok to get rich even if others poor

richpoor

## Source

General Social Survey https://gss.norc.org

## Details

Question As I read each of the following statements, please tell me
whether you strongly agree, agree, neither agree or disagree, disagree,
or strongly disagree with it. B. People should be allowed to accumulate
as much wealth as they can even if some make millions while others live
in poverty.
